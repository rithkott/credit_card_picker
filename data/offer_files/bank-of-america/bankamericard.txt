BankAmericard (Bank of America)

Source: https://www.bankofamerica.com/credit-cards/products/bankamericard-credit-card/

Annual Fee
- $0

Rewards
- None — this is a low-interest/balance-transfer focused card, not a rewards card.

APR
- Introductory: 0% for 21 billing cycles for purchases, and for any balance transfers made within 60 days of account opening.
- Standard variable APR thereafter: 14.99% to 25.99%.

Balance Transfer Fee
- 5% fee applies to all balance transfers.

Foreign Transaction Fee
- Not stated on the fetched product page.

Other Benefits
- No penalty APR for late payments.
- $0 Liability Guarantee for fraudulent transactions.
- Balance Connect® overdraft protection (optional, no transfer fees).
- Paperless statement option.
- Digital wallet compatibility (Apple Pay, Android Pay, Samsung Pay).
- Online and mobile banking access, account alerts via email or text.

Positioning: Marketed explicitly as a "low interest" card intended to help cardholders save money on interest and pay down balances faster, rather than earn rewards or cash back.

Issued by Bank of America, N.A.
