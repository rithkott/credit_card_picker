Atmos Rewards Summit Visa Infinite (Bank of America / Alaska Airlines)

Source: https://www.bankofamerica.com/credit-cards/products/alaska-airlines-infinite-credit-card/

Product note: New premium tier launched August 2025 alongside the Atmos Rewards rebrand (merger of Alaska Airlines and Hawaiian Airlines loyalty programs). Official current product name confirmed: "Atmos™ Rewards Summit Visa Infinite® Credit Card." $395 annual fee confirmed.

Annual Fee
- $395

Signup Bonus
- 100,000 bonus points and a 25,000-point Global Companion Award after spending $6,500 or more on purchases within the first 90 days, plus a 50% flight discount code. (Note: some third-party sources report a lower/targeted offer of 80,000 points + 25,000-point Global Companion Award after $4,000 spend — treat the $6,500/100,000 figure from the official product page as authoritative; targeted offers may vary.)

Earn Rates
- 3 points per $1 on dining, foreign transactions, and Alaska Airlines/Hawaiian Airlines purchases.
- 1 point per $1 on all other purchases.
- 10% bonus on all points earned with an eligible Bank of America account (Preferred Rewards).

Companion Award Benefit
- 25,000-point Global Companion Award every account anniversary.
- 100,000-point Global Companion Award annually after spending $60,000+ on purchases in that anniversary year.

Other Benefits
- 8 Alaska Lounge passes annually (2 per calendar quarter, a $500+ value) and 8 Wi-Fi passes annually (a $60+ value).
- Free checked bag and preferred boarding for cardholder and up to 6 guests on the same reservation when paying with the card (Alaska/Hawaiian Airlines).
- Up to $120 Airport Security Statement Credit every four years for TSA PreCheck/Global Entry.
- $50 Instant Travel Delay Credit for 2+ hour delays/cancellations.
- Waived same-day flight change fees (up to $75 savings).

APR
- 19.49% - 27.49% variable (no introductory APR).

Balance Transfer Fee
- 5% of each transaction.

Foreign Transaction Fee
- None.

Issued by Bank of America, N.A. Alaska Airlines/Hawaiian Airlines/Atmos Rewards program.
