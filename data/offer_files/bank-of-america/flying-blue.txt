Air France KLM Visa Signature (Bank of America / Flying Blue) — formerly "Air France KLM Flying Blue World Elite Mastercard"

Source: https://www.bankofamerica.com/credit-cards/products/air-france-credit-card/

REBRAND/NETWORK NOTE: The backlog lists this as "Air France KLM Flying Blue World Elite [Mastercard]." The current official product, per the fetched Bank of America page, is the "Air France KLM Visa Signature® Credit Card" — i.e. it has moved from Mastercard to Visa Signature network. Per third-party reporting, the transition from the old World Elite Mastercard to the new Visa card was expected to start in March 2026, with existing cardholders getting the new perks. Same $89 annual fee carried over from the prior Mastercard product.

Annual Fee
- $89

Signup Bonus
- 50,000 Bonus Miles + 100 Experience Points (XP) after making $2,000 or more in purchases within the first 90 days of account opening. (Some third-party/limited-time reporting cites a 70,000-mile offer — verify current live offer against the official page at curation time, promotions vary.)

Earn Rates
- 3 Miles per $1 on eligible Air France, KLM, and SkyTeam airline purchases.
- 3 Miles per $1 on eligible dining purchases.
- 1.5 Miles per $1 on all other purchases.

Elite Status Credits (Experience Points / XP)
- 20 XP annually on account anniversary.
- Additional 60 XP (80 XP total) if $15,000+ spent in the anniversary year.
- Additional 60 XP (140-160 XP total per some sources) if $25,000+ spent in the anniversary year. (Note: sources conflict slightly on cumulative XP totals at each spend tier — 80 XP/100 XP/160 XP appear in different summaries; verify exact tiered structure against the official Terms.)
- Anniversary bonus: 5,000 Miles annually after spending $50+ in that year.

APR
- 19.49% - 27.49% variable (no introductory APR period).

Balance Transfer Fee
- 5% of each transaction.

Foreign Transaction Fee
- None — no foreign transaction fees when making purchases outside the United States.

Other Benefits
- $0 Liability Guarantee for fraudulent transactions.
- Balance Connect® overdraft protection available.
- Digital wallet compatibility (Apple Pay, Google Pay, Samsung Pay).

Issued by Bank of America, N.A.
