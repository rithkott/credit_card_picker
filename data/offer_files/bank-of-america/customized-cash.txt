Customized Cash Rewards (Bank of America)

Source: https://www.bankofamerica.com/credit-cards/products/cash-back-credit-card/

Annual Fee
- $0

Signup Bonus
- $200 online cash rewards bonus after making at least $1,000 in purchases in the first 90 days of account opening.

Earn Rates
- 6% cash back in the category of your choice — for the first year (then 3% thereafter, per third-party sources; official page states "for the first year" for the 6% rate). Choice categories: gas and EV charging, online shopping, dining, travel, drug stores, or home improvement.
- Automatic 2% cash back at grocery stores and wholesale clubs.
- Unlimited 1% cash back on all other purchases.
- Cap: 6% and 2% rates apply to the first $2,500 in combined purchases each quarter in the choice category + grocery store/wholesale club purchases combined; once the quarterly cap is reached, those purchases earn 1% for the remainder of the quarter.

APR
- 0% Intro APR for 15 billing cycles for purchases, and for any balance transfers made in the first 60 days.
- After the intro APR offer ends, Variable APR currently 17.49% - 27.49%.

Balance Transfer Fee
- 3% intro balance transfer fee for the first 60 days the account is open.
- After the intro balance transfer fee offer ends, the fee for future balance transfers is 5%.

Foreign Transaction Fee
- Not stated on official product page (third-party aggregators report 3%).

Other Benefits
- $0 Liability Guarantee for fraudulent transactions.
- Balance Connect® overdraft protection.
- Digital wallet support (Apple Pay, Google Pay, Samsung Pay).
- Paperless statements, online/mobile banking, account alerts.
- BofA Rewards™ / Preferred Rewards program: eligible cardholders can earn a 25%-75% bonus on cash back based on Bank of America/Merrill relationship tier (Gold/Platinum/Platinum Honors).

Issued by Bank of America, N.A.
