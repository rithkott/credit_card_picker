Royal ONE Visa Signature (Bank of America / Royal Caribbean Group) — $0 AF
Source: https://www.bankofamerica.com/credit-cards/products/royal-one-credit-card/

Replaced the prior single "Royal Caribbean® Visa Signature® Credit Card" as of a March 2026 Bank of America / Royal Caribbean Group launch of a two-tier lineup (this card + Royal ONE Plus) covering Royal Caribbean, Celebrity Cruises, and Silversea. Existing cardholders approved on or before December 31, 2025 began migrating to this card (the no-AF tier) starting June 2026.

Annual Fee: $0

Signup Bonus: 45,000 bonus points, redeemable for $450 in onboard credit or cruise discounts, after spending $2,000 on purchases within the first 90 days of account opening.

Earn Rates:
- 3 points per $1 on eligible Royal Caribbean, Celebrity Cruises, and Silversea purchases.
- 2 points per $1 on grocery, gas, and EV charging stations.
- 1 point per $1 on all other purchases.

Anniversary Reward: $100 cruise discount after spending $10,000 in the anniversary year.

APR: 17.49% - 27.49% variable (no introductory APR).
Balance Transfer Fee: 5% of each transaction.
Foreign Transaction Fee: None.
Other Benefits: Priority boarding for Royal Caribbean and Celebrity Cruises; access to exclusive cardholder events; Visa Signature travel protections.

Redemption: Points can only be redeemed for cruise-related rewards (onboard credit, cruise discounts). Points valued at 1 cent each; minimum redemption 5,000 points for $50 of credit/discount.

Issued by Bank of America, N.A.
