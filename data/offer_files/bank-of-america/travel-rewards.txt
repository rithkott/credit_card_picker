Travel Rewards (Bank of America)

Source: https://www.bankofamerica.com/credit-cards/products/travel-rewards-credit-card/

Annual Fee
- $0

Signup Bonus
- 25,000 online bonus points (a $250 value) after making at least $1,000 in purchases in the first 90 days of account opening.

Earn Rates
- Unlimited 1.5 points for every $1 spent on all purchases everywhere, every time.

APR
- 0% Intro APR for the first 15 billing cycles for purchases, and for any balance transfers made within the first 60 days.
- Standard Variable APR thereafter: 17.49% to 27.49%.

Balance Transfer Fee
- 3% intro balance transfer fee for the first 60 days; 5% for future transfers after intro period ends.

Foreign Transaction Fee
- No foreign transaction fees.

Redemption
- Points redeemable as a statement credit to pay for flights, hotel stays, vacation rentals, baggage fees, and at restaurants (including takeout) — no blackout dates, redeem toward any travel purchase.

Other Benefits
- Balance Connect® for overdraft protection.
- $0 Liability Guarantee for fraudulent transactions.
- Digital wallet compatibility.
- Access to Online and Mobile Banking.
- BofA Rewards™ / Preferred Rewards program: eligible cardholders earn bonus rewards (25%-75% boost) on purchases based on Bank of America/Merrill relationship tier.
