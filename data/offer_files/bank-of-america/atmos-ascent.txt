Atmos Rewards Ascent Visa Signature (Bank of America / Alaska Airlines)

Source: https://www.bankofamerica.com/credit-cards/products/alaska-airlines-credit-card/

REBRAND NOTE: This is the rebranded former "Alaska Airlines Visa Signature® credit card." In August 2025, Alaska Airlines officially rebranded its credit cards and combined loyalty program (merging with Hawaiian Airlines) under the new name "Atmos Rewards." Official current product name found: "Atmos™ Rewards Ascent Visa Signature® Alaska Airlines Credit Card." Existing cardholders keep benefits uninterrupted; newly-branded cards are issued at renewal/for new applicants.

Annual Fee
- $95

Signup Bonus
- "Buy one ticket, get one for just the taxes and fees" ($0 fare plus taxes and fees from $23) plus 50,000 bonus points, after spending $2,000 in purchases within the first 90 days of account opening.

Earn Rates
- 3 points per $1 on eligible Alaska Airlines and Hawaiian Airlines purchases.
- 2 points per $1 on gas, EV charging, cable, streaming services, and local transit (including rideshare).
- 1 point per $1 on all other purchases.
- 10% bonus on all points earned with an eligible Bank of America deposit/Merrill account (Preferred Rewards).

Companion Fare Benefit
- Annual $99 Companion Fare (plus taxes and fees from $23) after spending $6,000+ in the prior anniversary year — now valid for all Alaska Airlines and Hawaiian Airlines flights within North America (per the merged program).

Other Benefits
- $100 off an annual Alaska Lounge+ Membership purchased with the card.
- Free checked bag and preferred boarding for cardholder and up to 6 guests on the same reservation when paying with the card.
- 20% back on Alaska Airlines and Hawaiian Airlines inflight purchases.
- No blackout dates on award flights.

APR
- 19.49% - 27.49% variable (no introductory APR).

Balance Transfer Fee
- 5% of each transaction.

Foreign Transaction Fee
- No foreign transaction fees (per aggregated official card-directory listing; not independently confirmed on the dedicated product page fetch).

Issued by Bank of America, N.A. Alaska Airlines/Hawaiian Airlines/Atmos Rewards program.
