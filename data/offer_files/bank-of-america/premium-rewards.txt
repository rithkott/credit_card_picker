Premium Rewards (Bank of America)

Source: https://www.bankofamerica.com/credit-cards/products/premium-rewards-credit-card/

Annual Fee
- $95 (not waived first year)

Signup Bonus
- 60,000 online bonus points — a $600 value — after making at least $4,000 in purchases in the first 90 days of account opening.

Earn Rates
- 2 points for every $1 spent on travel and dining purchases.
- 1.5 points per $1 on all other purchases.
- No introductory APR offered on this card.

APR
- 19.49% - 27.49% variable (no intro APR).

Balance Transfer Fee
- 5% of each transaction.

Foreign Transaction Fee
- No foreign transaction fees.

Credits/Benefits
- Up to $100 in Airline Incidental Statement Credits annually for qualifying purchases such as seat upgrades, baggage fees, in-flight services, and airline lounge fees.
- Up to $100 in Airport Security Statement Credits for TSA PreCheck® or Global Entry application fees, every four years.
- Points do not expire while account remains open.
- Multiple redemption options: cash back, Merrill account credits, statement credits, gift cards.
- Visa Signature® Luxury Hotel Collection benefits (late checkout, room upgrades, etc.).
- Travel, purchase, and automotive protections included.
- Complimentary concierge service access.
- BofA Rewards™ / Preferred Rewards program: eligible cardholders earn a bonus (25%-75%) on points based on Bank of America/Merrill relationship tier — at Platinum Honors, 2x travel/dining effectively becomes ~3.5x and 1.5x base becomes ~2.62x.

Issued by Bank of America, N.A.
