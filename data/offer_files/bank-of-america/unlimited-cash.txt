Unlimited Cash Rewards (Bank of America)

Source: https://www.bankofamerica.com/credit-cards/products/unlimited-cash-back-credit-card/

Annual Fee
- $0

Signup Bonus
- $200 online cash rewards bonus after making at least $1,000 in purchases in the first 90 days of account opening.

Earn Rates
- 2% cash back on purchases for the first year from account opening.
- Thereafter, unlimited 1.5% cash back on all purchases.
- No limit to the amount of cash back that can be earned; cash rewards do not expire as long as the account remains open.

APR
- 0% Intro APR for the first 15 billing cycles for purchases, and for any balance transfers made within the first 60 days.
- Standard APR thereafter: 17.49% to 27.49% variable.

Balance Transfer Fee
- 3% intro balance transfer fee for the first 60 days the account is open.
- After the intro balance transfer fee offer ends, the fee for all future balance transfers is 5%.

Foreign Transaction Fee
- Not stated on official product page.

Other Benefits
- $0 Liability Guarantee for fraudulent transactions.
- Balance Connect® overdraft protection (no transfer fees).
- Paperless statement option.
- Digital wallet support (Apple Pay, Google Pay, Samsung Pay).
- Online and mobile banking access, account alerts.
- BofA Rewards™ / Preferred Rewards program: eligible cardholders can earn bonus rewards (25%-75% boost) on purchases using eligible Bank of America credit cards, based on relationship tier.

Issued by Bank of America, N.A.
