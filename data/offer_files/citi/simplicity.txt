Citi Simplicity® Card

FEES
Annual Fee: None ($0)
Foreign Transaction Fee: 3% of each purchase transaction in U.S. dollars.

APPROVAL / ELIGIBILITY NOTES
- Credit limits may be as low as $500.

CARD TYPE / VALUE PROP
- 0% APR / balance-transfer focused card. No rewards program (no points/cash back/miles) is disclosed on this page. Intro offers: 0% APR on purchases for 18 months; 0% APR on balance transfers for 18 months (transfers must complete within 4 months of account opening).
- Notably $0 late payment fee (a differentiator vs typical Citi cards' late fees), no penalty APR disclosed.
- No signup bonus, no ongoing earn rates, no credits are offered on this card per this source.
