Citi Strata Elite℠ Card

FEES
Annual Fee: $595 ($75 per Authorized User)
Foreign Transaction Fee: None

APPROVAL / ELIGIBILITY NOTES
- Credit limits may be as low as $5,000.
- Signup bonus not available if you received a new-account bonus for a Citi Strata Elite account in the past 48 months, or converted a Citi card that earned a new-account bonus in the last 48 months into a Strata Elite.
- Must maintain an open AAdvantage account to redeem the Admirals Club Strata Elite Passes and other AA-related benefits.

EARN RATES (Citi ThankYou Points, standard ongoing rates unless a limited-time offer applies)
- 12 points per $1 on hotels, car rentals, and attractions booked through Citi Travel (cititravel.com or phone); portal-only.
- 6 points per $1 on air travel booked through Citi Travel; portal-only.
- 6 points per $1 at restaurants (incl. delivery) during "Citi Nights" — purchases authorized Fri 6PM-Sat 6AM ET and Sat 6PM-Sun 6AM ET (based on authorization time, not posting time).
- 3 points per $1 at restaurants (incl. delivery) outside Citi Nights. Excludes bakeries, caterers, restaurants inside another business (hotels, stores, stadiums, grocery stores, warehouse clubs).
- 1.5 points per $1 on everything else.
- No points earned on the portion of a Citi Travel hotel purchase offset by the $300 Annual Hotel Benefit (see below).
- Citi Travel is powered by Rocket Travel by Agoda. MCC determines category qualification; Citi does not control MCC assignment.

CREDITS
- Up to $300/year hotel benefit via Citi Travel: applies to 2+ night stays booked/prepaid through Citi Travel; unused portion (if stay <$300) carries to next qualifying booking same calendar year; restored on cancellation (but forfeits if cancelled in a later calendar year than booked); cannot combine with Citi Prestige's 4th-night-free benefit in the same transaction; primary cardmember must make the reservation; package rates (air+hotel, hotel+car) don't qualify; portal-only (Citi Travel).
- Up to $200/year "Splurge Credit": cardholder selects up to 2 merchants (via citi.com/splurgecredit) to activate; credits post for purchases at activated merchant(s) up to $200/year; activation is time-of-authorization sensitive; special carve-outs for Live Nation/Ticketmaster (direct site purchases only, US venues only) and American Airlines (billed by AA/AA Vacations as merchant of record; excludes car rentals, hotel reservations, AAdvantage status boost/renewal, AA Cargo).
- Up to $200/year Blacklane chauffeur credit: up to $100 for Jan-Jun and up to $100 for Jul-Dec (based on Blacklane-reported sale date, not service date); direct Blacklane app/site bookings only; unused portion within a half carries to next qualifying purchase in that half.
- Citigold Private Client banking-relationship credit: $595 statement credit in year 1 and $145/year thereafter, ONLY if primary cardmember also holds a Citigold Private Client tier deposit account when the annual fee posts (existing customers can "Up-Tier" by meeting balance requirements for 3 consecutive months). Posts within 3 billing cycles of annual fee charge.
- Citigold (non-Private-Client) banking-relationship credit: $145/year statement credit under the same annual-fee/deposit-tier-at-charge-time mechanics as above, but requiring only the standard Citigold Relationship Tier.
- Global Entry or TSA PreCheck application fee credit: one statement credit up to $120 every 4 years, for whichever program's fee is charged first to the card.
- Credits generally require enrollment/activation as described and post as statement credits, typically 1-3 billing cycles; not applied toward the Minimum Payment Due.

NOTABLE PERKS (benefit_flags candidates)
- Priority Pass Select lounge membership (complimentary) for primary cardmember and Authorized Users: 1,500+ airport lounges (lounge access only, not restaurants/cafes/markets in the network); up to 2 guests per visit included, $35/guest for additional guests where allowed; requires physical card + same-day boarding pass.
- Four digital Admirals Club "Strata Elite Passes" per calendar year for the primary cardmember: each admits the cardmember plus up to 3 accompanying adults (18+) for 24 hours from redemption; unused passes forfeit at year end; requires same-day boarding pass on AA/oneworld/Alaska-operated flight plus government ID.
- The Reserve hotel benefits (Strata Elite/Strata Premier/Prestige cardmembers booking via Citi Travel "The Reserve" rates): ~$100 property-determined experience credit, room upgrade (availability-based), daily breakfast for two, complimentary wifi, early check-in/late check-out (availability-based); one award per stay, no refund for unused portion; portal-only.
- Hotel Collection benefits (any Citi Travel-eligible cardmember booking Hotel Collection rates): daily breakfast for two, complimentary wifi, early check-in/late check-out (availability-based); portal-only.
- Travel protection benefits (underwritten by New Hampshire Insurance Company, an AIG company); secondary coverage generally, except MasterRental (primary rental insurance) which is primary outside the renter's home country — treat as primary_rental_insurance flag.
- Misc lifestyle access perks (not directly monetizable): Death & Co "Fashioned" platform access (card as payment method to enroll), The Supper Club dining event ticket access (purchase with card).

No public signup bonus dollar/point amount was present in this source file (only the 48-month eligibility-restriction clause is shown, implying a bonus offer exists elsewhere).
