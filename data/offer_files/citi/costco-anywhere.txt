Costco Anywhere Visa® Card by Citi

FEES
Annual Fee: $0 with paid Costco membership.
Foreign Transaction Fee: None

APPROVAL / ELIGIBILITY NOTES
- Requires a current paid Costco membership in the primary cardholder's name; each Authorized User must also have their own Costco membership. Account/rewards program can be cancelled if membership lapses.
- Credit limits may be as low as $800.
- Costco membership fee ($65/yr Gold Star/Business, +$65/yr Executive upgrade, +$65/yr per Affiliate Business Member) auto-renews onto this card unless a different payment method is chosen or auto-renew is cancelled — not a card reward, just a billing mechanic worth knowing about.

EARN RATES (Cash Back Rewards Program)
- 5% cash back on gas at Costco warehouses worldwide; 4% on other eligible gas and EV charging worldwide — combined cap of $7,000/year in Costco+other gas/EV spend at the elevated rate, 1% thereafter. Excludes gas/EV bought at some superstores/supermarkets/warehouse clubs other than Costco, and non-automotive fuel use (full excluded-merchant list at citi.com/Costco_Exclusions).
- 3% cash back on restaurants (cafes, bars, lounges, fast food) and eligible travel worldwide (airfare, hotels, car rentals, travel agencies, cruise lines, Costco Travel). Excludes bakeries, certain in-department-store restaurants/cafes, grocery/warehouse-club restaurants, timeshares, campgrounds, B&Bs, train/commuter travel — those earn 1%.
- 2% cash back on all other Costco and Costco.com purchases.
- 1% cash back on everything else.
- MCC determines category qualification; Citi does not control MCC assignment.

REWARD MECHANICS (important for realistic capture)
- Rewards distributed annually as a single certificate after the February billing statement closes, covering the prior Jan-Dec period.
- Certificate redeemable only in person at a US Costco warehouse (incl. Puerto Rico) for merchandise or cash, through Dec 31 of the year issued; unredeemed balance forfeits.
- Rewards over $10,000/year may instead be paid by check/electronic transfer at Citi/Costco's discretion.
- Must maintain Costco membership to redeem; account must be open (not closed) when the certificate is scheduled to be sent, or the reward forfeits. No reward issued if balance is under $1.
- Cash back has no value until redeemed; not transferable, assignable, or inheritable in the usual sense (paid to estate via check on cardholder death).
