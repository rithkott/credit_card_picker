Citi Strata℠ Card

FEES
Annual Fee: None ($0)
Foreign Transaction Fee: 3% of each purchase transaction in U.S. dollars.

APPROVAL / ELIGIBILITY NOTES
- Credit limits may be as low as $500.
- Signup bonus not available if you received a new-account bonus for a Citi Strata, Citi Strata Student, Citi Rewards+, or Citi Rewards+ Student account in the past 48 months, or converted a Citi card that earned a new-account bonus in the last 48 months into one of those products.

EARN RATES (Citi ThankYou Points, standard ongoing rates unless a limited-time offer applies)
- 5 points per $1 on hotels, car rentals, and attractions booked through Citi Travel (cititravel.com or phone); portal-only. Citi Travel is powered by Rocket Travel by Agoda. No points on cancelled bookings; benefit ends if account is closed/converted.
- 3 points per $1 at supermarkets. Excludes general merchandise/discount superstores, freezer/meat locker provisioners, dairy stores, food/convenience stores, markets, drugstores, warehouse/wholesale clubs, specialty vendors, bakeries, candy/nut/confectionery stores, meal-kit delivery, and online supermarkets not using the supermarket MCC.
- 3 points per $1 on "Select Transit" (car rentals, ferries, commuter rail, subway, taxi/limo/car service, passenger rail, tolls, parking, buses, RV rentals, EV charging) and Gas Stations. Excludes bike/scooter rentals, auto clubs/insurance; gas exclusions: warehouse/wholesale clubs, superstores, discount stores, department stores, convenience stores not using gas station MCC.
- 3 points per $1 in one Self-Select Category chosen by the cardholder from: Fitness Clubs, Select Streaming Services, Live Entertainment, Cosmetic Stores/Barber Shops/Hair Salons, or Pet Supply Stores. Default category is Select Streaming Services until changed. Changeable once per calendar quarter via citi.com/selfselect or phone; new category takes effect the day after selection (12:00 AM CT). Fine print per option:
  - Streaming: only listed named providers (Amazon Prime Video, Apple Music, Disney+, DirecTV Stream, ESPN+, Fandango at Home, fuboTV, HBO Max, Hulu, MLB.TV, NBA League Pass, Netflix, Pandora, Paramount+, Sling TV, Spotify, Starz, SiriusXM, Tidal, YouTube Premium, YouTube TV); excludes bundled/third-party-billed charges. List subject to change.
  - Fitness Clubs: membership/other fees at athletic/sports/rec facilities requiring membership; excludes virtual/home-equipment/streaming-class fees.
  - Live Entertainment: concerts, live sports, theater, amusement parks, orchestras; excludes charity events, sports camps, golf/country clubs, bowling, movie theaters, tourist attractions, museums, art galleries.
  - Cosmetic Stores/Barber Shops/Hair Salons: excludes cosmetics bought at department stores/drugstores/supermarkets unless coded as Cosmetic Stores; excludes barber/salon services inside spas/hotels unless separately coded.
  - Pet Supply Stores: excludes pet items at drugstores/markets/wholesale clubs/discount superstores/supermarkets; vet services excluded unless coded as Pet Supply Stores.
- 2 points per $1 at restaurants (cafes, bars, lounges, fast food, delivery, takeout; restaurant gift cards if coded as restaurant). Excludes bakeries, caterers, and restaurants located inside another business (hotels, stores, stadiums, grocery stores, warehouse clubs).
- 1 point per $1 on everything else.
- MCC determines category qualification; Citi does not control MCC assignment.

Points program: Citi ThankYou Rewards (points post at end of billing cycle; redemption details at thankyou.com/terms).

No public signup bonus dollar/point amount was present in this source file (only the 48-month eligibility-restriction clause is shown, implying a bonus offer exists elsewhere).
