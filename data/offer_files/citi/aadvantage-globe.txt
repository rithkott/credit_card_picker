Citi® / AAdvantage® Globe™ Mastercard®

FEES
Annual Fee: $350
Foreign Transaction Fee: None

APPROVAL / ELIGIBILITY NOTES
- Must have an open AAdvantage® program membership; account can be closed if AAdvantage® membership is deactivated.
- Credit limits may be as low as $3,500.
- New-account AAdvantage bonus miles are NOT available if received a new account bonus for a Globe account in the past 48 months, or converted another Citi card that earned a new account bonus in the last 48 months into a Globe account.
- Authorized Users do not receive first checked bag free, boarding benefit, or the Four Admirals Club Passes.

EARN RATES
- Base: 1 AAdvantage mile per $1.
- 3X miles on eligible American Airlines purchases (billed by AA as merchant of record; excludes AAdvantage status boost/renewal and AA Cargo).
- 6X miles on hotels booked through aadvantagehotels.com (excludes AA Vacations bookings and "Reserve Now, Pay Later").
- 2X miles at restaurants (cafes, bars, lounges, fast food, delivery, takeout). Excludes: supercenters, warehouse clubs, discount stores, restaurants inside department stores, bowling alleys, golf courses, country clubs, convenience stores, movie theaters, caterers, meal-kit delivery. (Admirals Club F&B purchases earn 3X instead, under the AA-purchases rate.)
- 2X miles on "Rides and Rails" (ferries, commuter rail, subway, taxi/limo/rideshare, passenger rail, tolls, parking, buses). Excludes charter/tour buses, car rental agencies, RV rentals.
- Miles post 8-10 weeks after qualifying purchases; balance transfers, cash advances, fees, interest, money-order-like transactions do not earn miles.
- No mileage cap. Miles do not expire while the account is open.

LOYALTY POINTS (status-qualifying, not redeemable)
- 1 Loyalty Point per eligible AAdvantage base mile (bonus/accelerator miles excluded).
- Flight Streak™ bonus: 5,000 Loyalty Points per 4 qualifying AA/American Eagle flight segments flown (including Basic Economy), up to 3x (15,000 points) per status-qualification year (March-February). Award tickets and pre-cardmembership flights don't qualify.

CREDITS
- Up to $100/year statement credit on American Airlines inflight purchases (domestic AA/American Eagle-operated flights only; excludes codeshares operated by other carriers).
- American Airlines Companion Certificate: $99 domestic economy companion fare (plus taxes/fees) issued annually after account renewal; account must stay open one full billing cycle past the anniversary month; ~8-10 week posting; valid only for travel within the 48 contiguous states (AK/HI/PR/USVI residents get some added routing); not valid on oneworld carriers or AA codeshares.
- Four digital Admirals Club® "Globe Passes" per calendar year for the primary cardmember: each pass admits the cardmember plus up to 3 accompanying adults (18+) for 24 hours from redemption, valid across multiple lounges in that window; unused passes forfeit at year end. Requires same-day boarding pass on AA/oneworld/Alaska-operated flight plus government ID.
- Up to $240/year on Turo: $30 statement credit per completed Turo trip (up to 8 trips/year), for bookings made directly on turo.com/app with the card linked to the Turo account (one linked account per card); digital-wallet payments don't count.
- Up to $100/year "Splurge Credit": cardholder selects up to 2 merchants (via citi.com/splurgecredit) to activate; credits post for purchases at the activated merchant(s) up to $100/year total, activation/deactivation is time-of-purchase sensitive; merchant list subject to change.
- Global Entry or TSA PreCheck application fee credit: one statement credit up to $120 every 4 years, for whichever program's fee is charged first to the card.
- 25% savings (as statement credit) on inflight food/beverage on AA-operated domestic flights (not wifi).
- Credits generally require enrollment/linking as described above and post as statement credits, typically 1-2 billing cycles (some 8-10 weeks).

NOTABLE PERKS (benefit_flags candidates)
- Preferred boarding: boards after priority boarding, before rest of economy (Group 5); up to 8 companions on same reservation.
- Travel protection benefits included (underwritten by New Hampshire Insurance Company, an AIG company); secondary coverage within the renter's home country.
- Mastercard ID Theft Protection (via Generali Global Assistance) — opt-in enrollment required.
