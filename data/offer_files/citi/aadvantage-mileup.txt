American Airlines AAdvantage® MileUp® Card

FEES
Annual Fee: None ($0)
Foreign Transaction Fee: 3% of each purchase transaction in U.S. dollars.

APPROVAL / ELIGIBILITY NOTES
- Must have an open AAdvantage® program membership; account can be closed if AAdvantage® membership is deactivated.
- Credit limits may be as low as $1,000.
- New-account AAdvantage bonus miles are NOT available if received a new account bonus for a MileUp account in the past 48 months, or converted another Citi card that earned a new account bonus in the last 48 months into a MileUp account.

EARN RATES
- Base: 1 AAdvantage mile per $1.
- 2X miles on eligible American Airlines purchases (billed by AA as merchant of record; excludes car rentals, hotel reservations, AAdvantage status boost/renewal, AA Cargo).
- 2X miles on grocery store purchases (supermarkets, freezer/meat locker provisioners, dairy stores, food/convenience stores, markets, specialty vendors, bakeries). Excludes: general merchandise/discount superstores, wholesale/warehouse clubs, candy/confectionery stores, cafes, bars, lounges, fast food.
- Miles post 8-10 weeks after qualifying purchases; balance transfers, cash advances, fees, interest, money-order-like transactions do not earn miles.
- No mileage cap.

LOYALTY POINTS (status-qualifying, not redeemable)
- 1 Loyalty Point per eligible AAdvantage base mile (bonus/accelerator miles excluded).

CREDITS / PERKS
- 25% savings (as statement credit) on inflight food/beverage purchases on AA-operated domestic flights (not wifi); posts 8-10 weeks after transaction.
- Mastercard ID Theft Protection (via Generali Global Assistance) — opt-in enrollment required.

No public signup bonus terms were present in this source file (not shown on this disclosure page).
