Macy's Card (Citi / Citibank, N.A.)

Note: Citi issues two Macy's-branded cards — this file covers only the STORE-ONLY "Macy's Card" (closed-loop, usable only at Macy's, Macy's Backstage, and macys.com). A separate open-loop "Macy's American Express Card" also exists and is NOT covered here. When applying, Citi may consider you for both cards and let you choose if approved for both.

Annual Fee
- No annual fee.

Closed-Loop
- Store-only card. Usable at Macy's, Macy's Backstage, and macys.com only — not accepted anywhere Amex/Visa/Mastercard is accepted (that's the separate Macy's American Express Card).

Star Rewards Tiers (loyalty program; card ownership affects tier and earn rate)
- Bronze: default status for shoppers without a Macy's Credit Card. Earn 1 point per $1 on Rewards Earning Purchases at Macy's, Macy's Backstage, and macys.com.
- Silver: starting status for new Macy's Credit Card accounts. Earn 2 points per $1 on Rewards Earning Purchases.
- Gold: Macy's Credit Cardholders are upgraded to Gold 7 days after spending $500–$1,199 at Macy's on the card within a calendar year. Earn 3 points per $1.
- Platinum: Macy's Credit Cardholders are upgraded to Platinum 7 days after spending $1,200+ at Macy's on the card within a calendar year. Earn 5 points per $1.
- Points-to-reward conversion: 1,000 points = $10 in "Star Money."
- Star Money is valid for 30 calendar days from issuance; check expiration at macys.com/mystarrewards or at checkout.
- Star Money can be applied to nearly all purchases (including items that rarely go on sale, e.g. top brands/beauty/designer) but NOT to Gift Cards or services/fees. Furniture and mattress orders on macys.com require calling Macy's Customer Service to redeem Star Money.
- In-store redemption requires providing the phone number linked to the Star Rewards membership; online redemption requires being signed in with membership linked to the account/wallet.

Free Shipping by Tier (when paying with the Macy's Credit Card)
- Bronze (no card): free shipping with $39 minimum.
- Silver: free shipping with $25 minimum.
- Gold and Platinum: free shipping with no minimum.
- Standard shipping only, to one location in the contiguous U.S.; upgrade fees may still apply for premium/overnight/same-day delivery. Does not apply to furniture, mattresses, rugs, or prior purchases.

Signup / New-Account Offer
- New cardholders who receive immediate approval get 20% off eligible merchandise in Macy's stores and 30% off eligible merchandise on macys.com, valid the day of approval and the following day, capped at a combined $100 in total savings across both days.
- Applicants whose accounts require additional review/processing (not immediately approved) do not receive this discount.
- Exclusions: services, select licensed departments, Apple products, smart watches/jewelry, select tech accessories, Gift Cards, restaurants, gourmet food and wine.
- Cannot be combined with other discount offers except where specifically indicated.

Financing / Deferred-Interest Offers (special financing on furniture & mattresses, credit approval required)
- No interest if paid in full within 12 months: furniture or mattress purchases of $499+.
- No interest if paid in full within 18 months: furniture purchases of $1,499+ or mattress purchases of $999+.
- No interest if paid in full within 24 months: furniture purchases of $2,499+ or mattress purchases of $1,999+.
- No interest if paid in full within 36 months: select furniture purchases of $2,999+ or mattress purchases of $2,499+.
- If the balance is not paid in full within the promotional period, interest is charged retroactively from the original purchase date. Minimum payments required; offer valid for consumer accounts in good standing.

Perks
- Birthday surprise: primary cardholder (and Bronze members) receive a birthday-month surprise (value varies); requires a Macy's/macys.com purchase in the prior 12 months, a current mailing address on file, and not having opted out of marketing mail.
- Star Passes: cardholder-exclusive 25%-off promo codes usable on items that rarely go on sale (e.g. cosmetics, fragrances), redeemable any day the member chooses; exclusions disclosed per Star Pass.
- Star Money Bonus Days: periodic promotional days where members earn points at an elevated, offer-specific rate on Macy's purchases.
- Account management via Macy's App / macys.com; "Macy's Pay" for contactless in-store payment.

Fees / Rate Notes (from Citi disclosures, partial — see gap note below)
- Purchase APR: 32.74% (variable, tied to Prime Rate), as disclosed effective December 16, 2025.
- Cash Advance Fee: greater of $5.00 or 4% of the amount of each cash advance.
- Minimum Interest Charge: $2.00.

Redemption
- Points convert automatically to Star Money (1,000 points = $10); no manual redemption/choice mechanism — Star Money is issued and applied at checkout as described above.
