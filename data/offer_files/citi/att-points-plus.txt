AT&T Points Plus® Card from Citi

FEES
Annual Fee: None ($0)
Foreign Transaction Fee: None

APPROVAL / ELIGIBILITY NOTES
- Citi issues only one AT&T Points Plus account per person.
- Credit limits may be as low as $1,500.
- Signup bonus not available if you received a new-account bonus for this card in the past 48 months.
- Points earned by an Authorized User post to the primary cardholder's ThankYou account.

EARN RATES (Citi ThankYou Points, standard ongoing rates unless a limited-time offer applies)
- 3 points per $1 at gas and EV charging stations (must use gas/EV station MCC; excludes warehouse clubs, discount stores, convenience stores not coded as such).
- 2 points per $1 at grocery stores (supermarkets, grocery delivery, meat/seafood, dairy, bakeries, food/convenience stores). Excludes general merchandise/discount superstores, wholesale/warehouse clubs, candy/nut/confectionery stores.
- 2 points per $1 on products/services purchased directly from AT&T, including monthly phone/internet bill payments. Excludes independent wireless dealers, non-AT&T-owned stores, and AT&T resellers (unless paying an AT&T service bill).
- 1 point per $1 on all other purchases.
- Points post at the end of the billing cycle in which purchase was made; balance transfers, cash advances, fees, interest, money-order-like transactions do not earn points.

CREDITS
- Recurring statement credit: $20 per billing cycle when net new purchases (spend minus returns/refunds) reach $1,000+ in that cycle.
- AT&T AutoPay + paperless billing discount (provided by AT&T, not a card benefit): $10/month per wireless line and/or internet bill ($5/month for AT&T Internet Air) when enrolled in both paperless billing and AutoPay using a bank account or this card; reduced to $5/month if AutoPay uses a debit card; no discount for AutoPay with any other credit card. AT&T employees/retirees not eligible.

Points program: Citi ThankYou Rewards (points/redemption details at thankyou.com/terms).

No public signup bonus terms were present in this source file (not shown on this disclosure page); note text references a "Bonus Offer" eligibility clause implying one may exist/have existed but no dollar/point amount is disclosed here.
