Fees
Annual Fee: None.
Foreign Transaction: 3% of each purchase transaction.

Earn Rates
- 1 ThankYou point per $1 spent on purchases, plus 1 additional point per $1 paid on the purchase balance (as tracked by the Purchase Tracker) — net 2% back on spend paid off, 1% if unpaid.
- 3 additional points/$1 (5 total combined w/ purchase+payment points) on hotels, car rentals, and attractions booked via Citi Travel (cititravel.com) — portal-only.
- Points don't earn on: balance transfers, cash advances, Citi Flex Loans/Pay, cash-like transactions, gift/prepaid card loads, person-to-person payments, fees/interest.
- Account must stay current to keep earning; missing a Minimum Payment Due suspends point-earning for that cycle and until reinstated.

Redemption
10,000 ThankYou points = $100 direct deposit/statement credit/check. Also redeemable for gift cards, travel, etc. at varying values.

Signup Bonus
No specific bonus amount stated in source. Bonus ThankYou points not available if you received a new account bonus for Double Cash in the past 48 months, or converted another Citi account with a bonus in the last 48 months into Double Cash.

Approval
Some credit limits may be as low as $500.
