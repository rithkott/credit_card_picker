Home Depot Consumer Credit Card (Citi / Citibank, N.A.)

Note: Home Depot/Citi issues multiple cards. This file covers ONLY the standard "Home Depot® Consumer Credit Card" (closed-loop store card, usable only at The Home Depot and homedepot.com). It does NOT cover the "Home Depot® Pro Xtra Credit Card" (business/professional-focused, automatic Pro Xtra loyalty enrollment, rewards earned "4x faster," employee authorized-user cards, business accounting/itemized-billing tools, rewards redeemable as Pro Xtra Dollars or Rental Perks) or the "Home Depot Commercial Revolving Charge Card" / "Home Depot Commercial Account." Those are out of scope here and have materially different terms.

Annual Fee
- No annual fee.

Closed-Loop
- Store-only card. Per Citi's official product page: "can only be used at The Home Depot stores and at homedepot.com" — not accepted anywhere Visa/Mastercard/Amex is accepted.

Signup / New-Account Offer
- Tiered discount on a new cardholder's first purchase (limited-time offer; per a secondary aggregator source, listed as valid through July 29, 2026 — treat expiration date as unconfirmed against an official page):
  - $25 off a first purchase of $25–$299.
  - $50 off a first purchase of $300–$999.
  - $100 off a first purchase of $1,000 or more.
- Official Citi page corroborates this in aggregate as "up to $100 savings on qualifying purchases" for new accounts, without breaking out the exact tiers in the fetched excerpt — tier breakdown above is aggregator-sourced (joinkudos.com) and NOT independently confirmed against raw official page text. Treat exact dollar/threshold tiers as best-available, not certain.
- Exact purchase window (e.g., "within 30 days of account opening") reported by one aggregator source but not confirmed on an official Citi/Home Depot page — see gaps below.

Financing / Deferred-Interest Offers (special financing, credit approval required)
- Standard/ongoing offer: 6 months no interest on purchases of $299 or more, if paid in full within 6 months. If not paid in full by the end of the promotional period, interest is charged retroactively from the original purchase date (deferred interest, not true 0% APR).
- Extended promotions: "Up to 24-Month Financing during Special Promotions throughout the year" per Citi's official page — exact durations (12/18/24 months) and which purchase categories/thresholds qualify were not itemized on the fetched official excerpt. A secondary aggregator source states 12–24 month extended terms are offered on items such as installed HVAC systems, but this pairing of duration-to-category was not confirmed against an official page.
- Per aggregator sources (unconfirmed against an official Schumer-box style page): for purchases of $299+, the cardholder may be offered a choice between a deferred-interest financing plan or a straight percentage-off discount in lieu of financing — this "discount vs. financing" choice mechanic could not be verified on an official Citi page and should be treated as unconfirmed.
- Deferred interest mechanic (confirmed pattern, standard across Citi retail cards): minimum payments are required during the promo period; if any balance remains unpaid when the promotional period ends, interest accrues retroactively from the date of purchase, not just from the end of the promo period.

Perks
- Extended returns: cardmembers get 1 year to make returns, rather than the standard non-cardholder return window (Home Depot's general non-cardholder policy is commonly reported as ~90 days) — official Citi page confirms: "Cardmembers get 1 year to make returns, not just 3 months."
- Flexible payment due date: cardholder may choose to have their payment due date fall at the beginning, middle, or end of the month.
- 24/7 customer service availability (per official Citi page).
- $0 liability on unauthorized charges (per official Citi page).
- No cash-advance capability — this is a closed-loop store card with no cash-advance feature (per aggregator consensus; consistent with the card having no cash-advance fee/APR disclosed anywhere found).

Fees / Rate Notes
- Purchase APR: reported by aggregator sources as a variable APR ranging from approximately 17.99% to 29.99% depending on applicant creditworthiness at time of application (one source cites a "standard"/go-to APR around 29.99%). NOT independently confirmed against an official Citi Schumer-box/rates-and-fees disclosure page — the official citi.com product page fetched for this file states only "See card agreement for details including APR applicable to you" without printing the actual rate table.
- Minimum Interest Charge: reported by an aggregator source as $2.00 — NOT independently confirmed against an official disclosure page.
- Cash Advance Fee: not applicable — card does not support cash advances per aggregator sources; no official confirmation located either way.
- Late payment fee, returned payment fee, penalty APR: not found in any source consulted.

Redemption / How the Account Works
- Confirmed: this card carries NO points/cash-back/rewards program. Value is delivered entirely through the new-account discount and periodic deferred-interest financing promotions — there is nothing to "redeem" in the way of points or statement credits on ongoing spend. (This is explicitly distinct from the separate Pro Xtra Credit Card, which does earn rewards redeemable as Pro Xtra Dollars/Rental Perks.)
