Citi® / AAdvantage® Executive World Elite Mastercard

FEES
Annual Fee: $595 ($175 per Authorized User for up to 3, $175 for each thereafter)
Foreign Transaction Fee: None
Balance Transfer / Cash Advance fees exist but are not rewards-relevant (excluded).

APPROVAL / ELIGIBILITY NOTES
- Must have an open AAdvantage® program membership; account can be closed if AAdvantage® membership is deactivated.
- Credit limits may be as low as $5,000.
- New-account AAdvantage bonus miles are NOT available if you received a new account bonus for a Citi/AAdvantage Executive account in the past 48 months, or converted another Citi card that earned a new account bonus in the last 48 months into an Executive account.
- Loyalty Points/miles earned by Authorized Users post to the primary cardmember's AAdvantage account; Authorized Users do not get first checked bag free or priority boarding.

EARN RATES
- Base: 1 AAdvantage mile per $1 on general purchases.
- 4X miles on eligible American Airlines purchases (billed by American Airlines as merchant of record; excludes AAdvantage status boost/renewal and AA Cargo). Increases to 5X on AA purchases for the remainder of the calendar year once cardholder spend (on this card) reaches $150,000 in that calendar year.
- 10X miles on hotels booked through aadvantagehotels.com (excludes AA Vacations bookings and "Reserve Now, Pay Later").
- 10X miles on car rentals booked through aadvantagecars.com, for bookings with US/Mexico/Canada point of sale (excludes Payless and AA Vacations bookings).
- Miles post 8-10 weeks after qualifying purchases; balance transfers, cash advances, fees, interest, money orders, gift-card-like transactions, etc. do not earn miles.
- Merchant category code (MCC) determines category qualification; Citi does not control MCC assignment.

LOYALTY POINTS (status-qualifying, not redeemable)
- 1 Loyalty Point per eligible AAdvantage base mile earned on purchases (bonus/accelerator miles excluded).
- Up to 20,000 additional Loyalty Points per qualifying year: 10,000 after earning 50,000 Loyalty Points in the year, another 10,000 if 90,000 Loyalty Points earned in the same year. Cap applies per cardmember regardless of number of Executive cards held. Does not count toward Million Miler status; not redeemable for travel/miles.

CREDITS
- Up to $120/year in Lyft credits: $10 credit after 3 Lyft rides paid with the card in a calendar month (max 1 credit/month). Digital wallet/virtual card payments don't qualify. Credits expire 30 days after issuance.
- Up to $120 Grubhub statement credit: up to $10 per monthly billing statement (grubhub.com, seamless.com, and their apps only; campus dining and gift cards excluded), no minimum spend.
- Up to $120/year Avis or Budget prepaid car rental statement credit: only for prepaid bookings made directly via Avis.com/Budget.com (third-party/travel-agency bookings excluded).
- Global Entry or TSA PreCheck application fee credit: one statement credit up to $120 every 4 years, for whichever program's application fee is charged first to the card.
- 25% savings (as statement credit) on inflight food/beverage purchases on American Airlines-operated domestic flights (not wifi); posts 8-10 weeks after transaction.
- All credits require enrollment/qualifying use as described above and post as statement credits, generally within 8-10 weeks.

NOTABLE PERKS (benefit_flags candidates)
- Admirals Club® full membership for primary cardmember (18+); Authorized Users get lounge access but not full membership privileges. Includes reciprocal oneworld/Alaska lounge access under stated conditions. Prorated refund available if primary cardholder already has 60+ days left on an existing paid Admirals Club membership at approval.
- Priority check-in, priority security screening, and priority boarding (Group 4) on American Airlines / American Eagle-operated flights, for primary cardmember and up to 8 companions on the same reservation.
- First checked bag free on domestic AA/American Eagle itineraries, for primary cardmember and up to 8 companions on the same reservation (excludes codeshares operated by other airlines).
- Travel protection benefits included (underwritten by Virginia Surety Company); details in Guide to Protection Benefits.
- No cap on AAdvantage miles earned.
