Citi® / AAdvantage® Platinum Select® World Elite Mastercard

FEES
Annual Fee: $99, waived for the first 12 months.
Foreign Transaction Fee: None

APPROVAL / ELIGIBILITY NOTES
- Must have an open AAdvantage® program membership; account can be closed if AAdvantage® membership is deactivated.
- Credit limits may be as low as $1,500.
- New-account AAdvantage bonus miles are NOT available if received a new account bonus for a Platinum Select account in the past 48 months, or converted another Citi card that earned a new account bonus in the last 48 months into a Platinum Select account.
- Authorized Users do not receive first checked bag free or boarding benefits.

EARN RATES
- Base: 1 AAdvantage mile per $1.
- 2X miles on eligible American Airlines purchases (billed by AA as merchant of record; excludes car rentals, hotel reservations, AAdvantage status boost/renewal, AA Cargo).
- 2X miles at restaurants (cafes, bars, lounges, fast food) and gas stations. Restaurant exclusions: supercenters, warehouse clubs, discount stores, restaurants inside department stores, bowling alleys, golf courses, country clubs, convenience stores, movie theaters, caterers, meal-kit delivery. Gas station exclusions: warehouse clubs not coding as gas stations, discount stores, department stores, convenience stores. (Admirals Club F&B purchases earn 2X under the AA-purchases rate instead.)
- Miles post 8-10 weeks after qualifying purchases; balance transfers, cash advances, fees, interest, money-order-like transactions do not earn miles. No mileage cap.

LOYALTY POINTS (status-qualifying, not redeemable)
- 1 Loyalty Point per eligible AAdvantage base mile (bonus/accelerator miles excluded).

CREDITS
- $125 American Airlines Flight Discount certificate after $20,000+ in card purchases within the cardmembership year (12 months from anniversary billing cycle); account must stay open one full cycle past anniversary; certificate expires 1 year after issuance; redeemable toward base airfare + fare-calculation taxes/fees on AA or AA-codeshare flights originating in US/PR/USVI; not usable for award-ticket taxes; non-refundable, no cash value; ~8-12 week delivery after qualifying.
- Special/limited-time offer: up to $180 back on Turo (Oct 19, 2025 - Oct 18, 2026): $30 statement credit per completed Turo trip (up to 6 trips), requires linking card to Turo account via turo.com/AAdvantagePlatinum; digital-wallet payments don't count.
- 25% savings (as statement credit) on inflight food/beverage on AA-operated domestic flights (not wifi); posts 8-10 weeks after transaction.

NOTABLE PERKS (benefit_flags candidates)
- Preferred boarding: boards after priority boarding, before rest of economy (Group 5); up to 4 companions on same reservation.
- First checked bag free on domestic AA/American Eagle itineraries, for primary cardmember and up to 4 companions on the same reservation.
