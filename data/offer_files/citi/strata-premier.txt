Fees
Annual Fee: $95.
Foreign Transaction: None.

Earn Rates
- 10 total points/$1 on hotel, car rental, and attraction bookings via the Citi Travel site (cititravel.com) — portal-only.
- 3 points/$1 on air travel and hotel purchases not booked through Citi Travel (travel agencies, airlines, hotels booked direct).
- 3 points/$1 at restaurants — excludes bakeries, caterers, and restaurants located inside another business (hotel, store, stadium, grocery store, warehouse club); depends on merchant's restaurant MCC.
- 3 points/$1 at supermarkets — excludes discount superstores, dairy stores, convenience stores, drugstores, warehouse/wholesale clubs, specialty food markets, bakeries, candy/nut stores, meal-kit delivery; depends on merchant's supermarket MCC.
- 3 points/$1 at gas stations and EV charging — excludes warehouse clubs, discount/department stores, convenience stores not coded as gas/EV.
- 1 point/$1 on all other purchases.
- Only purchases earn points (not balance transfers, cash advances, cash-like transactions, fees).

Credits
$100 Annual Hotel Benefit: once per calendar year, $100 off a single hotel stay of $500+ (excl. taxes/fees) booked via Citi Travel, prepaid in full with the card and/or points. Cannot combine with Citi Prestige's 4th-night-free benefit. Points not earned on the discounted portion.

The Reserve by Citi Travel (Strata Premier qualifies for Reserve-rate bookings): $100 experience credit (hotel-provided, varies by property), room upgrade (availability-based), daily breakfast for two, complimentary WiFi, early check-in/late check-out (availability-based) — only when booking a Reserve-rate room via Citi Travel.
Hotel Collection (broader access): daily breakfast for two, complimentary WiFi, early check-in/late check-out — only on Hotel Collection-rate bookings via Citi Travel.

Signup Bonus
No specific bonus amount stated in source. Bonus ThankYou points not available if you received a new account bonus for Citi Premier or Strata Premier in the past 48 months, or converted another Citi account with a bonus in the last 48 months into Premier/Strata Premier.

Notable Perks
Travel Protection Benefits (trip coverage) — secondary coverage only, provided by New Hampshire Insurance Company (AIG); details in Guide to Protection Benefits sent with card.

Approval
Some credit limits may be as low as $2,000.
