Product name: Bilt Obsidian Card (mid tier of "Bilt Card 2.0" three-card lineup)
Card network/tier: World Elite Mastercard
Issuer: Column N.A., Member FDIC, pursuant to license from Mastercard International Incorporated. Cardless Inc. is the servicer of the Bilt Cards.
Program: Bilt Rewards
Launch: Bilt Card 2.0 lineup launched Feb 7, 2026, replacing the single Wells Fargo-issued Bilt Mastercard.

Annual Fee
- $95
- $50 annual fee for authorized users

Signup / Welcome Offer
- "$200 of Bilt Cash when you apply and get approved" (Upgraded Points)
- Newsroom phrasing: "$200 in Bilt Cash on account opening"

Earn Rates
- 3x points on choice of dining OR groceries (grocery spend capped at $25,000/year, then drops to 1x) — Upgraded Points: "3x points plus 4% back in Bilt Cash on your choice of dining or grocery spending (up to $25,000 of purchases per year, then 1x earnings)"
- 2x points on travel spend, plus 4% back in Bilt Cash
- 1x points on all other everyday spend, plus 4% back in Bilt Cash
- Up to 1x points on rent and mortgage payments, no transaction fee (same mechanic as other tiers — see below)

Rent / Mortgage No-Fee Mechanic (central Bilt value prop, applies across all three cards)
- Rent and mortgage payments made through Bilt incur no transaction fee.
- Housing payments run through a separate credit line from everyday spend.
- Housing-payment point-earning (up to 1x) is unlocked by redeeming Bilt Cash: ratio of $3 Bilt Cash per 100 points (equivalently $30 per 1,000 points, per the support-article phrasing).

Bilt Cash
- Earned at 4% back on everyday spend.
- Expires annually Dec 31, with up to $100 allowed to roll into the next year.

Credits & Benefits
- $100/year in Bilt Travel hotel credits, distributed as $50 in each half of the year; per Upgraded Points, using each $50 credit "requires a minimum 2-night stay."
- Newsroom phrasing: "$100 in annual Bilt Travel Hotel credits"
- Trip Delay Insurance (per newsroom summary: "Trip Delay Insurance, no foreign transaction fees, and more")
- No foreign transaction fees
- World Elite Mastercard benefits
