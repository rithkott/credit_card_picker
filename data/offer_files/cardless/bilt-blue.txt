Bilt Blue Card (Cardless / Column N.A.) — official Bilt Rewards Card Offer Terms, last updated April 30, 2026
Source: biltrewards.com/terms (Bilt Rewards Card Offer Terms, covers all three Bilt Card tiers)

Fees
Annual Fee: $0.
No Authorized User fee (AU fee only applies to Obsidian/Palladium tiers).

Rewards Options (cardholder picks one, can change after an initial 7-day selection window, then locked for the Billing Cycle)
- Housing-only Option: earn Bilt Points automatically on Bilt Housing Payments (rent/mortgage, HOA dues, utilities, property taxes, etc. paid via Bilt App) at a variable 0X–1.25X multiplier based on your "Everyday Spend Ratio" (Everyday Spend ÷ Housing Payment that Billing Cycle): 25-49% ratio = 0.5X, 50-74% = 0.75X, 75-99% = 1X, 100%+ = 1.25X. Minimum floor of 250 points/cycle even below the 25% threshold. No Bilt Cash earned on everyday spend under this option. No cap on housing points.
- Flexible Bilt Cash Option: earn 4% back in Bilt Cash on all Everyday Spend (non-housing purchases); no points multiplier on housing. Redeem Bilt Cash to unlock points on housing payments: every $3 Bilt Cash unlocks 100 points, capped at 1 point per $1 of the payment.
- No transaction fee to earn rewards on housing payments under either option.
- Default option if none selected: Housing-only.

Earn Rates (Bilt Blue Card)
- Up to 1.25 points per $1 on Bilt Housing Payments (per Rewards Option above).
- 1 point per $1 on all other eligible purchases.
- Excluded from earning (not "Eligible Purchases"): balance transfers, cash advances, prepaid/gift cards, person-to-person payments (Venmo/PayPal/Cash App/Zelle), cryptocurrency, fees/interest, lottery/gaming/betting transactions.

Signup Bonus
$100 Bilt Cash, credited on or about Account Open Date (no spend requirement). Not available if you currently hold, previously held, or already received a Welcome Bonus for any Bilt Card 2.0 (Blue/Obsidian/Palladium).

Redemption
Points redeemable as a statement credit toward the card's principal balance (min. 1,000 points per redemption; max per redemption = principal balance or $100,000-equivalent, whichever is less). Statement credits do not count toward minimum payment due.

Partner earning (stacks with base 1X on top of partner-specific bonus points, all tiers): Lyft rideshare, Bilt Dining (participating restaurants), Bilt Travel Portal bookings, Bilt Fitness classes, Metropolis parking, Walgreens pharmacy purchases, Bilt Home Delivery. Rates vary by partner/merchant, set in the Bilt app.

Issuer: Column N.A., Member FDIC. Servicer: Cardless Inc. Network: Mastercard.
