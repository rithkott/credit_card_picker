Bilt Palladium Card (Cardless / Column N.A.) — official Bilt Rewards Card Offer Terms, last updated April 30, 2026
Source: biltrewards.com/terms (Bilt Rewards Card Offer Terms, covers all three Bilt Card tiers)

Fees
Annual Fee: $495.
Authorized User Fee: $95 per Authorized User added to the account.

Rewards Options (cardholder picks one, can change after an initial 7-day selection window, then locked for the Billing Cycle)
- Housing-only Option: earn Bilt Points automatically on Bilt Housing Payments (rent/mortgage, HOA dues, utilities, property taxes, etc. paid via Bilt App) at a variable 0X–1.25X multiplier based on your "Everyday Spend Ratio" (Everyday Spend ÷ Housing Payment that Billing Cycle): 25-49% ratio = 0.5X, 50-74% = 0.75X, 75-99% = 1X, 100%+ = 1.25X. Minimum floor of 250 points/cycle even below the 25% threshold. No Bilt Cash earned on everyday spend under this option. No cap on housing points.
- Flexible Bilt Cash Option: earn 4% back in Bilt Cash on all Everyday Spend (non-housing purchases); no points multiplier on housing. Redeem Bilt Cash to unlock points on housing payments: every $3 Bilt Cash unlocks 100 points, capped at 1 point per $1 of the payment.
- No transaction fee to earn rewards on housing payments under either option.
- Default option if none selected: Housing-only.

Earn Rates (Bilt Palladium Card)
- Up to 1.25 points per $1 on Bilt Housing Payments (per Rewards Option above).
- 2 points per $1 on all other eligible purchases (flat rate, no category restrictions).
- Excluded from earning: balance transfers, cash advances, prepaid/gift cards, person-to-person payments, cryptocurrency, fees/interest, lottery/gaming/betting transactions.

Signup Bonus
50,000 points + Bilt Gold Elite Status after $4,000 Everyday Spend within 90 days of Account Open Date (97 days for accounts opened 2/7/2026), PLUS $300 Bilt Cash credited immediately at account opening regardless of spend. Gold Elite Status lasts through Jan 15 of the second following year. Not available if you currently hold, previously held, or already received a Welcome Bonus for any Bilt Card 2.0 (Blue/Obsidian/Palladium).

Credits
- $200 Bilt Cash annually, auto-credited on Account Open Date and each Jan 1 thereafter while account is open/in good standing (no proration if not in good standing on the credit date).
- $400 Bilt Travel Hotel Credit: $200 per half-year (Jan-Jun / Jul-Dec) toward hotel bookings of 2+ nights made through the Bilt Travel Portal; must opt in at checkout; does not roll over between halves; forfeited if a non-refundable booking is canceled.

Redemption
Points redeemable as a statement credit toward the card's principal balance (min. 1,000 points per redemption; max per redemption = principal balance or $100,000-equivalent, whichever is less). Statement credits do not count toward minimum payment due.

Notable Perks
Priority Pass airport lounge access: complimentary membership for the cardholder plus up to two guests per visit ($35/guest for additional guests beyond two). Access to 1,200+ Priority Pass lounges across 400+ airports in 120+ countries, regardless of airline, frequent flyer status, or ticket class.

Partner earning (stacks with base rate, all tiers): Lyft rideshare, Bilt Dining (participating restaurants), Bilt Travel Portal bookings, Bilt Fitness classes, Metropolis parking, Walgreens pharmacy purchases, Bilt Home Delivery. Rates vary by partner/merchant, set in the Bilt app.

Issuer: Column N.A., Member FDIC. Servicer: Cardless Inc. Network: Mastercard (World Legend tier).
