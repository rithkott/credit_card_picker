Product name: Bilt Palladium Card (premium/top tier of "Bilt Card 2.0" three-card lineup)
Card network/tier: World Legend Mastercard (per Upgraded Points: "World Legend Mastercard benefits" — this is Mastercard's tier above World Elite, used for super-premium products)
Issuer: Column N.A., Member FDIC, pursuant to license from Mastercard International Incorporated. Cardless Inc. is the servicer of the Bilt Cards.
Program: Bilt Rewards
Launch: Bilt Card 2.0 lineup launched Feb 7, 2026, replacing the single Wells Fargo-issued Bilt Mastercard.

Annual Fee
- $495
- $95 annual fee for authorized users

Signup / Welcome Offer
- "Earn 50,000 points after spending $4,000 on non-housing expenses in the first 90 days + Gold elite status" (Upgraded Points)
- Plus "$300 Bilt Cash when you apply and get approved" (Upgraded Points)
- Newsroom phrasing: "50,000-point sign-up bonus + Gold Status (after qualifying spend)" and "$300 in Bilt Cash on account opening"

Earn Rates
- 2x points on everyday (non-housing) spend, plus 4% back in Bilt Cash — Upgraded Points: "Earn 2x points plus 4% back in Bilt Cash on everyday spending"
- Up to 1x points on rent and mortgage payments, no transaction fee (same mechanic as other tiers — see below)

Rent / Mortgage No-Fee Mechanic (central Bilt value prop, applies across all three cards)
- Rent and mortgage payments made through Bilt incur no transaction fee.
- Housing payments run through a separate credit line from everyday spend.
- Housing-payment point-earning (up to 1x) is unlocked by redeeming Bilt Cash: ratio of $3 Bilt Cash per 100 points. Example given by Upgraded Points: a $2,000 mortgage payment earning full 1x points requires $60 of Bilt Cash, in turn requiring roughly $1,500 of everyday card spend at the 4% Bilt Cash rate.

Bilt Cash
- Earned at 4% back on everyday spend.
- Expires annually Dec 31, with up to $100 allowed to roll into the next year.
- Additionally, Palladium deposits "$200 in Bilt Cash annually" at the start of each year (on top of the 4% earn rate and the $300 welcome-offer Bilt Cash) — per newsroom and Upgraded Points.

Credits & Benefits
- $400/year in Bilt Travel hotel credits, distributed as $200 in each half of the year (per Upgraded Points; newsroom phrasing: "$400 Bilt Travel Hotel credits").
- $200 in Bilt Cash deposited annually (see above; newsroom bundles this with the travel credit as "$600 in annual credits ($400 Bilt Travel Hotel credits + $200 in Bilt Cash)").
- Priority Pass lounge membership, including access for the cardholder plus up to 2 guests.
- World Legend Mastercard benefits (Mastercard's top-tier benefit suite).
- No foreign transaction fees.
- Purchase protection mentioned generically in newsroom summary ("Priority Pass access, purchase protection, no foreign transaction fees, authorized users and more") — specific coverage limits not found in fetched sources.
- Gold elite status in Bilt Rewards program (via welcome offer, after qualifying spend).
