Fidelity Rewards Visa Signature Card (Elan Financial Services)

Signup Bonus
- No standard welcome/signup bonus found on official Fidelity or Elan sources as of this writing. (Some third-party aggregators reference occasional targeted/promotional offers, e.g. a cited $150 bonus after spending $1,000 in 90 days, but this could not be confirmed on an official issuer page and appears to be a limited-time or channel-specific promotion rather than the standard, ongoing offer — omit unless independently verified at conversion time.)

Earn Rates
- 2 points per dollar on all eligible net purchases (net of returns/credits), no category restrictions and no cap mentioned.
- Points are worth 1 cent each when redeemed for deposit into an eligible Fidelity account, making the effective rate "unlimited 2% cash back" for that redemption path.
- Redemption value differs (lower) for other redemption options (e.g., travel, merchandise, gift cards, statement credit) — exact alternate redemption values not independently verified here; verify via fidelityrewards.com account portal or Guide to Benefits before citing specific alternate rates.

Annual Fee
- None ("no annual fee" stated explicitly on official Fidelity card page).

Foreign Transaction Fee
- None ("no foreign transaction fee" stated explicitly on official Fidelity card page).

Redemption Mechanics
- Points convert to cash and deposit into a designated eligible Fidelity account; no other redemption path is required to get the full 2% value.
- Minimum redemption threshold: 2,500 points before a redemption/conversion can occur.
- Auto-redemption available: if enrolled, accumulated points are automatically converted and deposited into the designated Fidelity account at the end of each month once the 2,500-point minimum is met.
- Cardholders may also accrue points and redeem on-demand rather than via auto-redemption.

Eligible Accounts for Point Deposit
- Most nonretirement Fidelity brokerage account registrations.
- Fidelity Cash Management Account.
- Retirement accounts: Traditional IRA, Roth IRA, Rollover IRA, SEP IRA.
- Fidelity Charitable Giving Account.
- Fidelity HSA (Health Savings Account).
- Fidelity-managed 529 College Savings Plan accounts.
- Fidelity Go accounts.

Administration / Eligibility Caveats
- Elan Financial Services is the creditor/issuer and reviews all credit applications; Fidelity is not involved in the credit qualification decision and cannot guarantee approval for any existing Fidelity customer.
- Card is issued under the Visa Signature brand, pursuant to a license from Visa U.S.A. Inc.
- Cardholders must have or open an eligible Fidelity account to direct point redemptions to; the card and the Fidelity brokerage/banking relationship are administered separately (Elan handles the credit card account; Fidelity holds the destination account).
