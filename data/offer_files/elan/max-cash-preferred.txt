Max Cash Preferred Card (Elan Financial Services)

Signup Bonus
- $150 bonus after meeting a minimum spend requirement in an initial period (commonly cited by third-party sources as ~$150; exact spend threshold and timeframe not confirmed on an official Elan/issuing-institution page found during research — NEEDS verification before relying on exact figures).

Earn Rates
- 5% cash back on the first $2,000 in combined eligible purchases per calendar quarter, across two categories chosen by the cardmember, from a list including: TV, internet & streaming services; fast food; cell phone providers; department stores; home utilities; furniture stores; electronics stores; recreation; entertainment. (Category list per official Elan cardmember benefits page for the Visa Signature Max Cash Preferred variant; exact list may vary slightly by distributing institution/card network variant, e.g. World Mastercard version.)
- 2% cash back, unlimited, on one additional everyday category chosen by the cardmember from: Grocery Stores + Grocery Delivery; Restaurants + Fast Food; or EV Charging & Gas Stations + Ground Transportation.
- 1% cash back, unlimited, on all other eligible purchases.
- Once the $2,000 quarterly combined cap in the two chosen 5% categories is exceeded, additional spend in those categories earns 1% (not 2% or 5%).
- Cardmember must actively enroll in their chosen categories (5% pair and 2% category) via the online Rewards Center; if a cardmember does not enroll, all purchases default to earning no more than 1% cash back.

Annual Fee
- $0 (no annual fee).

Foreign Transaction Fee
- Foreign transaction fee applies; sources vary slightly (around 2-3% depending on whether the transaction is in a foreign currency vs. billed in USD, and depending on distributing institution). Exact percentage not independently confirmed on the primary official Elan page found — NEEDS verification against the specific issuing institution's Schumer box/rates & fees table.

Redemption
- Cash rewards redeemed by logging into Account Access online, navigating to Rewards & Benefits, then "Redeem rewards at Rewards Center," or by calling the number on the back of the card.
- Account must be open and in good standing (not past due or over limit) on the billing cycle closing date to earn and redeem cash rewards.
- Cash rewards do not expire as long as the account remains active.

Distribution Notes
- This card is a co-branded/private-label product issued by Elan Financial Services and distributed white-label through numerous partner credit unions and community banks (e.g. Associated Bank, WCU, CorePlus, and many others). Core earn structure, fee structure, and enrollment mechanics appear consistent across distributing institutions based on Elan's own cardmember benefits and category-enrollment pages, but specific promotional signup bonuses, exact APRs, and some fee disclosures may vary by the specific credit union or bank offering the card. Confirm current terms against the specific distributing institution's Schumer box before treating fee/bonus figures as universal.
