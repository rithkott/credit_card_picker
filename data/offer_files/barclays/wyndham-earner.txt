Wyndham Rewards Earner Premier Card (Barclays)

Signup Bonus
- Up to 120,000 bonus points (up to 16 free nights at participating properties): 90,000 bonus points after spending $6,000 on purchases and paying the annual fee in full, both within the first 120 days, plus 30,000 bonus points after spending $750 at Hotels by Wyndham in the first 180 days.

Earn Rates
- 8 points/$1 on eligible purchases at Hotels by Wyndham, including Wyndham Travel Bundles.
- 4 points/$1 on eligible Vacation Club, travel, dining, and grocery store purchases (excludes Target and Walmart).
- 1 point/$1 on all other purchases (excludes Vacation Club down payments).

Credits & Benefits
- Anniversary Bonus: 30,000 bonus points each anniversary year after annual fee payment (up to 4 free nights at participating hotels).
- Automatic Wyndham Rewards DIAMOND member level: suite upgrades, welcome amenity at check-in at select hotels, and more.
- Cardmember Booking Discount on Standard Rate at participating Hotels by Wyndham.
- No limit on points earned.
- Redeem 25% fewer points when booking free nights.
- 1 free night (up to 30,000 points) after 5+ nights stayed in a calendar year.
- No foreign transaction fees.
- $0 Fraud Liability protection.
- $400 in annual benefits: $100 annual credit for eligible hotel purchases (Wyndham, Wyndham Grand, Dolce Hotels and Resorts, Trademark Collection, Registry Collection Hotels; $100+ aggregate spend/calendar year); $120 annual meal delivery credit (third-party delivery services, up to $10/month, $120/calendar year aggregate); $100 annual streaming credit (video/music/audio services, per calendar year); $65 annual wholesale statement credit (including membership fees, $65+ aggregate spend/calendar year).
- Complimentary Wyndham Rewards Insider subscription while account is open and in good standing.
- Global Entry or TSA PreCheck application fee statement credit, up to $120, resets every 4 years.

Interest Rates and Fees
- APR for purchases: 19.24%-29.99% variable, based on creditworthiness. Varies with Prime Rate. (0% promotional APR for 6 billing cycles on Wyndham Timeshare Purchases, then reverts to standard purchase APR.)
- APR for balance transfers: 0% introductory for first 15 billing cycles on transfers posted within 45 days of account opening; after that (and for transfers posted later), 19.24%-29.99% variable.
- APR for cash advances: 29.99% variable.
- Annual fee: $395.
- Balance transfer fee: $5 or 5% of transfer amount, whichever is greater.
- Cash advance fee: 5% (min. $10).
- Foreign transaction fee: 0%.
- Minimum monthly interest charge: $1.00.

Issued by Barclays Bank Delaware (Wyndham Rewards Earner Premier Card, Visa Signature).
