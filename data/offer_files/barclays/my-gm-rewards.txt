GM Rewards Mastercard (Barclays)

Signup Bonus
- 30,000 bonus points after spending $1,000 in the first 90 days of account opening.

Earn Rates
- Up to 10x points: 7x points/$1 on eligible GM purchases + up to 3x points for being a GM Rewards member (may earn less than 3x on some purchases).
- 3x points/$1 on all other purchases, including groceries, gas, restaurants, and travel.

Credits & Benefits
- Redemption: toward purchase/lease of a new GM vehicle, GM accessories, exclusive experiences, GM dealership service costs, or GM Financial account balance.
- Introductory 0% APR for 9 months on qualifying GM purchases greater than $499 made within the first 30 days of account opening; after that, 22.24%-32.24% variable.
- Promotional 0% APR for 6 months on qualifying GM purchases greater than $499 made after the first 30 days of account opening; after that, 22.24%-32.24% variable. (Qualifying GM Purchases: new/certified pre-owned vehicles or customer-paid Certified Service at a GM Dealership, GM Genuine/ACDelco parts, GM Accessories, SiriusXM, GM Energy, General Motors Company Store, General Motors Insurance, and OnStar transactions, as determined by merchant ID.)
- Digital wallet support (Apple Pay, Google Pay).
- Complimentary online access to FICO Credit Score.
- $0 Fraud Liability protection.
- Add authorized users to increase earnings (primary cardmember remains financially responsible for authorized user purchases).
- No annual fee.

Interest Rates and Fees
- APR for purchases: 22.24%-32.24% variable, based on creditworthiness. Varies with Prime Rate.
- APR for balance transfers: 22.24%-32.24% variable, based on creditworthiness. Varies with Prime Rate.
- APR for cash advances: 33.24% variable.
- Annual fee: $0.
- Balance transfer fee: $5 or 5% of transfer amount, whichever is greater.
- Cash advance fee: 5% (min. $10).
- Foreign transaction fee: 3%.
- Minimum monthly interest charge: $1.00.

Issued by Barclays Bank Delaware (GM Rewards Mastercard); General Motors LLC operates and administers the GM Rewards Program.
