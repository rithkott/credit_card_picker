Wyndham Rewards Earner Plus Card (Barclays)

Signup Bonus
- Up to 100,000 bonus points (up to 13 free nights at participating properties): 45,000 bonus points after spending $1,000 on purchases in the first 90 days, plus 55,000 bonus points after spending $500 at Hotels by Wyndham in the first 180 days. Free nights can require up to 30,000 points per bedroom at participating properties, subject to availability; resort fees may apply and cannot be paid with points.

Earn Rates
- 6 points/$1 on eligible purchases at Hotels by Wyndham, including Wyndham Travel Bundles.
- 4 points/$1 on eligible dining, grocery store (excludes Target and Walmart), and travel purchases.
- 1 point/$1 on all other purchases (excludes Vacation Club down payments).

Credits & Benefits
- Anniversary Bonus: 15,000 bonus points each anniversary year after annual fee payment (up to 2 free nights at participating hotels).
- Automatic Wyndham Rewards DIAMOND member level in first year; PLATINUM level after that (early check-in, late checkout, and more).
- Cardmember Booking Discount on Standard Rate at participating Hotels by Wyndham.
- No limit on points earned.
- Redeem 10% fewer points when booking free nights.
- 1 free night (up to 15,000 points) after 5+ nights stayed in a calendar year.
- No foreign transaction fees.
- $0 Fraud Liability protection.

Interest Rates and Fees
- APR for purchases: 19.24%-29.99% variable, based on creditworthiness. Varies with Prime Rate. (0% promotional APR for 6 billing cycles on Wyndham Timeshare Purchases, then reverts to standard purchase APR.)
- APR for balance transfers: 0% introductory for first 15 billing cycles on transfers posted within 45 days of account opening; after that (and for transfers posted later), 19.24%-29.99% variable.
- APR for cash advances: 29.99% variable.
- Annual fee: $95.
- Balance transfer fee: $5 or 5% of transfer amount, whichever is greater.
- Cash advance fee: 5% (min. $10).
- Foreign transaction fee: 0%.
- Minimum monthly interest charge: $1.00.

Issued by Barclays Bank Delaware (Wyndham Rewards Earner Plus Card, Visa Signature).
