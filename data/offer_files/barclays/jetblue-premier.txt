JetBlue Premier Card (Barclays)

Signup Bonus
- 100,000 bonus points after spending $5,000 on purchases and paying the annual fee in full, both within the first 90 days.

Earn Rates
- 6 points/$1 on eligible JetBlue purchases.
- 2 points/$1 at restaurants and eligible grocery stores.
- 1 point/$1 on all other purchases.

Credits & Benefits
- Complimentary access to BlueHouse for cardmembers with eligible fares.
- Priority Pass: access to 1,800+ participating lounges in 700+ airports across 146 countries.
- Up to $300/calendar year in statement credits on TrueBlue Travel purchases.
- Points toward Mosaic status with every purchase; 5,000 bonus points each year after account anniversary; annual 25-tile bonus after year-end to fast-track Mosaic status.
- 15% of points back after redeeming for and traveling on an Award Flight.
- Points never expire while account is open.
- Global Entry or TSA PreCheck application fee statement credit, up to $120 every 4 years.
- Group 3 boarding on JetBlue-operated flights.
- 50% savings on eligible inflight purchases on JetBlue-operated flights.
- Free first checked bag for cardmember and up to 3 companions on JetBlue-operated flights (codeshare/partner-operated flights not eligible for free bag, inflight savings, or Group 3 boarding).
- Points Payback: redeem points for a statement credit.
- Cash + Points: pay with almost any combination of dollars and TrueBlue points, starting as low as 500 points.
- No blackout dates: any seat, any time on JetBlue-operated flights (points required vary with published base fare at time of booking).
- No foreign transaction fees.
- $0 Fraud Liability protection.
- Companion Pass statement credits: up to $2,000/calendar year. Specifically, a one-time $500 credit after $15,000 in eligible net purchases in a calendar year, plus an additional one-time $1,500 credit after $75,000 in eligible net purchases (including the initial $15,000) in the same calendar year.

Interest Rates and Fees
- APR for purchases: 19.49%-29.49% variable, based on creditworthiness. Varies with Prime Rate.
- APR for balance transfers: 0% introductory for first 12 billing cycles on transfers posted within 45 days of account opening; after that (and for transfers posted later), 19.49%-29.49% variable.
- APR for cash advances: 29.74% variable.
- Annual fee: $499 ($150 for each authorized user).
- Balance transfer fee: $5 or 5% of transfer amount, whichever is greater.
- Cash advance fee: 5% (min. $10).
- Foreign transaction fee: 0%.
- Minimum monthly interest charge: $1.00.

Issued by Barclays Bank Delaware (JetBlue Premier World Elite Mastercard).
