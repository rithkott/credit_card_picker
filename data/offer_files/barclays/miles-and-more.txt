Miles & More World Elite Mastercard (Barclays)

Signup Bonus
- 50,000 miles after spending $3,000 in purchases and paying the annual fee in full, both within the first 90 days.

Earn Rates
- 2 miles/$1 on Miles & More partner airline tickets.
- 1 mile/$1 on all other purchases.

Credits & Benefits
- Two complimentary Lufthansa Business Lounge vouchers annually.
- Annual Companion Ticket after each account anniversary (taxes and fees apply).
- Redeemable for flight tickets across the Lufthansa Group and Star Alliance network.
- No foreign transaction fees.
- 0% introductory APR for 15 months on balance transfers made within 45 days of account opening; balance transfer fee applies.
- Achieve Miles & More status faster: once each calendar year, convert 5,000-25,000 miles earned on purchases into Points and Qualifying Points (e.g., 5,000 miles convert to 20 Points + 20 Qualifying Points).
- Chip technology for secure purchases at chip-card terminals in the US and abroad.
- Annual fee waived for Senator and HON Circle Members, as long as status is maintained.
- Personal Miles & More service card number required during application; enroll at the Miles & More website if not already a member.

Interest Rates and Fees
- APR for purchases: 19.49%-29.49% variable, based on creditworthiness. Varies with Prime Rate.
- APR for balance transfers: 0% introductory for first 15 billing cycles on transfers posted within 45 days of account opening; after that (and for transfers posted later), 19.49%-29.49% variable.
- APR for cash advances: 29.74% variable.
- Annual fee: $89 (waived for Senator/HON Circle Members).
- Balance transfer fee: $5 or 5% of transfer amount, whichever is greater.
- Cash advance fee: 5% (min. $10).
- Foreign transaction fee: 0%.
- Minimum monthly interest charge: $1.00.

Issued by Barclays Bank Delaware (Miles & More World Elite Mastercard).
