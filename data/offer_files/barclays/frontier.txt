Frontier Airlines World Mastercard (Barclays)

Signup Bonus
- 60,000 Travel Miles after spending $1,000 on purchases and paying the annual fee in full, both within the first 90 days.
- Instant Elite Gold Status after first purchase within the first 90 days of account opening; to maintain Elite Gold for 12 months from account opening, must also spend $3,000 in net purchases within the first 90 days (otherwise Elite Gold expires 90 days after account opening).

Earn Rates
- Up to 15x miles on eligible purchases at flyfrontier.com (10 miles for Frontier Miles membership + 5 miles for card usage), through Frontier Airlines channels (flyfrontier.com, reservations, onboard purchases, airport/city ticket counters), on net purchases.
- 3x miles on eligible restaurant purchases.
- 1x mile on all other purchases.

Credits & Benefits
- Two free checked bags on Frontier flights.
- Companion travel benefit: Elite Platinum/Diamond status members can bring a companion on unlimited eligible bookings for cost of government taxes and fees (from $5.60 one-way / $11.20 round trip).
- 1 Elite Status Point per $1 spent toward reaching Elite Status faster.
- Free seat upgrades with Elite Gold Status.
- $100 flight voucher every account anniversary after spending $2,500+ on purchases during the cardmembership year.
- Miles never expire while account is open and in good standing.
- No blackout dates on award travel.
- Award redemption fee waiver when booking with miles and paying taxes/fees with this card (from $5.60 one-way). Award travel bookable one-way with 5,000+ miles or round trip with 10,000+ miles.
- Priority boarding: Group 4.
- 0% introductory APR for 15 months on balance transfers made within 45 days of account opening; balance transfer fee applies.
- No foreign transaction fees.
- Complimentary online access to FICO Credit Score.

Interest Rates and Fees
- APR for purchases: 19.49%-29.49% variable, based on creditworthiness.
- APR for balance transfers: 0% introductory for first 15 billing cycles on transfers posted within 45 days of account opening; after that (and for transfers posted later), 19.49%-29.49% variable.
- APR for cash advances: 29.74% variable.
- Annual fee: $99.
- Balance transfer fee: $5 or 5% of transfer amount, whichever is greater.
- Cash advance fee: 5% (min. $10).
- Foreign transaction fee: 0%.
- Minimum monthly interest charge: $1.00.

Issued by Barclays Bank Delaware (Frontier Airlines World Mastercard).
