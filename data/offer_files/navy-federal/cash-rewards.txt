cashRewards Credit Card (Navy Federal Credit Union)

Membership Eligibility Requirement (IMPORTANT)
- Navy Federal Credit Union membership is required to hold this card. Membership is open to: Department of Defense and Coast Guard Active Duty, Veterans, and Retired personnel; DoD and Coast Guard civilian and contractor personnel; and family members of existing Navy Federal members (including household members). Not available to the general public without qualifying military/DoD/family affiliation.

Card Tier (determined by approved credit limit)
- Approved credit limit $5,000 or greater: issued as cashRewards Plus, earning unlimited 2% cash back on all purchases.
- Approved credit limit under $5,000: issued as standard cashRewards, earning unlimited 1.5% cash back on all purchases.
- (A separate cashRewards Secured card exists for building credit, earning 1% cash back on eligible purchases — not the card covered by this file.)

Signup Bonus
- $250 bonus cash after spending $2,500 in purchases within 90 days of account opening. Eligible purchases exclude fees, interest charges, balance transfers, gambling transactions, cash advances, and cash-equivalent transactions.

Annual Fee
- $0.

Foreign Transaction Fee
- $0.

Introductory APR
- 1.99% intro APR for 12 months on balance transfers made within 60 days of account opening (balance transfers do not earn cash back rewards).

Standard APR
- 14.15%–18.00% variable, based on creditworthiness; varies with the market based on the U.S. Prime Rate.

Cash Advance Fee
- None if performed at a Navy Federal branch or Navy Federal ATM. Otherwise $0.50 per domestic transaction or $1.00 per foreign transaction.

Redemption
- Cash back rewards do not expire as long as the account remains open; no cap on accrual. Redeem at member's convenience (no forced minimum/schedule found in official sources reviewed).

Other Notable Terms
- cashRewards Plus tier includes Visa Signature cardholder benefits; both tiers include standard Visa/Mastercard protections and a Zero Liability policy.
- 24/7 U.S.-based member support.

Gaps Not Found (flagged rather than estimated)
- Exact redemption mechanics/options (e.g., statement credit vs. direct deposit vs. gift cards) were not itemized on the pages reviewed — Navy Federal's dedicated "Earn & Redeem Credit Card Rewards" page was not directly fetchable during this research; only the no-expiration/no-cap terms were confirmed.
