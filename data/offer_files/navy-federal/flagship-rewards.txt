Visa Signature Flagship Rewards Card (Navy Federal Credit Union)

Membership Eligibility Requirement
- Navy Federal Credit Union membership is required to apply. Membership is open to active duty military, veterans/retirees, DoD officer candidates, DoD civilian employees/contractors assigned to DoD installations, and immediate family/household members of current Navy Federal members. Not open to the general public.

Signup Bonus
- 35,000 bonus points (valued at $350) after spending $3,500 in eligible purchases within 90 days of account opening. Allow up to 8 weeks after the 90-day spending period ends for points to post.
- Eligible purchases exclude fees, interest, balance transfers, gambling transactions, convenience checks, cash advances, and cash-equivalent items (e.g., gift cards).
- Also includes a complimentary Amazon Prime annual membership (valued at $139) as part of the offer.
- Limited to one promotional bonus per new account opening; account must remain open and in good standing when points are credited.
- Offer as advertised valid for applications 07/01/2026-01/03/2027; check current offer terms as this window will roll over.

Earn Rates
- 3 points/$1 on travel purchases (airlines, hotels, car rentals, cruises, campgrounds, ride-sharing, tolls, museums, golf courses, and other travel-related categories).
- 2 points/$1 on all other eligible purchases.
- No spending caps and points do not expire while the account remains open.

Annual Fee
- $49.

Foreign Transaction Fee
- None.

Other Fees
- Balance transfer fee: none.
- Cash advance fee: none at Navy Federal branches/ATMs; $0.50 per domestic transaction or $1.00 per foreign transaction elsewhere.

APR
- 14.74%-18.00% variable, based on creditworthiness (varies with the U.S. Prime Rate).

Credits & Benefits
- Global Entry/TSA PreCheck: up to $120 statement credit per eligible card, once every 4 years.
- GigSky: one complimentary 3GB global mobile data plan, valid 15 days.
- Travel accident insurance: automatic coverage for common-carrier fares charged to the card.
- Auto rental collision damage waiver: secondary coverage, rentals of 15 days or less.
- 24/7 emergency assistance: medical referral, lost luggage assistance, prescription assistance.
- Visa Signature benefits (concierge, luxury hotel collection, rental car privileges) apply per standard Visa Signature terms.
- Zero Liability policy; tap-to-pay capable.

Redemption
- Flexible redemption for travel, cash back, gift cards, or merchandise.

Gaps / Not Verified
- Did not find a published lounge-access benefit (e.g., Priority Pass) for this card on the official product page — appears this card does NOT include lounge access, but this is a negative finding (absence of a stated benefit) rather than a confirmed exclusion from an authoritative benefits guide; the linked PDF benefit guide (nfcu_824cc.pdf) was not directly parsed for this file, so double-check before asserting "no lounge access" definitively.
- Exact wording of the primary vs. secondary rental car coverage and full travel/accident insurance terms should be cross-checked against the official Guide to Benefits PDF before relying on it for claims-level detail.
