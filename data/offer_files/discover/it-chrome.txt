Will Discover give me a credit line increase?
Discover reviews customer accounts to see if they qualify for credit line increases periodically. Customers can also apply for a credit line increase. Whether you qualify to increase your credit line depends on a number of factors, including the age of your account, your responsible credit use, including accounts outside of Discover, how recently you received any prior credit line increases, and more.
Does Discover approve instantly?
In some cases, Discover may instantly approve your online credit card application depending on your credit score and other information. If you’re not approved instantly, you may need to provide documentation or speak with an agent to verify application information. We’ll be sure to let you know if we need more information from you.
