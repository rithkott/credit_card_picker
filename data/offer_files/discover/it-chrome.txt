Discover it® Chrome Credit Card (Gas & Restaurant)

FEES
- Annual fee: None
- Foreign transaction fee: None
- Cash advance fee: greater of $10 or 5% of the amount
- Late fee: up to $41 (first late payment fee waived)
- Balance transfer fee: 3% intro fee (within first 60 days account is open), then 5% for future transfers

APR (two figures, kept separate — do not blend)
- Live JSON-LD verified (authoritative, as of fetch date 2026-07-04): 28.24% flat ongoing APR
- Third-party sourced (WalletHub/NerdWallet/Bankrate/US News/CompareCards/Motley Fool, citing Discover's Schumer box, may be stale): purchase intro APR 0% for 6 months then 17.49%–26.49% variable ongoing; balance transfer intro APR 0% for 18 months then 17.49%–26.49% variable ongoing

EARN RATES
- 2% cash back at gas stations and restaurants (combined), on up to $1,000 in combined purchases per quarter — automatic, no activation needed
- 1% cash back on all other purchases, unlimited
- Cash back does not expire for the life of the account

SIGNUP BONUS
- Unlimited Cashback Match: Discover automatically matches all cash back earned at the end of the first year, dollar-for-dollar, no cap.

NOTABLE PERKS
- $0 fraud liability for unauthorized purchases
- Account freeze on/off switch via mobile app/website
- Free Social Security number dark-web alerts (opt-in)
- Free FICO Score access
- 24/7 human customer service
- Tap to pay (contactless)
- 99% nationwide merchant acceptance (Discover's own stat)
