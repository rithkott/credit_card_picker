HILTON HONORS AMERICAN EXPRESS CARD

FEES
Annual Fee: $0
Foreign Transaction Fee: None

EARN RATES (Hilton Honors points; base 3x)
7x points per $1 (3 base + 4 bonus) at Hilton portfolio properties — bookings must be made directly through a Hilton reservation channel; incidental charges qualify only if charged to the room and paid with this card at checkout.
5x points per $1 (3 base + 2 bonus) at US restaurants, US supermarkets, and US gas stations. Restaurants: excludes locations inside another establishment (hotel/casino/venue), nightclubs, convenience/grocery stores, and non-US locations of US chains. Supermarkets/gas: superstores, warehouse clubs, convenience stores, and meal-kit delivery only earn 3x (base rate), not the bonus.

SIGNUP BONUS
100,000 Hilton Honors Bonus Points + $100 statement credit after $2,000 spend in first 6 months. Both post 8-12 weeks after meeting spend. Points don't count toward elite tier qualification. Additional Card Members not eligible for this offer. Offer ends 07/29/2026 (public offer — check for renewal). Not eligible if you have/had this card or prior versions, or per general Amex bonus-eligibility history rules.

CREDITS
None.

NOTABLE PERKS
- Complimentary Hilton Honors Silver status for the Basic Card Member; lost if card is cancelled.
- Automatic upgrade to Hilton Honors Gold status if $20,000+ eligible purchases in a calendar year; Gold status valid through the following calendar year, then requalification needed.
- Amex Venue Collection Concessions Credit: 10% back, up to $250/calendar year, on stadium/arena concessions in the Amex Venue Collection (separate enrollment; ends 12/31/2026).
- Car Rental Loss/Damage Insurance, Extended Warranty, Purchase Protection — standard Amex secondary-coverage benefits underwritten by AIG/Amex Assurance.

APPROVAL NOTES
May not be eligible for welcome offer if you have/had this card or previous versions.
