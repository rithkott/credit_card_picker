Blue Cash Preferred® Card from American Express

FEES
Annual Fee: $0 intro for first year, then $95
Foreign Transaction Fee: 2.7% of each transaction after conversion to US dollars

EARN RATES
Base rate: 1% cash back on all other eligible purchases (Reward Dollars)
6% cash back on the first $6,000/year of purchases at U.S. supermarkets (superstores, convenience stores, warehouse clubs, and meal-kit delivery services NOT considered supermarkets), then 1%
6% cash back on eligible U.S. streaming subscriptions from select providers (current list at americanexpress.com/rewards-info). Bundled subscriptions or those billed by a third party (cable/telecom/internet provider, car manufacturer) may not be eligible.
3% cash back on transit (trains, taxis, rideshare, ferries, tolls, parking, buses, subways) — airfare, car rental, cruises NOT considered transit
3% cash back on gas station purchases in the U.S. (superstores, supermarkets, warehouse clubs selling gas NOT considered gas stations)
Redemption: statement credit or at Amazon.com checkout (Reward Dollars linked to Amazon account), no minimum redemption amount.

SIGNUP BONUS
As high as $300 cash back (Reward Dollars). Exact eligible amount disclosed after application/before card acceptance. Requires $3,000 in eligible purchases within first 6 months of card membership; Reward Dollars post 8-12 weeks after threshold met. Not eligible if you have or have had this Card, the Morgan Stanley Blue Cash Preferred® Card, or previous versions.

CREDITS
$120 Disney Streaming Credit — up to $10/month statement credit for subscription purchases at DisneyPlus.com, Hulu.com, or Stream.ESPN.com ($120/year max). Requires enrollment via Amex Benefit Dashboard. Only one $10 credit per month even for annual subscriptions. Third-party device/platform purchases and cable bundles excluded. Does not roll over (monthly forfeit).

American Express Venue Collection Concessions Credit — 10% back (up to $250/year) on food & beverage concessions purchases at participating stadiums/arenas in the Venue Collection. Requires separate offer enrollment. Third-party resellers/delivery excluded. Offer ends 12/31/2026.

NOTABLE PERKS
American Express Venue Collection: Card Member entrances/fast lanes at select stadiums/arenas.
Global Assist Hotline: travel/emergency coordination assistance (Card Member pays third-party service costs).
Lowest Hotel Rates Guarantee on amextravel.com bookings (excludes discounted/loyalty/opaque rates and certain packages).
Return Protection (underwritten by AMEX Assurance Company).
Car Rental Loss and Damage Insurance (secondary/supplemental, underwritten by AMEX Assurance Company).
Extended Warranty (underwritten by AMEX Assurance Company).
Purchase Protection (underwritten by AMEX Assurance Company).
