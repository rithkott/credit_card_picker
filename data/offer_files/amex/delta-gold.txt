Delta SkyMiles® Gold American Express Card

FEES
Annual Fee: $0 intro for first year, then $150
Foreign Transaction Fee: None

EARN RATES (SkyMiles)
Base rate: 1 mile per dollar on eligible purchases
2x miles at U.S. supermarkets (superstores, convenience stores, warehouse clubs, meal-kit delivery NOT considered supermarkets)
2x miles at restaurants worldwide (excludes restaurants within another establishment like a hotel/casino/venue, third-party food delivery outside the U.S., bars, nightclubs, cafeterias, convenience stores)
2x miles on Delta purchases where Delta is merchant of record (delta.com, Fly Delta app, phone, ticket counters — upgrades, miles, seat selection, Sky Club membership, in-flight food/beverage, Delta Vacations, Delta Stays bookings, Basic Economy fares). Excludes in-flight wireless internet, charitable contributions, Delta Gift Cards, deltashop.com, Delta Flight Museum.
Miles post to SkyMiles account 8-12 weeks after each month ends.

SIGNUP BONUS
Up to 90,000 bonus miles: 70,000 miles after $3,000 in eligible purchases within first 6 months; additional 20,000 miles (90,000 total) after $5,000 total eligible purchases within first 6 months. Miles post 8-12 weeks after each threshold is met. Limited-time offer, ends 07/15/2026 at 11:59pm MST. Not eligible if you have or have had this Card, Delta SkyMiles® Platinum, or Reserve Amex cards, or previous versions.

CREDITS
$200 Delta Flight Credit — annual, if $10,000+ in eligible purchases in a calendar year, issued as a Delta eCredit toward a Delta-marketed flight (delta.com or Delta Reservations) or Delta Vacations airfare portion. Not valid for bag/change/upgrade fees. Expires 1 year from issuance. Limit one per calendar year. Posts 6-8 weeks after threshold met.

$120 Rideshare Credit — up to $10/month statement credit on U.S. purchases with Uber, Lyft, Curb, Alto (subject to change). Requires enrollment, available starting 12 months after account opening (immediate if transferring from Platinum/Reserve). Excludes delivery services (food/package/grocery/pet/retail), bikeshare, scooter, car rental, transit, memberships. Does not roll over (monthly forfeit).

$100 Delta Stays Credit — up to $100/year statement credit for prepaid hotel/vacation rental bookings on Delta Stays platform (delta.com/stays). Excludes cancellation/property/resort fees. No enrollment mentioned.

20% Back on In-Flight Purchases — statement credit on pre-purchased meals and in-flight food/beverage on Delta-operated flights (not wireless internet/charitable contributions). Automatic when charged to card.

NOTABLE PERKS
First Checked Bag Free on Delta/Delta Connection flights (Basic Card Member + up to 9 companions on reservation; codeshares excluded; not additive to existing free-bag benefits).
Zone 5 Priority Boarding on Delta/Delta Connection flights (not Delta Shuttle).
TakeOff 15: at least 15% off the mileage portion of Award Tickets on Delta/Delta Connection flights booked at delta.com/FlyDelta app; not combinable with Pay with Miles or other discounts.
Pay with Miles: redeem SkyMiles (5,000-mile increments, ~$50 value per 5,000 miles) toward Delta/Delta Connection ticketed flights.
Medallion Complimentary Upgrades: subject to availability.
No Delta blackout dates on Delta-operated flights (partner flights may have blackouts).
American Express Venue Collection: 10% back (up to $250/year) on stadium/arena concessions; entrances/fast lanes at select venues. Requires enrollment.
Global Assist Hotline; Lowest Hotel Rates Guarantee on amextravel.com bookings.
Baggage Insurance Plan, Car Rental Loss and Damage Insurance, Extended Warranty, Purchase Protection (underwritten by AMEX Assurance Company).
