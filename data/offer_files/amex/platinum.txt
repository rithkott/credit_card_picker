THE PLATINUM CARD FROM AMERICAN EXPRESS

FEES
Annual Fee: $895. Additional Platinum Card: $195/each. Companion Platinum Cards: no annual fee.
Foreign Transaction Fee: None.

EARN RATES
- Base: 1 point/$.
- 5 points/$ (4 additional) on: scheduled-flight airfare (up to $500,000/year) booked directly with airlines or via Amex Travel; prepaid hotel reservations via Amex Travel; prepaid vacation rentals via AmexTravel.com. Excludes charter/private jet flights, tour/cruise packages, ticketing fees, hotel group bookings.
- 2 points/$ (1 additional) on cruise bookings made via AmexTravel.com or by phone (Platinum/Business Platinum only get this extra point on cruises specifically, since air/hotel already earn the 5x above).
- Points post to Membership Rewards account 10-12 weeks after payment.

CREDITS (each requires Amex enrollment where noted; standard mechanics: post as statement credit, ~8 weeks after charge, forfeited if account cancelled/past due, reversed if purchase returned)
- $120 Uber One Credit: up to $120/year on Uber One membership fees (monthly or annual), Card must be set as payment method with Uber Cash toggled off.
- $200 Airline Fee Credit: up to $200/year on incidental fees (bags, seat select, etc., NOT tickets/upgrades/miles/gift cards) charged by one airline you select annually (Alaska, American, Delta, Hawaiian, JetBlue, Spirit, Southwest, United).
- $200 Oura Ring Credit: up to $200/year, Oura Ring purchases only at ouraring.com; excludes memberships, accessories, gift cards.
- $200 Uber Cash: $15/month + $20 in December, auto-loaded to Uber Cash balance (must add Card to Uber app, toggle Uber Cash on); expires monthly, does not roll over. (Uber VIP replaced by "Signature Support for Amex" phone support as of 5/7/2026.)
- $209 CLEAR+ Credit: up to $209/year on CLEAR+ membership.
- $300 Digital Entertainment Credit: up to $25/month ($300/year) on Disney+, ESPN, Hulu, NYT, Paramount+, Peacock, WSJ, or YouTube subscriptions, direct-purchased only (not bundled/third-party/gift cards).
- $300 Equinox Credit: up to $300/year on Equinox membership (incl. Equinox+) or standalone Equinox+ subscription; requires eligibility validation at platinum.equinox.com.
- $300 lululemon Credit: up to $75/quarter ($300/year), lululemon stores/site/app only; excludes Studio, Like New, outlet, gift cards.
- $400 Resy Credit: up to $100/quarter ($400/year) on purchases at U.S. restaurants via Resy.com/app or Resy Pay.
- $600 Hotel Credit: up to $300 semiannually ($600/year) on prepaid Fine Hotels+Resorts / The Hotel Collection bookings made via Amex Travel; Delta SkyMiles Platinum not eligible.
- SoulCycle At-Home Bike Credit: $300 statement credit per bike purchase (up to 15/year) at equinoxplus.com/bike; requires 12-month Equinox+ membership; online only.
- Walmart+ Monthly Credit: covers one Walmart+ monthly membership fee (~$12.95) per month; annual Walmart+ plans don't qualify (must switch to monthly).
- Global Entry/TSA PreCheck fee credit: one statement credit every 4 years, up to $120 (Global Entry, includes TSA PreCheck access) or $85 (TSA PreCheck alone).

Realistic capture: this card's credits are numerous but each requires a specific merchant/enrollment; total sticker credit value is large but real capture depends on actually using Uber One, Equinox, Resy, lululemon, Walmart+, CLEAR, digital subscriptions, Oura, and airline/hotel travel via Amex Travel — most cardholders capture only a subset.

SIGNUP BONUS
Up to 175,000 Membership Rewards points (exact amount disclosed at application) after $12,000 spend in first 6 months. Not eligible if you have/had this Card, the Schwab Platinum, or the Morgan Stanley Platinum (or prior versions). Points post 8-12 weeks after threshold met.

NOTABLE PERKS
- The Centurion Lounge: unlimited complimentary access (arrive within 3 hrs of departure, 5 hrs during layover as of 7/8/2026); 2 complimentary guests/visit after $75,000/year spend, otherwise $50/guest ($30 for kids 2-17).
- Sidecar by The Centurion Lounge: same access model/guest fee structure as Centurion Lounge.
- Escape Lounges, Plaza Premium Lounges: complimentary access, 2 guests.
- Delta Sky Club: 10 visits/year (issued annually); unlimited access after $75,000/year spend on the Card.
- Lufthansa Lounge access: only through 9/30/2026, then removed.
- Priority Pass Select: enrollment required, 2 free guests/visit then guest fees.
- Global Lounge Collection additional partner lounges: complimentary.
- Fine Hotels + Resorts / The Hotel Collection: noon check-in, room upgrade, ~$100 property credit, breakfast for two, subject to availability, when booked via Amex Travel.
- Complimentary Hilton Honors Gold status and Marriott Bonvoy Gold Elite status (enrollment required).
- Car rental elite status: Avis, Hertz (President's Circle, 4-hr grace period, up to 20% off base rates), National Emerald Club (free day rentals) — enrollment required.
- Cruise Privileges Program: $100-$300 onboard credit per stateroom (min. 5 nights, booked via Amex Travel).
- Global Dining Access by Resy, Platinum Nights by Resy: reservation access at select restaurants.
- Cell phone protection, trip cancellation/interruption insurance, trip delay insurance, return protection (secondary coverage on insurance benefits).
- Premium Global Assist Hotline (incl. emergency medical transport assistance if approved).
- Concierge service.
- No foreign transaction fees; no preset spending limit.
