Delta SkyMiles® Blue American Express Card

FEES
Annual Fee: $0
Foreign Transaction Fee: None

EARN RATES (SkyMiles)
Base rate: 1 mile per dollar on eligible purchases
2x miles at restaurants worldwide (excludes restaurants located within another establishment like a hotel/casino/venue, and third-party food delivery outside the U.S.; excludes bars, nightclubs, cafeterias, convenience stores)
2x miles on Delta purchases where Delta is merchant of record (delta.com, Fly Delta app, phone, ticket counters — upgrades, miles, seat selection, Sky Club membership, in-flight food/beverage). Excludes in-flight wireless internet, charitable contributions, Delta Gift Cards, deltashop.com, Delta Flight Museum. Includes Delta Vacations packages, Delta Stays bookings, and Basic Economy fares (though those don't earn SkyMiles otherwise).
Miles post to SkyMiles account 8-12 weeks after each month ends.

SIGNUP BONUS
10,000 bonus miles after $1,000 in eligible purchases within first 6 months. Miles post 8-12 weeks after threshold met. Not eligible if you have or have had this Card, Delta SkyMiles® Options, Gold, Platinum, or Reserve Amex cards, or previous versions.

CREDITS
20% Back on In-Flight Purchases — statement credit on pre-purchased meals and in-flight food/beverage on Delta-operated flights (not wireless internet or charitable contributions). No enrollment mentioned; automatic when charged to card. Credits typically post 6-8 weeks after purchase.

NOTABLE PERKS
Pay with Miles: redeem SkyMiles (5,000-mile increments, ~$50 value per 5,000 miles) toward Delta/Delta Connection ticketed flights booked at delta.com.
Medallion Complimentary Upgrades: subject to availability.
No Delta blackout dates on Delta-operated flights (partner flights may have blackouts).
American Express Venue Collection: 10% back (up to $250/year) on stadium/arena concessions; entrances/fast lanes at select venues. Requires enrollment.
Global Assist Hotline: travel/emergency coordination assistance (Card Member pays third-party service costs).
Lowest Hotel Rates Guarantee on amextravel.com bookings.
Car Rental Loss and Damage Insurance, Extended Warranty, Purchase Protection (underwritten by AMEX Assurance Company).
