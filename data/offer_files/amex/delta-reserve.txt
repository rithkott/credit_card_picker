DELTA SKYMILES RESERVE AMERICAN EXPRESS CARD

FEES
Annual Fee: $650 (Additional Card: $175)
Foreign Transaction Fee: None

EARN RATES
1x mile per $1 on eligible purchases (base rate)
3x miles per $1 (2 additional) on Delta purchases (delta.com, Fly Delta app, phone reservations, ticket counters — Delta as merchant of record; covers upgrades, miles, seat selection, Sky Club membership, in-flight food/beverage). Excludes in-flight purchases where Delta isn't merchant of record (wifi, charitable donations), Delta Gift Cards, deltashop.com, Delta Flight Museum. Includes Delta Vacations packages and Delta Stays bookings.
MQD Boost: 1 MQD per $10 of eligible purchases (elite-qualifying dollars, not redeemable miles).

SIGNUP BONUS
Earn up to 125,000 bonus miles: 100,000 miles after $6,000 spend in first 6 months; additional 25,000 miles after additional $3,000 spend (total $9,000) in first 6 months. Miles post 8-12 weeks after meeting each threshold. Not eligible if you have/had this card or prior versions (or per general Amex history-based eligibility rules). Offer ends 07/15/2026 (public offer — check for renewal).

CREDITS (require enrollment via americanexpress.com; typically post as statement credit 6-8 weeks after purchase; forfeited if unused in period; general Amex mechanics: relies on merchant transaction coding/date, may not post if made via third-party processor/reseller)
- $240 Resy Credit: up to $20/month statement credit on eligible Resy-bookable US restaurant/food & beverage purchases (direct at qualifying restaurant, Resy.com/app, or Resy Pay). Excludes Resy software purchases, Resy-branded gift cards, gift cards, delivery/catering, third-party-processed on-site event sales. Does not roll over.
- 20% Back on In-Flight Purchases: 20% statement credit on pre-purchased meals and in-flight food/beverage on Delta-operated flights (not wifi or Delta Sync portal, not charitable donations).
- $120 Rideshare Credit: up to $10/month statement credit on US Uber, Lyft, Curb, Alto purchases. Excludes delivery, bikeshare/scooter, car rental, transit, memberships/subscriptions.
- $200 Delta Stays Credit: up to $200/calendar year statement credit on new Delta Stays prepaid hotel/vacation rental bookings (delta.com/stays). Excludes cancellation/property/resort fees. Delta Vacations bookings don't qualify.
- Amex Venue Collection Concessions Credit: 10% back, up to $250/calendar year, on food & beverage concessions at participating stadiums/arenas in the Amex Venue Collection. Requires separate enrollment; excludes third-party resellers/delivery. Offer ends 12/31/2026.
- Global Entry/TSA PreCheck Credit: one statement credit every 4 years, up to $120 (Global Entry) or $85 (TSA PreCheck), for the application fee.
