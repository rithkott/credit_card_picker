AMERICAN EXPRESS GOLD CARD

FEES
Annual Fee: $325 (up to 5 Additional Cards free; $35/card for 6th and beyond)
Foreign Transaction Fee: None

EARN RATES (Membership Rewards points; base 1x)
5x points per $1 on prepaid hotels booked through American Express Travel
4x points per $1 on restaurants worldwide, capped at $50,000/calendar year eligible spend, then 1x. Excludes restaurants within another establishment (hotel/casino/venue), events/event tickets, non-US third-party delivery, bars/nightclubs/cafeterias/caterers/breweries/bakeries.
4x points per $1 on US supermarkets, capped at $25,000/calendar year eligible spend, then 1x. Excludes superstores, convenience stores, warehouse clubs, meal-kit delivery services.
3x points per $1 on scheduled-airline airfare booked directly or via Amex Travel/app (excludes charter/private jet).
2x points per $1 on prepaid car rentals and cruise reservations via AmexTravel.com/app (excludes non-prepaid car rentals/hotels, ticketing fees).
2x points per $1 on Amex Travel portal purchases generally (1 base + 1 bonus point on travel booked at AmexTravel.com/app); doesn't stack with category bonuses above; Platinum/Biz Platinum cardholders only get the extra point on cruises.

SIGNUP BONUS
Up to 100,000 Membership Rewards points after $8,000 spend in first 6 months (exact offer amount disclosed at approval, may be less than 100k). Points post 8-12 weeks after meeting spend. Not eligible for welcome offer if you have/had this card, Premier Rewards Gold, Platinum, Platinum Schwab, or Platinum Morgan Stanley cards, or per general Amex bonus-eligibility history rules (balance transfer history, cards opened/closed, etc.).

CREDITS (enrollment required via americanexpress.com; not available to Classic/Traditional/Senior Gold; general mechanics: statement credit posts ~8 weeks after purchase, doesn't roll over, relies on merchant transaction coding, excludes third-party processors/delivery apps/gift cards unless noted)
- $100 Resy Credit: up to $50 semiannual statement credit (Jan-Jun, Jul-Dec) on eligible Resy-bookable US restaurant/food & beverage purchases. Excludes Resy software, Resy gift cards, delivery/catering, third-party-processed on-site event sales.
- $120 Dining Credit: up to $10/month statement credit at Grubhub (incl. Seamless), Buffalo Wild Wings, Five Guys, The Cheesecake Factory, Wonder; Goldbelly and Wine.com removed as partners effective 7/1/2026.
- $120 Uber Cash: $10/month in Uber Cash (US only), auto-applied via Uber app with card added as payment method; expires monthly, does not roll over.
- $84 Dunkin' Credit: up to $7/month statement credit at US Dunkin' locations. Excludes gift cards, delivery apps, Dunkin' products bought at other retailers.

NOTABLE PERKS
- Hertz Five Star status (requires separate enrollment + Hertz Gold+ account).
- The Hotel Collection: $100 property credit + noon check-in/late checkout/room upgrade (subject to availability) on 2+ night bookings via Amex Travel; not available to Delta SkyMiles Gold/Platinum cardholders.
- Amex Venue Collection Concessions Credit: 10% back, up to $250/calendar year, on stadium/arena concessions purchases in the Amex Venue Collection (separate enrollment; ends 12/31/2026).
- No Preset Spending Limit; Baggage Insurance, Car Rental Loss/Damage Insurance, Extended Warranty, Purchase Protection, Trip Delay Insurance — standard Amex secondary-coverage benefits underwritten by AIG/Amex Assurance.

APPROVAL NOTES
