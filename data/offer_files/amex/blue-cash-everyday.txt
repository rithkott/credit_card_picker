Blue Cash Everyday® Card from American Express

FEES
Annual Fee: $0
Foreign Transaction Fee: 2.7% of each transaction after conversion to US dollars

EARN RATES
Base rate: 1% cash back on all other eligible purchases (Reward Dollars)
3% cash back on the first $6,000/year of purchases at U.S. supermarkets (superstores, convenience stores, warehouse clubs, and meal-kit delivery services are NOT considered supermarkets), then 1%
3% cash back on the first $6,000/year of U.S. online retail purchases, then 1%. Must be a website/app purchase from a U.S. retail merchant selling physical goods directly to consumers, paid online (not phone/mail, not in-store pickup, not "buy now pay later"); restaurants, supermarkets, gas stations, automotive dealers, travel/entertainment/services excluded.
3% cash back on the first $6,000/year of gas station purchases in the U.S. (superstores, supermarkets, and warehouse clubs that sell gasoline are NOT considered gas stations), then 1%
Redemption: statement credit or at Amazon.com checkout (Reward Dollars linked to Amazon account), no minimum redemption amount.

SIGNUP BONUS
As high as $200 cash back (Reward Dollars). Exact eligible amount disclosed after application/before card acceptance. Requires $2,000 in eligible purchases within first 6 months of card membership; Reward Dollars post 8-12 weeks after threshold met. Not eligible if you have or have had this Card, the Cash Magnet® Card, the Blue Cash Preferred® Card, the Morgan Stanley Blue Cash Preferred® Card, or previous versions.

CREDITS
$180 Home Chef Credit — up to $15/month statement credit for Home Chef meal kit subscription purchased online at homechef.com/blue-cash-everyday ($180/year max). Requires enrollment via Amex Benefit Dashboard. Kitchenware, individual meal kits, and gift cards not eligible. Does not roll over (monthly forfeit).

$84 Disney Streaming Credit — up to $7/month statement credit on subscription purchases at DisneyPlus.com, Hulu.com, or Stream.ESPN.com ($84/year max). Requires enrollment. Only one $7 credit per month even if paying annual subscription. Third-party device/platform purchases and bundles with cable service excluded. Does not roll over.

American Express Venue Collection Concessions Credit — 10% back (up to $250/year) on food & beverage concessions purchases at participating stadiums/arenas in the Venue Collection. Requires separate offer enrollment. Third-party resellers/delivery excluded. Offer ends 12/31/2026.

NOTABLE PERKS
American Express Venue Collection: Card Member entrances/fast lanes at select stadiums/arenas (availability varies by venue/event).
Global Assist Hotline: travel/emergency coordination assistance (Card Member pays third-party service costs).
Lowest Hotel Rates Guarantee on amextravel.com bookings (excludes discounted/loyalty/opaque rates and certain packages).
Car Rental Loss and Damage Insurance (secondary/supplemental, underwritten by AMEX Assurance Company).
Purchase Protection (underwritten by AMEX Assurance Company).
