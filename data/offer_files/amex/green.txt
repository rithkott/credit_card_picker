AMERICAN EXPRESS GREEN CARD

FEES

Foreign Transaction Fee: None

EARN RATES (Membership Rewards points; base 1x)
3x points per $1 on travel (airfare, hotels, cruises, tours, car rentals, campgrounds, vacation rentals, third-party travel sites, Amex Travel/app). Excludes timeshare purchases.
3x points per $1 on transit (trains, taxis, rideshare, ferries, tolls, parking, buses, subways).
3x points per $1 at restaurants. Excludes restaurants within another establishment (hotel/casino/venue), non-US third-party delivery, bars/nightclubs/cafeterias/convenience stores.

SIGNUP BONUS
40,000 Membership Rewards points after $3,000 spend in first 6 months. Points post 8-12 weeks after meeting spend. Not eligible for welcome offer if you have/had this card, Platinum, Platinum Schwab, Platinum Morgan Stanley, or Gold cards, or per general Amex bonus-eligibility history rules (balance transfer history, cards opened/closed, etc.).

CREDITS
- $219 CLEAR+ Credit: up to $219/calendar year statement credit for an annual CLEAR+ Membership. Requires separate CLEAR enrollment; posts ~8 weeks after charge; doesn't roll over.

NOTABLE PERKS
- Amex Venue Collection Concessions Credit: 10% back, up to $250/calendar year, on stadium/arena concessions purchases in the Amex Venue Collection (separate enrollment; ends 12/31/2026).
- No Preset Spending Limit; Trip Delay Insurance (secondary coverage via AIG/Amex Assurance).
