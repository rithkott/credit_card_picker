MARRIOTT BONVOY BEVY AMERICAN EXPRESS CARD

FEES
Annual Fee: $250.
Foreign Transaction Fee: None.

EARN RATES
- Base: 2 points/$.
- 6 points/$ (4 additional) at Marriott Bonvoy properties (charged directly with participating properties, standalone Marriott-branded retail, or Marriott-branded online stores/gift cards).
- 4 points/$ (2 additional) at restaurants worldwide and U.S. supermarkets (excludes superstores, convenience stores, warehouse clubs, meal-kit delivery, nightclubs; restaurants located within another establishment like a hotel/casino may not qualify), on the first $15,000/year combined spend in these categories; reverts to 2 points/$ above that cap.
- Marriott Bonvoy Gold Elite status (see below) earns 25% more points at participating Marriott hotels (on top of member base earn of up to 10 pts/$ from Marriott itself).
- Points post to Marriott Bonvoy account up to 12 weeks after month end.

CREDITS
- Amex Venue Collection Concessions: 10% back (up to $250/year) on food & beverage purchases at participating stadiums/arenas in the Amex Venue Collection, enrollment required. Not valid via resellers/delivery apps. Offer ends 12/31/2026.

SIGNUP BONUS
Up to 135,000 Marriott Bonvoy points: 85,000 after $5,000 spend in first 6 months, plus additional 50,000 after a further $2,000 spend (total $7,000) in first 6 months. Not eligible if you have/had this Card or the Marriott Bonvoy Brilliant Card (or prior versions); also excluded if holding/recently held/received bonus for several Chase-issued Marriott/Ritz-Carlton cards (Bountiful, Boundless, Rewards Premier Plus, Premier, Bonvoy, Rewards, Bold, and business variants) within stated lookback windows (30/90/24-month, per specific card). Points post 8-12 weeks after each threshold.

NOTABLE PERKS
- 15 Elite Night Credits deposited within 60 days of account opening (only one Marriott card's Annual ENC benefit counted per member per year across issuers, highest-ENC card wins; separate benefit if you also hold a Marriott business card).
- Complimentary Marriott Bonvoy Gold Elite status (~12 weeks to apply).
- Free Night Award after $15,000 in eligible spend in a calendar year, redeemable for one night at a property with redemption level ≤50,000 points (can add up to 25,000 purchased points to expand value); limit one per year; expires 1 year from issuance.
- 1,000 bonus points per eligible paid stay (direct-booked, Qualifying Rate) at participating Marriott properties.
- Premium on-property internet access at participating hotels (one room per stay).
- Points transfer to airlines with a 5,000-mile bonus per 60,000 points transferred.
- Trip Delay Insurance (secondary coverage).
- No foreign transaction fees.
