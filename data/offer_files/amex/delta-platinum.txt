Delta SkyMiles® Platinum American Express Card

FEES
Annual Fee: $350
Foreign Transaction Fee: None

EARN RATES (SkyMiles)
Base rate: 1 mile per dollar on eligible purchases
2x miles at U.S. supermarkets (superstores, convenience stores, warehouse clubs, meal-kit delivery NOT considered supermarkets)
2x miles at restaurants worldwide (excludes restaurants within another establishment like a hotel/casino/venue, third-party food delivery outside the U.S., bars, nightclubs, cafeterias, convenience stores)
3x miles at hotels worldwide when paid directly with the hotel (not for prepaid third-party/OTA bookings or vacation packages; incidental charges count if billed to room and paid at checkout)
3x miles on Delta purchases where Delta is merchant of record (delta.com, Fly Delta app, phone, ticket counters — upgrades, miles, seat selection, Sky Club membership, in-flight food/beverage, Delta Vacations, Delta Stays bookings, Basic Economy fares). Excludes in-flight wireless internet, charitable contributions, Delta Gift Cards, deltashop.com, Delta Flight Museum.
Miles post to SkyMiles account 8-12 weeks after each month ends.

SIGNUP BONUS
Up to 100,000 bonus miles: 80,000 miles after $4,000 in eligible purchases within first 6 months; additional 20,000 miles (100,000 total) after $6,000 total eligible purchases within first 6 months. Miles post 8-12 weeks after each threshold is met. Limited-time offer, ends 07/15/2026 at 11:59pm MST. Not eligible if you have or have had this Card, Delta SkyMiles® Reserve, or previous versions.

CREDITS
$120 Resy Credit — up to $10/month statement credit for purchases at Resy-partnered U.S. restaurants/food & beverage establishments (direct purchase, Resy.com/app, or Resy Pay). Requires enrollment. Excludes Resy software, Resy-branded gift cards, delivery/catering orders, and third-party-processed transactions. Does not roll over (monthly forfeit).

$120 Rideshare Credit — up to $10/month statement credit on U.S. purchases with Uber, Lyft, Curb, Alto. Requires enrollment. Excludes delivery services, bikeshare, scooter, car rental, transit, memberships. Does not roll over (monthly forfeit).

$150 Delta Stays Credit — up to $150/year statement credit for prepaid hotel/vacation rental bookings on Delta Stays platform (delta.com/stays). Excludes cancellation/property/resort fees.

20% Back on In-Flight Purchases — statement credit on pre-purchased meals and in-flight food/beverage on Delta-operated flights. Automatic when charged to card.

Fee Credit for Global Entry or TSA PreCheck — one statement credit every 4 years, up to $120 (Global Entry) or $85 (TSA PreCheck), for whichever program applied to first.

Delta Platinum Companion Certificate — one round-trip Delta Main companion ticket per year after card renewal, for travel to a defined list of U.S./Latin America/Caribbean destinations on Delta/Delta Connection/Delta Shuttle flights only (excludes codeshares and Basic Economy). Requires paying government-imposed taxes/fees (up to $80 domestic, up to $250 international round-trip). Void if card is cancelled/downgraded within 60 days of the annual fee billing.
