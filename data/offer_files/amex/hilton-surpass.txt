HILTON HONORS AMERICAN EXPRESS SURPASS CARD

FEES
Annual Fee: $0 intro for first year, then $150.
Foreign Transaction Fee: None.

EARN RATES
- Base: 3 points/$ on eligible purchases.
- 12 points/$ (9 additional) at Hilton portfolio properties — bookings and incidental charges. Booking must be made directly through a Hilton reservation channel; incidental charges must be charged to the room and paid with this Card at checkout. Third-party/OTA bookings do not qualify.
- 6 points/$ (3 additional) at U.S. restaurants, U.S. supermarkets, and U.S. gas stations — excludes superstores and warehouse clubs. Restaurant purchases must be at a restaurant physically located in the US (not a US brand abroad); nightclubs/convenience stores/grocery stores excluded; restaurants located within another establishment (e.g., inside a hotel/casino) may not qualify.
- 4 points/$ (1 additional) on eligible online retail purchases from U.S. retail merchants selling physical goods, made online (in-app/browser, not in-store via mobile wallet). Excludes restaurants, supermarkets, gas stations, auto dealers, and travel/entertainment/services purchases.
- Points post to Hilton Honors account up to 12 weeks after billing period end.

CREDITS
- Hilton Statement Credit: up to $50/quarter ($200/year) for purchases made directly with a Hilton portfolio property (bookings + room-charged incidentals via Hilton-direct channel only, not OTAs). Capture rate: easy for people who book Hilton direct regularly; useless for OTA bookers or non-Hilton stays.
- Amex Venue Collection Concessions: 10% back (up to $250/year) on food & beverage purchases at participating stadiums/arenas in the Amex Venue Collection, with enrollment required. Not valid through resellers/delivery apps. Offer ends 12/31/2026.

SIGNUP BONUS
130,000 Hilton Honors Bonus Points after $3,000 spend in first 6 months. Not eligible if you have/had this Card or the Hilton Honors Amex Ascend Card (or prior versions). Points post 8-12 weeks after threshold met. Offer ends 07/29/2026.

NOTABLE PERKS
- Complimentary Hilton Honors Gold status (applied within ~12 weeks of account opening).
- Free Night Reward after $15,000 in eligible spend in a calendar year (redeemable for one standard room, double occupancy, at most Hilton properties; excludes list at HiltonHonors.com/freenightreward; expires 1 year from issuance).
- Upgrade to Hilton Honors Diamond status after $40,000 in eligible spend in a calendar year; valid through the following calendar year.
- National Car Rental Emerald Club Executive status (requires enrollment/linking).
- No foreign transaction fees.
