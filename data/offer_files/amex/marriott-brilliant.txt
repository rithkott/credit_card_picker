MARRIOTT BONVOY BRILLIANT AMERICAN EXPRESS CARD

FEES
Annual Fee: $650.
Foreign Transaction Fee: None.

EARN RATES
- Base: 2 points/$.
- 6 points/$ (4 additional) at Marriott Bonvoy properties (charged directly with participating properties, standalone Marriott-branded retail, or Marriott-branded online stores/gift cards).
- 3 points/$ (1 additional) on scheduled-airline airfare (excludes charter/private jet) and worldwide restaurants (excludes nightclubs/convenience/grocery/supermarkets; restaurant within another establishment like a hotel/casino may not qualify).
- Marriott Bonvoy Platinum Elite status (see below) earns 50% more points at participating Marriott hotels (on top of member base earn of up to 10 pts/$ from Marriott itself).
- Points post to Marriott Bonvoy account up to 12 weeks after month end.

CREDITS
- $300 Brilliant Dining Credit: up to $25/month ($300/year) for worldwide restaurant purchases; excludes gift cards/merchandise and non-restaurant merchants (bars, nightclubs, convenience/grocery/supermarkets, cafeterias, caterers, breweries, bakeries, meeting venues, restaurant within another establishment). Does not roll over month to month.
- $100 Marriott Property Credit: up to $100 per booking at Ritz-Carlton or St. Regis, only when booked with the specific "$100 Property Credit, Luxury Credit Card Rate" (2-night minimum) and paid with this Card at checkout; covers on-property dining/spa/recreation, not room/taxes/gratuities; doesn't carry over, forfeited if unused during the stay.
- Amex Venue Collection Concessions: 10% back (up to $250/year) on food & beverage purchases at participating stadiums/arenas in the Amex Venue Collection, enrollment required. Not valid via resellers/delivery apps. Offer ends 12/31/2026.
- Global Entry/TSA PreCheck fee credit: one statement credit every 4 years, up to $120 (Global Entry) or $85 (TSA PreCheck), whichever program is applied to first.

SIGNUP BONUS
Up to 150,000 Marriott Bonvoy points: 100,000 after $6,000 spend in first 6 months, plus additional 50,000 after a further $2,000 spend (total $8,000) in first 6 months. Not eligible if you have/had this Card or the Starwood Preferred Guest Amex Luxury Card (or prior versions); also excluded if holding/recently held/received bonus for Chase-issued Ritz-Carlton/Marriott Bonvoy cards (Bountiful, Boundless, Bold) within stated lookback windows (30/90/24-month, per specific card). Points post 8-12 weeks after each threshold.

NOTABLE PERKS
- 25 Elite Night Credits deposited within 60 days of account opening (only one Marriott card's Annual ENC benefit counted per member per year across issuers, highest-ENC card wins; separate benefit if you also hold a Marriott business card).
- Complimentary Marriott Bonvoy Platinum Elite status (~12 weeks to apply).
- Free Night Award each year after card renewal, redeemable at properties with redemption level ≤85,000 points (can add up to 25,000 purchased points to expand value); expires 1 year from issuance.
- Brilliant Earned Choice Award: after $60,000 in eligible spend in a calendar year, choose one of: 5 Nightly Upgrade Awards, Free Night Award (≤85K points), or 50K Marriott Bonvoy points. Must select by Feb 1 following the year earned or defaults to Nightly Upgrade Awards.
- Priority Pass Select lounge access (enrollment required; 2 free guests per visit, then guest fees apply).
- Cell phone protection, trip cancellation/interruption insurance, trip delay insurance, return protection (secondary coverage on the insurance benefits).
- Premium Global Assist Hotline (includes emergency medical transport assistance if approved/coordinated).
- Premium on-property internet access at participating hotels (one room per stay).
- Points transfer to airlines with a 5,000-mile bonus per 60,000 points transferred.
- No foreign transaction fees.
