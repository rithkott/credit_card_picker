REI Co-op Mastercard (Capital One)

Membership Eligibility Requirement (IMPORTANT)
- An active REI Co-op Membership is required. REI Co-op Membership is the standard one-time $30 lifetime membership sold by REI (not a recurring dues structure) — this is a separate purchase from the card itself. The card is marketed as "just for REI Co-op members."
- Capital One may cancel the REI Co-op Mastercard and participation in the REI Co-op Mastercard Rewards program if the cardholder cancels their REI Co-op Membership.
- REI Co-op Mastercard Rewards can only be redeemed while the cardholder maintains an active REI Co-op Membership.

Annual Fee
- $0.

Foreign Transaction Fee
- $0 (none).

Signup Bonus
- $100 REI gift card after your first purchase made outside of REI, within 60 days of account opening.
- Not available to existing or previous REI Co-op Mastercard holders.

Earn Rates (REI Co-op Mastercard Rewards program — distinct from the REI Co-op annual member dividend)
- 5% back in REI Co-op Mastercard Rewards on all purchases at REI: full-price and sale merchandise, REI gift cards, REI classes/events, and services/fees.
- 5% in REI Co-op Mastercard Rewards on donations to the REI Cooperative Action Fund (a 501(c)(3)).
- 1.5% in REI Co-op Mastercard Rewards on all other (non-REI) purchases.
- No cap on rewards earned.
- Cash advances and balance transfers do not earn rewards (net purchases only).
- Periodic "annual double rewards event" promotion: 10% total back on REI purchases during a promotional window (exact dates/frequency not specified in sources reviewed).
- IMPORTANT: this card-earned rewards balance is a separate program from REI's standalone Co-op annual member dividend (the ~10%-back dividend tied to base $30 membership, paid once a year on prior-year purchases). Do not conflate the two when modeling value.

APR
- Variable Purchase and Balance Transfer APR: 17.49%, 24.49%, or 28.49%, based on creditworthiness (as of 02/02/2026 per Capital One disclosure — may adjust with the market).
- Variable Cash Advance APR: 28.49%.

Other Fees
- Balance Transfer Fee: no fee at the standard (non-promotional) transfer APR; 3% of each balance transferred that posts at a promotional APR.
- Cash Advance Fee: greater of $5 or 5% of the cash advance amount.

Redemption
- Rewards post within 1-2 business days after the purchase itself posts; no minimum balance required to redeem.
- Redeemable ONLY at REI — online (REI.com), in-store, or via the REI app. Not a general-purpose cash-back/points program; cannot be redeemed elsewhere.
- Rewards do not expire for the life of the account, contingent on maintaining active REI Co-op Membership (see Eligibility above).
- Redemption is restricted to the primary cardholder only.

Other Notable Terms
- Card is a Mastercard World Elite product (no annual fee despite World Elite tier).
- Capital One/REI note a $2 million annual donation commitment to the REI Cooperative Action Fund tied to the card program.
- Preferred/chosen name option available on the physical card.
