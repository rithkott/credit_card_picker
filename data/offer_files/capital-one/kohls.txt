Card Types
- Two variants exist under one application: applicants apply for the Kohl's Rewards Visa
  (usable anywhere Visa is accepted); if declined for the Visa, they are automatically considered
  for the Kohl's Credit Card instead (closed-loop, "exclusively for use at Kohl's and Kohls.com").
- Credit level required: Good-Excellent (per Capital One's page).

Annual Fee
- $0 / "No annual fee" (stated directly on capitalone.com/credit-cards/kohls/).

Closed-Loop Status
- Kohl's Credit Card: closed-loop, Kohl's/Kohls.com only.
- Kohl's Rewards Visa: open-loop, accepted anywhere Visa is accepted, with bonus categories
  outside Kohl's (see Earn Rates).

Earn Rates / Kohl's Rewards Structure
- Kohl's Rewards Visa cardholders (linked to a Kohl's Rewards account), rates on transaction
  subtotal for qualifying purchases:
  - 7.5% at Kohl's stores or Kohls.com (excludes gift cards)
  - 3% on gasoline purchases
  - 2% at grocery stores
  - 1% on purchases everywhere else Visa is accepted
- Kohl's Credit Card (store-only) and general "Kohl's Rewards" program members (any payment
  method, not just the card): 5% rewards on qualifying purchases "any way you pay" at Kohl's
  stores or Kohls.com, vs. 7.5% specifically when paying with a Kohl's Card.
- Kohl's Rewards balance converts and is issued as Kohl's Cash in $5 increments "on the first day
  of the following month." Cardholders then have 30 days to spend Rewards-issued Kohl's Cash.
  "Not valid on Sephora at Kohl's."

Kohl's Cash (separate/promotional mechanic, layered on top of the Rewards program above)
- Also earned via periodic "Kohl's Cash" store promotions: customers earn a $10 Kohl's Cash
  coupon for every $50 spent in a single transaction during a stated earn window (these are
  storewide promotions available to any shopper, not exclusive to cardholders, though the card
  can be used to pay).
- Kohl's Cash coupon is earned on the amount of qualifying merchandise purchases after coupons
  are applied and before tax.
- Exclusions/fine print: qualifying purchases exclude Sephora at Kohl's merchandise and gift
  cards. Kohl's Cash may not be redeemed to purchase Sephora at Kohl's merchandise; to purchase
  Kohl's Cares cause merchandise or other charitable items; to purchase Gift Cards; as price
  adjustments; to reduce a Kohl's Card or any third-party charge account balance; or to pay for
  services or fees, including taxes and shipping. Kohl's Cash is not legal tender; no cash back.
  Kohl's Cash is applied prior to percent-off total-purchase discounts. Redemption/expiration
  windows are promotion-specific and based on Central Time.

Signup / Welcome Offer
- "Save 40% today": a one-time coupon, valid within 30 days of credit availability, usable on
  one transaction of qualifying purchases upon account opening (per capitalone.com page).

Financing / Deferred-Interest Offers
- Kohl's periodically offers promotional financing plans of 6, 12, 18, or 24 months with waived
  interest charges (standard minimum payment required) on qualifying purchases — described by
  Kohl's/secondary sources as NOT structured as deferred-interest (i.e., interest is genuinely
  waived for the promo term rather than retroactively applied if unpaid at term end).
  NEEDS human verification directly against the Cardmember Agreement / current promo terms, since
  this characterization came from a WebSearch summary rather than a direct read of the agreement.
- After a promotional plan expires, any remaining promotional-purchase balance is reported to
  carry a variable APR of 30.24% (WebSearch summary of the CFPB-filed Cardmember Agreement —
  NEEDS verification).

Rates & Fees (NEEDS verification — sourced via WebSearch summary of Cardmember Agreement /
aggregator cross-checks, not a direct parse of the official PDF)
- Purchase APR: reported as Prime + 22.74% (variable).
- Late payment fee: reported as up to $40.
- Foreign transaction fee: reportedly not applicable/not usable internationally (card cannot be
  used for purchases outside the U.S.).
- Balance transfer fee (Kohl's Rewards Visa only): reported as no fee if transferred at the
  standard Transfer APR; 4% fee if transferred at a promotional APR.
- Cash advance: not offered on the store-only Kohl's Credit Card; availability/fee on the Kohl's
  Rewards Visa not confirmed — omitted.
- Minimum interest charge, penalty APR trigger conditions, and grace-period specifics: not
  confirmed from any source fetched — omitted rather than estimated.

Perks
- Monthly exclusive savings opportunities (cardholder-only offers).
- Special anniversary/birthday-style offer every year ("special anniversary offer every year").
- Most Valued Customer (MVC) status: reach $600 in spend per year at Kohl's to receive monthly
  free-shipping opportunities.

Redemption
- Kohl's Rewards balance redeems only as Kohl's Cash (store credit), not cash back, gift cards,
  travel, or points transfer — no alternate redemption paths found.

Issued by Capital One, N.A., pursuant to a license from Visa Inc. (for the Kohl's Rewards Visa).
