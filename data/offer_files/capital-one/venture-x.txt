=== Capital One Venture X Rewards Card ===
Issuer: Capital One, N.A. (Visa Infinite)

--- Fees ---
Annual Fee: $395
APR: 19.49% - 28.49% variable APR (per product page; not cross-checked against disclosure PDF)
Foreign Transaction Fees: None

--- Earn Rates ---
- Everyday purchases: unlimited 2X miles on all transactions
- Capital One Travel bookings: 10X miles on hotels and rental cars; 5X miles on flights and
  vacation rentals
- Capital One Entertainment purchases: 5X miles per dollar
- Miles do not expire for the life of the account.

--- Signup Bonus ---
"Earn 75,000 bonus miles when you spend $4,000 on purchases within three months of opening an
account." Applicants cannot have received a Venture X bonus within the prior 48 months.

--- Credits ---
- $300 annual Capital One Travel credit
- 10,000 bonus miles every year, starting on the cardholder's first anniversary
- Global Entry/TSA PreCheck: up to $120 statement credit on application fee, one credit per
  account every four years.
- Price drop protection: up to $50 in travel credit for the difference if flight prices drop
  within 10 days of a Capital One Travel booking.
- Hotel & vacation rental stays booked via Capital One Travel: Premier Collection gets $100
  experience credit per stay + daily complimentary breakfast for two + room upgrades/early
  check-in/late checkout when available; Lifestyle Collection gets $50 experience credit per
  stay + similar upgrade/check-in benefits.

--- Notable Perks ---
- Lounge Access: primary cardholders get access to Capital One Lounges plus 1,300+ lounges
  worldwide via Priority Pass membership (after enrollment). Additional/authorized cardholders
  may purchase $125/year lounge access. Guest pricing: Capital One Lounges $45/guest visit ($25
  for ages 17 and under, free under 2); Priority Pass lounges $35/guest visit. Cardholders
  spending $75,000+ annually get up to two complimentary guests at Capital One Lounges and one
  at Capital One Landings per visit.
- Hertz Status: complimentary Hertz Gold+ upgrade to President's Circle status.
- Price match guarantee: travel credit for price differences found on flights, hotels, or rental
  cars.
- Cancel for any reason (CFAR): option to cancel a flight up to 3 hours before scheduled
  departure (per review/marketing copy; verify exact mechanics/credit form).
- Price watch alerts for flight monitoring.
- Visa Infinite protections: cellphone protection (up to $800 reimbursement for theft/damage),
  trip cancellation/interruption coverage, 90-day return protection, extended warranty
  protection, travel accident insurance, auto rental collision damage waiver, concierge
  services.
- Complimentary PRIOR travel subscription ("a $149 value" per marketing copy).
- The Cultivist membership: 50% off Enthusiast membership (up to $440 value, "up to 2 years").
- Complimentary 6-month Vinous wine magazine subscription.
- Authorized users earn rewards on their spend; primary cardholder is responsible for all
  authorized-user transactions.
- Redemption: Capital One Travel bookings, transfer to 15+ travel loyalty programs, or statement
  credit for recent travel purchases (vacation rentals, flights, hotels, campgrounds, cruises).

--- Approval ---
Credit Level Required: Excellent
