=== Capital One Venture X Rewards Card ===

Annual Fee: $395

APR: 19.49% - 28.49% variable APR (per product page; not cross-checked against disclosure PDF)

Credit Level Required: Excellent

Welcome / Signup Bonus:
"Earn 75,000 bonus miles when you spend $4,000 on purchases within three months of opening an
account." Applicants cannot have received a Venture X bonus within the prior 48 months.

Rewards Structure:
- Everyday purchases: unlimited 2X miles on all transactions
- Capital One Travel bookings: 10X miles on hotels and rental cars; 5X miles on flights and
  vacation rentals
- Capital One Entertainment purchases: 5X miles per dollar
- Miles do not expire for the life of the account.

Annual Credits & Recurring Bonuses:
- $300 annual Capital One Travel credit
- 10,000 bonus miles every year, starting on the cardholder's first anniversary

Travel / Airport Benefits:
- Lounge Access: primary cardholders get access to Capital One Lounges plus 1,300+ lounges
  worldwide via Priority Pass membership (after enrollment). Additional/authorized cardholders
  may purchase $125/year lounge access.
- Guest pricing at lounges: Capital One Lounges $45/guest visit ($25 for ages 17 and under, free
  under 2). Cardholders spending $75,000+ annually get up to two complimentary guests at
  Capital One Lounges and one at Capital One Landings per visit. Priority Pass lounges: $35/guest
  visit.
- Global Entry/TSA PreCheck: up to $120 statement credit on application fee, one credit per
  account every four years.
- Hertz Status: complimentary Hertz Gold+ upgrade to President's Circle status.
- No foreign transaction fees on purchases made outside the United States.
- Price drop protection: up to $50 in travel credit for the difference if flight prices drop
  within 10 days of a Capital One Travel booking.
- Price match guarantee: travel credit for price differences found on flights, hotels, or rental
  cars.
- Cancel for any reason (CFAR): option to cancel a flight up to 3 hours before scheduled
  departure (per review/marketing copy; verify exact mechanics/credit form).
- Price watch alerts for flight monitoring.

Hotel & Vacation Rental Benefits (book via Capital One Travel):
- Premier Collection: $100 experience credit per stay; daily complimentary breakfast for two;
  room upgrades, early check-in, late checkout when available.
- Lifestyle Collection: $50 experience credit per stay; similar upgrade/check-in benefits as
  Premier.

Visa Infinite Protections:
- Cellphone protection: up to $800 reimbursement for theft or damage.
- Trip cancellation/interruption coverage for medical emergencies.
- 90-day return protection.
- Extended warranty protection on eligible purchases.
- Travel accident insurance.
- Auto rental collision damage waiver.
- Concierge services for reservations, event tickets, gift arrangements.

Additional Perks:
- Complimentary PRIOR travel subscription (marketing copy cites "a $149 value").
- The Cultivist membership: 50% off Enthusiast membership (up to $440 value, "up to 2 years").
- Complimentary 6-month Vinous wine magazine subscription.
- Security alerts and Eno assistant monitoring.
- Virtual card numbers for secure online shopping.
- $0 liability for unauthorized charges.

Authorized Users:
"Share Venture X benefits with someone you trust and earn rewards on every dollar they spend by
adding a cardholder to your account." Primary account holder is responsible for all authorized
user transactions.

Redemption Options:
Miles can be redeemed through Capital One Travel bookings, transferred to 15+ travel loyalty
programs, or redeemed for recent travel purchases including vacation rentals, flights, hotels,
campgrounds, and cruises.

Issuer: Capital One, N.A. (Visa Infinite)
