=== Capital One Savor Rewards Credit Card ===

Annual Fee: $0

Welcome / Signup Bonus:
"Earn a one-time $250 cash bonus once you spend $500 on purchases within the first 3 months from
account opening." Limited-time offer; existing cardmembers ineligible if they received a bonus
in the past 48 months.

Rewards Structure:
- Unlimited 5% cash back on hotels, vacation rentals, and rental cars booked through Capital One
  Travel.
- Unlimited 3% cash back at grocery stores, on dining, entertainment, and popular streaming
  services (grocery category excludes superstores like Walmart and Target).
- Unlimited 1% cash back on all other purchases.
- Rewards don't expire for the life of the account.

APR:
0% intro APR for 12 months on purchases and balance transfers; 18.49% - 28.49% variable APR
after that. Balance transfer fee applies.

Foreign Transaction Fees: None ("no foreign transaction fees on purchases made outside of the
United States").

Credit Level Required: Excellent.

Travel Benefits:
- Free price drop protection (up to $50 travel credit) if a booked flight's price drops.
- Price match guarantee with 24-hour window for flights, hotels, and rental cars.
- Cancel for any reason (CFAR) option up to 3 hours before departure.
- Price watch alerts.

Issuer: Capital One, N.A.
