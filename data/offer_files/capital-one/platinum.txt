=== Capital One Platinum Credit Card ===
Issuer: Capital One, N.A.

--- Fees ---
Annual Fee: $0 ("No Annual Fees" — confirmed via direct page fetch).
APR (per secondary-source aggregation, verify):
Purchase APR: 28.99% variable.
Cash advance APR: 28.99% variable.
Cash advance fee: $5 or 5% of the amount of each cash advance, whichever is greater.
Foreign Transaction Fees: None (confirmed via direct page fetch: "you don't have to pay foreign
transaction fees when you use your card outside the U.S.").

--- Earn Rates ---
None. Page explicitly states "No Annual Fees or Rewards" — this is a credit-building card, not
a rewards card.

--- Signup Bonus ---
None disclosed on either page.

--- Credits ---
None.

--- Notable Perks ---
- Security Deposit: Not required — this is an unsecured card (distinct from Capital One
  Platinum Secured).
- Credit Line Increase Policy (confirmed via direct page fetch): "Platinum cardholders are
  automatically considered for a credit limit increase in as little as six months." Credit
  limits vary based on financial situation and creditworthiness. Manual credit-limit-increase
  requests are also possible per secondary sources.
- Credit bureau reporting for building credit history.
- Tap-to-pay/contactless payment capability.
- Eno AI assistant monitoring; virtual card numbers for secure online shopping.
- Fraud alerts, card lock feature, $0 fraud liability for unauthorized charges.

--- Approval ---
Credit Level Required: Fair credit (FICO 580-669 per the Capital One "all about" page). Those
with lower scores may be directed to the Platinum Secured card instead.
