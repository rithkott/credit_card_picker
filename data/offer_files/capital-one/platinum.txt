=== Capital One Platinum Credit Card ===

Annual Fee: $0 ("No Annual Fees" — confirmed via direct page fetch).

Rewards: None. Page explicitly states "No Annual Fees or Rewards" — this is a credit-building
card, not a rewards card.

Welcome / Signup Bonus: None disclosed on either page.

APR (per secondary-source aggregation, verify):
Purchase APR: 28.99% variable.
Cash advance APR: 28.99% variable.
Cash advance fee: $5 or 5% of the amount of each cash advance, whichever is greater.

Foreign Transaction Fees: None (confirmed via direct page fetch: "you don't have to pay foreign
transaction fees when you use your card outside the U.S.").

Security Deposit: Not required — this is an unsecured card (distinct from Capital One Platinum
Secured).

Credit Line Increase Policy (confirmed via direct page fetch):
"Platinum cardholders are automatically considered for a credit limit increase in as little as
six months." Credit limits vary based on financial situation and creditworthiness. Manual
credit-limit-increase requests are also possible per secondary sources.

Credit Level Required: Fair credit (FICO 580-669 per the Capital One "all about" page). Those
with lower scores may be directed to the Platinum Secured card instead.

Other Benefits (confirmed via direct page fetch):
- Credit bureau reporting for building credit history.
- Tap-to-pay/contactless payment capability.
- Capital One Mobile app access.
- Eno AI assistant monitoring.
- Virtual card numbers for secure online shopping.
- Customizable payment options and automatic payments.
- Fraud alerts, card lock feature, $0 fraud liability for unauthorized charges.

Marketing metadata: "#1 Credit Card for Customer Satisfaction" per J.D. Power (as displayed on
page); 4.5-star rating based on 9,556 reviews, 91% would recommend.

Issuer: Capital One, N.A.
