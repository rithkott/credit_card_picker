Nordstrom Visa Signature® / Nordstrom Visa Platinum® Card (TD Bank) — open-loop

Note: Nordstrom also offers a closed-loop, store-only "Nordstrom Credit Card" that can
only be used at Nordstrom/Nordstrom Rack, with a different (simpler, Nordstrom-only)
rewards structure — see nordstrom.txt. Approval logic: applicants are first considered
for this Visa Platinum/Signature card based on creditworthiness; if they don't qualify,
they're instead considered for the store-only retail card. Which of the two Visa tiers
(Signature vs. Platinum) an approved applicant receives also depends on creditworthiness;
both tiers share the same credit-card terms and conditions, but Visa Signature carries
additional Visa-network benefits (see Visa Benefits below).

Annual Fee
- None.

Open-Loop Scope
- Usable anywhere Visa is accepted, not just at Nordstrom.
- The official Nordstrom Credit Card Agreement distinguishes "Nordstrom Purchases"
  (purchases at Nordstrom full-line stores, Nordstrom Rack stores, Nordstrom.com,
  NordstromRack.com, Nordstrom catalogs, and Nordstrom gift cards via CashStar) from
  "Non-Nordstrom Purchases" (everything else charged to the Visa).
- No foreign transaction fee.

Earn Rates
- At Nordstrom: same Nordy Club tiered earning as the store card — 1 / 1.5 / 2 / 3
  points per $1 for Member / Influencer / Ambassador / Icon status respectively, per the
  official Nordy Club Terms & Conditions (Influencer status is instant upon getting the
  card). See nordstrom.txt for the full tier-threshold detail and a flagged discrepancy
  between this T&Cs table and Nordstrom's own cardmember-benefits marketing page (which
  describes "2 base points/$1" at Influencer and "3 base points/$1" at Ambassador/Icon).
- Outside Nordstrom (Non-Nordstrom Purchases), per the official Nordy Club Terms &
  Conditions and Nordstrom's Visa cardmember-benefits page:
  - 2 points per $1 on: gas/EV charging, groceries, dining, and streaming services.
  - 1 point per $1 on everything else.
- No points earned at Nordstrom Rack or Last Chance beyond the 5% cardmember discount,
  per the Nordy Club T&Cs (this conflicts with the cardmember-benefits marketing page's
  suggestion that Rack purchases earn rewards; both are official Nordstrom sources and
  the discrepancy is unresolved — flagged rather than guessed at).
- Exclusions: no points on charitable donations, alcoholic beverages or certain brands,
  or purchases from standalone vending machines (per Nordy Club T&Cs).

Nordstrom Notes (Redemption)
- 1,000 points = $10 Nordstrom Note (Member/Influencer tiers); 2,000 points = $20 Note
  (Ambassador/Icon tiers) — same effective $10-per-1,000-points rate.
- Positive point balance required to convert to a Note.
- Points expire after 12 consecutive months without Nordy Club program activity; Notes
  expire per the date on the individual Note (Bonus Notes may expire sooner).
- Redemption is only toward Nordstrom Notes (store credit) — there is no cash-back,
  statement-credit, or travel-transfer redemption path found for this program.

Visa Signature / Visa Platinum Benefits
- Visa Signature cardholders get "preferred access to events" and enhanced hotel benefit
  tiers per Visa's network benefits, plus extended warranty (up to a year added) on
  eligible purchases made with a manufacturer's warranty.
- Visa Platinum cardholders get a separate, lesser set of "Visa Platinum Offers &
  Perks" (exact list of inclusions was not itemized on the pages reviewed — could not
  verify a full benefit list distinct from Signature beyond what's noted above).
- Roadside dispatch emergency service (referenced on Nordstrom's Visa benefits page;
  exact terms/costs of use not itemized there).
- Zero liability for confirmed fraudulent charges.

Nordstrom-Specific Perks (shared with the store card — see nordstrom.txt for full detail)
- Early access to the Nordstrom Anniversary Sale.
- Instant Influencer Nordy Club status upon card approval.
- Extra 5% discount at Nordstrom Rack when paying with the card.
- Alterations reimbursement (tiered by Nordy Club status, up to "Unlimited Free
  Alterations" at Icon status) — same program as the store card.

Welcome / Signup Offer
- Time-limited promotional offers were observed during research (e.g., "$40 off your
  next purchase" / "$60 off your next purchase" depending on page and date) — these
  change frequently and are shared with the store-card application flow; do not treat
  either figure as a permanent, version-specific bonus. Confirm the live offer at
  application time. Offer excludes current/previous cardmembers and employees; discount
  requires paying directly with the card (Apple Pay, PayPal, Afterpay excluded even if
  linked).

Financing
- A 12-month 0% introductory APR on purchases/qualifying balance transfers was reported
  by secondary (non-official) sources but was NOT confirmed in the official Account
  Opening Disclosure reviewed here (which shows only standard ongoing APRs with no
  stated intro period) — flagged as UNVERIFIED; omit from modeling until confirmed on
  an official page.
- The Nordstrom Credit Card Agreement provides for ad hoc "Special Payment Options" at
  TD Bank's discretion (temporary reduced APR/interest), not a standing offer.

Rates & Fees (from the official TD Bank Account Opening Disclosure, accurate as of
January 22, 2026)
- APR for Nordstrom Purchases: 31.90% (variable, tied to Prime Rate).
- APR for Non-Nordstrom Purchases: 31.90% (variable, tied to Prime Rate) — same rate as
  Nordstrom purchases.
- APR for Cash Advances: 32.90% (variable).
- Cash Advance Fee: greater of $10 or 5% of the cash advance amount.
- Foreign Transaction Fee: None.
- Minimum interest charge: $0.50 per billing cycle in which interest is charged.
- Grace period: due date is at least 25 days after the close of each billing cycle; no
  interest on purchases if the entire balance is paid by the due date (cash advances
  begin accruing interest immediately, with no grace period).
- Late Payment Fee: up to $40.
- Returned Payment Fee: up to $29.
- Balance calculation method: Average Daily Balance.
- An earlier (August 2020) version of this disclosure showed credit-tiered APRs
  (18.90%–25.90% depending on creditworthiness at account opening) rather than the flat
  31.90% now shown in the January 2026 disclosure — rates clearly change over time; use
  the January 2026 figures above as current, but confirm live rates before relying on
  them long-term.
