Nordstrom Credit Card (TD Bank) — store-only / closed-loop

Note: Nordstrom also offers an open-loop Nordstrom Visa Signature/Platinum version
that can be used anywhere Visa is accepted, with different (lower/simpler) earn rates
outside Nordstrom and additional Visa Signature/Platinum perks — see nordstrom-visa.txt.
Approval logic: applicants are first considered for a Visa Platinum/Signature card based
on creditworthiness; if they don't qualify for the Visa version, they're considered for
this retail-only card instead.

Annual Fee
- None.

Closed-Loop Scope
- Can be used ONLY at "Nordstrom locations," defined in the official Nordstrom Credit
  Card Agreement as: Nordstrom full-line stores, Nordstrom Rack stores, Nordstrom.com,
  NordstromRack.com, through Nordstrom catalogs, and for purchases of Nordstrom gift
  cards through CashStar. Per the agreement text, "the Nordstrom retail credit card
  cannot be used to make purchases at Nordstrom stores located in Canada."
- Not a Visa/Mastercard/etc. — cannot be used anywhere else.

Nordy Club Status & Earn Rates
- Cardmembers are automatically enrolled in the Nordy Club and get instant Influencer
  status (no minimum annual spend required to hold that status as a cardholder).
- Per official Nordy Club Terms & Conditions, points-per-$1 at Nordstrom/Nordstrom Rack/
  Nordstrom Local are tiered by Nordy Club status (based on Net Purchase Balance per
  calendar year):
  - Member (no card, $0 NPB): 1 point/$1
  - Influencer ($500 NPB, or automatic with any Nordstrom credit card): 1.5 points/$1
  - Ambassador ($5,000 NPB): 2 points/$1
  - Icon ($15,000 NPB — requires a Nordstrom credit card to reach): 3 points/$1
- NOTE ON CONFLICTING SOURCES: Nordstrom's own cardmember-benefits marketing page
  (nordstrom.com/browse/nordy-club/manage-card/visa-cardmember-benefits) describes
  cardholders earning "2 base points/$1" at Influencer status and "3 base points/$1" at
  Ambassador/Icon status — a different framing than the 1/1.5/2/3 table in the official
  Nordy Club Terms & Conditions page. Both are official Nordstrom sources; this
  discrepancy could not be resolved with certainty and is flagged here rather than
  guessed at. Treat the Terms & Conditions figures (1/1.5/2/3) as the more legally
  authoritative source, but verify current tier math before relying on it.
- No points earned at Nordstrom Rack or Last Chance locations beyond the standard
  cardmember discount (see below) — per Nordy Club T&Cs, "No points earned at Nordstrom
  Rack or Last Chance (except 5% cardmember discount)." (This conflicts with the
  cardmember-benefits page's claim of earning rewards at Rack; noting both.)
- Exclusions: per Nordy Club T&Cs, "Members do not earn points on charitable donations,
  purchases of alcoholic beverages or certain brands, or purchases from standalone
  vending machines."

Nordstrom Notes (Redemption)
- 1,000 points = $10 Nordstrom Note (Member/Influencer tiers).
- 2,000 points = $20 Nordstrom Note (Ambassador/Icon tiers) — i.e., same $10-per-1,000
  redemption rate, just issued as larger Notes at higher tiers.
- A positive point balance is required to convert points into a Nordstrom Note.
- All points expire after 12 consecutive months without Nordy Club program activity.
- Notes expire per the date printed on the individual Note; Bonus Notes (e.g. signup
  offers) may have shorter expiration windows than standard earned Notes.

Perks
- Extra 5% discount at Nordstrom Rack when paying with the card (cardmember discount).
- Free/reimbursed alterations: Influencer and Ambassador tiers get alterations
  reimbursed (as a Nordstrom Note) up to a tier-specific benefit amount; Icon tier gets
  "Unlimited Free Alterations" at Nordstrom locations on items purchased with the
  Nordstrom card. Exclusions include Nordstrom.com orders shipped outside the U.S. and
  Nordstrom Rack purchases.
- Early access to the Nordstrom Anniversary Sale (exact advance-access window/dates are
  set per sale event and were not independently verified here — could not find a
  standing, dated policy on nordstrom.com beyond "early access for cardmembers").
- Birthday gift/offer valid at Nordstrom Rack stores, NordstromRack.com, and
  Nordstrom.com during birthday month; birthdate must be saved to account at least 30
  days before the birthday month begins. (Sourced from secondary aggregator coverage,
  not confirmed verbatim on an official Nordstrom page — flagged as lower confidence.)

Welcome / Signup Offer
- As of research date, Nordstrom's official card-signup page (nordstromrack.com/
  customer-service/nordstromcard) advertised "$40 OFF your next purchase when you apply
  and get approved." A separate nordstrom.com application page showed a "$60 Off Your
  Next Purchase" offer expiring August 9 (year not stated on page). These are
  time-limited promotional offers that change frequently — do not treat either dollar
  figure as a permanent term; confirm the live offer at application time.
- Offer excludes current/previous cardmembers and Nordstrom employees. Discount
  requires paying directly with the credit card; Apple Pay, PayPal, and Afterpay are
  excluded even if linked to the card.

Financing
- 0% introductory APR for 12 months from account opening on purchases and qualifying
  balance transfers has been reported by secondary sources (not directly confirmed in
  the official Account Opening Disclosure reviewed here, which shows a standard ongoing
  APR with no stated intro period) — flagged as UNVERIFIED, omit from any modeling
  until confirmed on an official page.
- The Nordstrom Credit Card Agreement (official TD Bank agreement, effective/accurate
  as of January 22, 2026) provides for "Special Payment Options": "From time to time TD
  Bank may reduce my Annual Percentage Rate or interest charge, or allow me to take
  advantage of other options that may be made available." These are ad hoc, not a
  standing 0% offer.

Rates & Fees (from the official TD Bank Account Opening Disclosure, accurate as of
January 22, 2026)
- APR for Nordstrom Purchases: 31.90% (variable, tied to Prime Rate).
- Minimum interest charge: $0.50 per billing cycle in which interest is charged.
- Grace period: due date is at least 25 days after the close of each billing cycle; no
  interest on purchases if the entire balance is paid by the due date.
- Late Payment Fee: up to $40.
- Returned Payment Fee: up to $29.
- Balance calculation method: Average Daily Balance.
- No separately-disclosed cash-advance or foreign-transaction fee line item on this
  card's Schumer box (unlike the Visa version), consistent with it being a closed-loop
  store card without a cash-advance feature.
