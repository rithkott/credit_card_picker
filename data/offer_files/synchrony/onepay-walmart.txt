OnePay Walmart Spend Card (issued by Synchrony Bank, in partnership with OnePay/One Finance, Inc. and Walmart Inc.) — closed-loop, Walmart-only, private-label card

RELATIONSHIP TO THE OTHER ONEPAY/WALMART CARD (read before using this file):
This program has TWO distinct card products, confirmed via official Synchrony Bank cardholder-agreement PDFs (same filing family, "FR833282333"):
  1. THIS FILE — OnePay Walmart Spend Card: closed-loop/private-label, usable ONLY at Walmart, earns NO rewards.
  2. OnePay CashRewards World Mastercard — open-loop, usable anywhere Mastercard is accepted, earns cash back. See sibling file: data/offer_files/synchrony/onepay-walmart-mastercard.txt
Both are issued by Synchrony Bank; both require the applicant to have a OnePay account in good standing; both were announced together (June 9, 2025 press release) as a single "credit card program" with Walmart, going live "this fall" (fall 2025). Per Mastercard's official press release: "OnePay and Synchrony will introduce both a general-purpose card, which will serve as the program's signature card and be available to use anywhere Mastercard is accepted, and a private label card, which will be exclusively for Walmart purchases." This file covers that private-label card. Secondary coverage (not primary-sourced) characterizes the Spend Card as the option "for shoppers who don't qualify for the Mastercard version" — i.e., it appears to function as a lower-tier/soft-decline fallback within the same OnePay application funnel rather than an independently marketed card line. Terms differ materially: no rewards program, single flat APR (vs. two-tier APR on the Mastercard version), no cash-advance feature, and no foreign-transaction-fee clause (moot, since it's Walmart-only/domestic use).

Annual Fee
- $0 / no annual fee (confirmed on OnePay help-center article: "There is no annual fee for either the CashRewards Card or the Walmart Spend Card").

Signup/Welcome Bonus
- None found. This card does not appear to carry a welcome/signup bonus — none is mentioned in any official source fetched, consistent with it being a no-rewards product.

Earn Rates
- None. Per official help-center article and the card's Account Agreement, this card does not earn any cash back, points, or rewards of any kind. It is purely a store financing/spending instrument for Walmart purchases.

Foreign Transaction Fee
- Not applicable / not disclosed. The card can only be used "for purchases from Walmart" (per the official Account Agreement's "Use Of Your Account" clause) — it is domestic/closed-loop only, and no foreign-transaction-fee clause appears in the official Rates and Fees Table or fee sections (unlike the Mastercard version, which explicitly states no FTF). Presumed moot given the closed-loop, Walmart-only usage restriction, but not explicitly confirmed with a "no FTF" statement in this card's own agreement.

APR (per official Synchrony Rates and Fees Table, document dated 3/2026)
- Single tier (no Account Type 1/2 split, unlike the Mastercard version): Purchase APR = Prime Rate + 23.99% (illustrative APR as of January 1, 2026: 30.74%, daily rate .08422%).
- No cash-advance APR listed — this card does not offer cash advances (confirmed by the Account Agreement's "Use Of Your Account" clause, which only authorizes "purchases from Walmart," with no cash-advance provision, unlike the Mastercard version's explicit cash-advance section).
- Grace period: due date is at least 23 days after the close of each billing cycle; no interest charged on purchases if the entire balance is paid by the due date each month.
- Minimum Interest Charge: no less than $2.00 if interest is charged.

Other Fees (per official Synchrony Account Agreement, dated 3/2026)
- No cash advance fee (card does not support cash advances).
- No balance transfer fee/terms found (not offered/not mentioned).
- Late Payment Fee: $30 if the total minimum payment was paid on time in each of the prior six billing cycles; $41 if not. Never more than the total minimum payment that was due.
- Returned Payment Fee: $30 if no returned-payment fee occurred in the prior six billing cycles; $41 if one did occur in that period. Never more than the amount permitted by applicable law. May be charged even if the payment is honored on a subsequent representment.
- Minimum Payment: greater of (a) $30 (or $41 including past-due amounts, if the total minimum payment was missed by the due date in any of the prior six billing cycles), or (b) past-due amounts + 1% of new balance (excluding special-promotion balances) + late fees charged in the current cycle + interest charged in the current cycle + any special-promotion payment due. Rounded up to the next whole dollar; never more than the new balance.
- Balance calculation method: "daily balance" — interest calculated by applying the daily rate to the daily balance each day; late/returned-payment fees and credit-insurance/debt-cancellation fees (if any) treated as new purchases for balance purposes.

Account/Program Structure Notes (from the official Account Agreement)
- Governing law: federal law and, where state law applies, the laws of Utah (agreement "accepted by us in Utah"). Synchrony Bank address of record: 170 Election Road, Suite 125, Draper, UT 84020 — identical contracting-bank language to the CashRewards Mastercard agreement.
- "OnePay Account" clause: cardholders are not required to have this card to have a OnePay account, but must have a OnePay account in good standing to apply for and maintain this card account. If the linked OnePay account is closed for any reason, this card account will also be closed.
- Information sharing: applicant information is shared between Synchrony Bank, "One Finance, Inc." (OnePay's legal entity name), and Walmart Inc.
- Standard binding arbitration clause (with 45-day opt-out right, identical structure to the Mastercard version's agreement).
- Card usable only "for purchases from Walmart" (closed-loop; explicitly does not extend to any other merchant, unlike the Mastercard sibling product).
