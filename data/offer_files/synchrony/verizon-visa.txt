Verizon Visa Card (Synchrony Bank)

Annual Fee
- No annual fee (confirmed on both verizon.com marketing page and FAQ page).

Signup/Welcome Bonus
- $50 statement credit when you open an account and make a purchase in the first 90 days.
- Additional $100 statement credit when you spend $1,500 using the card in the first 90 days (total up to $150 cash back).
- Credits post within 1-2 billing cycles.
- Account must remain open, in good standing, and not become delinquent.
- Limited time offer; only one per account.

Earn Rates ("Verizon Dollars")
- 4% Verizon Dollars on grocery store purchases.
- 4% Verizon Dollars on gas stations and EV charging station purchases.
- 4% Verizon Dollars on restaurants, including takeout and delivery.
- 4% Verizon Dollars on Verizon purchases (excludes Verizon bill payment).
- 1% Verizon Dollars on Verizon Wireless account bill or Verizon Fios account bill payments.
- 1% Verizon Dollars on all other purchases where Visa credit cards are accepted.
- No limit on the amount of Verizon Dollars that can be earned ("There's no limit on the amount of Verizon Dollars you can earn for purchases.").
- Rewards Program Terms and Conditions note: rewards categories are determined by merchant category codes (MCCs) assigned by payment processors/networks, not by Verizon.
- Verizon Dollars are NOT earned on: unauthorized or fraudulent charges, the purchase of debt cancellation products, cash advances, balance transfers, or interest/fees charged on the account. (Verbatim from Rewards Program Terms and Conditions.)

Device Financing
- Verizon Wireless customers get 0% APR financing with 36 equal monthly payments on select device purchases made with the Verizon Visa Card.
- Interest-free financing also applies to accessories priced $100+ over 12 months.

Bill-Pay / Auto Pay Credit
- Auto Pay enrollees get up to $10/month off per eligible wireless line (max 12 lines) or Fios service. (Exact eligibility/mechanics beyond this were not further detailed on the pages fetched — not found in more granular form.)

Redemption of Verizon Dollars
- Redeemable toward:
  - Lowering/paying the Verizon Visa Card statement balance ($1 Verizon Dollar = $1 credit, $1.00 minimum redemption).
  - Verizon Wireless or Fios bill payments.
  - Purchasing devices and accessories (smartwatches, headphones, 5G phones, gaming consoles/accessories).
  - Device payment agreement down payments or payoffs.
- Redemption channels: My Verizon app/website, Verizon retail stores, the Synchrony account manager portal, or authorized retailers.
- Account-type redemption rules (from Rewards Program Terms and Conditions):
  - Wireless-only accountholders access/redeem rewards through their Wireless account.
  - Fios-only accountholders access/redeem through their Fios account.
  - Dual-account holders (both Wireless and Fios) can only redeem through the account under which the card was opened.
  - Account Owners and Account Managers can view/redeem rewards linked to their accounts.

Expiration / Forfeiture of Verizon Dollars
- No fixed expiration date on Verizon Dollars, but forfeiture rules apply:
  - 24-month inactivity rule: no transactions or reward activity for 24 months forfeits the balance.
  - Forfeited 90 days after credit card account closure (if no other linked cards exist).
  - Immediate forfeiture upon cancellation of the linked Verizon Wireless or Fios account.
  - Forfeiture also on change of account ownership.

Foreign Transaction Fee
- None. Marketing page: "no additional, surprise fees on foreign transactions." FAQ page: "No foreign transaction fees." Account Agreement (Synchrony, dated 6/2021): "Foreign Transaction [fee]: None," with standard Visa currency-conversion-procedure disclosure (Visa converts non-USD transactions using its own currency conversion rate, which may differ from the rate in effect on the transaction or posting date).

APR
- Per Verizon's official FAQ page, stated as "APR (New Accounts as of 1/1/26)": Variable Purchase APRs are 22.24%, 27.24%, or 33.24% (tiered by creditworthiness). Penalty APRs: 32.24%, 37.24%, or 39.99%.
- NOTE: The CFPB-filed Synchrony Bank Account Agreement & Pricing Information document is dated 6/2021 and shows older/different figures (Purchase APR = prime rate + 15.49%, with an illustrative 18.74% APR "as of May 1, 2020"; Cash Advance APR = 29.99% flat). This document is clearly out of date relative to the FAQ page's 1/1/26 figures — flagging the discrepancy rather than reconciling it, since no more current official Schumer box/account agreement PDF was found during this research pass.
- Minimum interest charge (per 6/2021 agreement): no less than $2.00 if interest is charged.

Other Fees (per Synchrony Bank Account Agreement, dated 6/2021 — not confirmed current as of 2026, but fee structure of this type typically changes less often than APR):
- Balance Transfer Fee: $5 or 5% of the transfer amount, whichever is greater.
- Cash Advance Fee: $10 or 5% of the cash advance amount, whichever is greater.
- Late Payment Fee: $28 if the total minimum payment was paid on time in each of the prior six billing cycles; $39 if not. Never more than the total minimum payment that was due.
- Returned Payment Fee: $28, never more than the amount permitted by applicable law.
- Minimum Payment: greater of (a) $28 (or $39 including past-due amounts, if a payment was missed in any of the prior six billing cycles), or (b) past-due amounts + 1% of new balance + late fees charged in the current cycle + interest charged in the current cycle + any special-promotion payment due. Rounded up to the next whole dollar; never more than the new balance.
- Balance calculation method: "daily balance" (interest applied daily to each balance type - purchases, balance transfers, cash advances - separately).
