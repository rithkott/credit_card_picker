Annual Fee
- $0 / no annual fee (consistent across all official sources found).

Everyday Discount / Rewards Structure
- 5% off every eligible purchase or order charged to the Lowe's Advantage Card ("5% Off Your Lowe's Advantage Card Purchase"), applied instantly at checkout, applied after all other applicable discounts. Customer must still pay applicable sales tax.
- Choice mechanic (verbatim from official PDF): "Special Financing and discount offers can't be combined. If your purchase or order is over the minimum purchase required, you will be asked to select the offer you would like." I.e., on any qualifying purchase, the cardholder must choose EITHER the 5% discount OR a special financing offer for that transaction — never both, and only one credit-related promotional offer can be applied to any one item on a sales receipt.
- 5% discount exclusions (verbatim, official PDF): can't be combined with or used on (i) any other promotion, discount, markdown, coupon/bar code, rebate or offer, including Lowe's volume/special discount programs (Contractor Packs™, Buy in Bulk, Quote Support Program "QSP", Special Value, New Lower Price, Was:Now, Military Discount, employee discount, Lowe's Price Match Guarantee); (ii) associate discretion price adjustments; (iii) any services (extended protection/replacement plans, shipping, delivery, assembly, or installation charges); (iv) fees or taxes; (v) previous sales; (vi) gift cards; or (vii) THOR, NXR, AGA, MARVEL, Bosch Benchmark, Heartland, ICON, Fisher & Paykel, Monogram, SMEG, Liebherr, Weber, or Kichler products.
- Offer subject to credit approval; excludes Lowe's Business Credit accounts, all Lowe's Visa® accounts, and Lowe's Canada Credit accounts.

Special Financing / Deferred Interest Promotions
All special-financing offers below are deferred-interest promotions: they are NOT automatic (must be requested/selected at time of purchase), cannot be combined with the 5% discount or with each other, and share this fine print (verbatim, official PDFs): "Interest will be charged to your account from the purchase date if the promotional purchase is not paid in full within [N] months. Minimum monthly payments required. No interest will be assessed on the promotional purchase if you pay the promotional purchase in full within [N] months from the purchase date. If you do not, interest will be assessed on the promotional purchase from the purchase date. Depending on purchase amount, promotion length and payment allocation, the required minimum monthly payment may or may not pay off purchase by end of promotional period. Some or all of the minimum payment based on the promotional balance may be applied to other account balances. Regular account terms apply to non-promotional purchases and, after promotion ends, to promotional purchases."

- Standard: No Interest if Paid in Full within 6 Months, on purchases of $299 or more (confirmed via multiple official/search sources; standalone 6-month PDF itself could not be directly fetched in this pass, but the $299/6-month combination is corroborated by both search-engine indexing of lowes.com and by the general deferred-interest fine print structure common to all the other promo PDFs fetched directly).
- Category-specific, longer deferred-interest window: No Interest if Paid in Full within 12 Months, on Appliance purchases or orders of $299 or more (confirmed directly from official PDF, offer window "now through 1/31/25" — a limited-time window, so the 12-month appliance offer is not necessarily always active). Lowe's official credit-center pages (per search snippets) also describe a parallel 12-month/$299+ offer covering installed flooring, installed HVAC, and custom cabinets/countertops, but this could not be independently verified against a directly-fetched PDF in this pass.
- Limited-time, higher-minimum offer: No Interest if Paid in Full within 18 Months, on an in-store product purchase or order of $999 or more WITH an Installation Service, made on the MyLowe's Rewards Credit Card (confirmed directly from official PDF; offer window 10/23/25 through 10/31/25 — i.e. this specific 18-month window had already lapsed by the 2026-07-04 research date; whether an equivalent/renewed 18-month offer is active now was not confirmed).
- Fixed Payment financing (longer-term, non-deferred-interest installment plan) for purchases of $2,000 or more: cardholder can choose 36 Fixed Monthly Payments at 7.99% APR, 60 Fixed Monthly Payments at 8.99% APR, or 84 Fixed Monthly Payments at 9.99% APR (36- and 60-month options apply to installed sales only, per search-engine snippet of official page). This is a fixed-rate installment plan, not a 0%-then-deferred-interest promotion — interest accrues from the purchase date at the stated reduced APR, with fixed monthly payments required until paid in full. Could not directly fetch the source PDF/page to verify verbatim; sourced from search-engine snippet of lowes.com official credit-offers page.

Signup Bonus
- 20% off the first purchase (verbatim: "20% Off Your Lowe's Advantage Card Purchase"), capped at a maximum discount of $100.
- Limit of one 20% off coupon per new credit account; not transferable.
- Cannot be combined with the 5% Off Every Day offer or any other credit-related discount offer, and carries the same category/service exclusions as the 5% offer (also excludes Weber, Kichler, and Miele products per the 20%-off terms specifically).
- Redemption mechanics: for accounts opened in-store, the discount is not automatic — must ask the cashier to apply it at time of purchase. For accounts opened online, via Text-to-Apply, or QR code, a one-time coupon/promo code is issued, usable in-store or online. If a credit decision isn't received the same day but approval comes later, a 20% coupon is mailed with the new card.

APR
- For new accounts, as of 3/7/24 (per official Synchrony account agreement / promo PDFs): Purchase APR 31.99%, Penalty APR 36.99%.
- Daily periodic rate: .08765% (corresponds to 31.99% APR); Penalty APR daily rate .10135% (corresponds to 36.99% APR). Interest = daily balance x daily rate x 1/365.
- Penalty APR trigger: may apply if the total minimum payment due is not received by the due date two or more times during any 12 consecutive billing cycles.
- Minimum interest charge: $2.00.
- One earlier-dated official promo PDF (2023 copyright, no update date shown) states a lower "Standard APR is 26.99%" for new accounts — this is superseded by the "as of 3/7/24" 31.99%/36.99% figures found in more recent (2025-dated) official PDFs; flagging the discrepancy rather than reconciling it, since the 2023 PDF may simply not have been refreshed after the 3/7/24 rate change.
- Existing cardholders should see their own credit card agreement for applicable terms (their APR may differ from the "new accounts" figures above).

Other Fees
- Late Payment Fee: the greater of $30, or $41 (inclusive of any past-due amount) if the total minimum payment due was not paid by the due date in one or more of the prior billing cycles. Never more than the total minimum payment that was due.
- Returned Payment Fee: charged for any returned/dishonored/unprocessable payment (check, other instrument, or electronic payment authorization); amount will not exceed what is permitted by applicable law (specific dollar figure not found).
- Paper Statement Fee: $1.99 per billing cycle in which the balance is greater than $2.50 and a paper monthly statement is sent.
- Balance/interest calculation: daily balance method — interest computed by applying the daily periodic rate to the account's daily balance each day (APR x 1/365).
- Minimum Payment: not found in exact formula from a directly-fetched official source in this pass (see gap below).
