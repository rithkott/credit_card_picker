PayPal Cashback Mastercard (Synchrony Bank)
[Official Synchrony/PayPal legal documents cover two co-badged products under one agreement: "PayPal Cashback Mastercard®" and "PayPal Cashback World Mastercard®." This file documents the standard PayPal Cashback Mastercard; the World Mastercard variant shares the same rewards terms and appears to share the same rates/fees table, per the combined credit card agreement below. There was an older, differently-named predecessor product ("PayPal Extras Mastercard") with a flat 2% + eBay rewards structure, documented in a 2017/2021-dated CFPB-filed agreement — that structure has been superseded; do not use it. All facts below are drawn from documents dated January 2026 (account agreement) and rewards terms revised 3/2026, i.e. current as of this research pass (July 2026).]

Annual Fee
- None. Confirmed in the Rates and Fees Table of the official Synchrony Bank combined credit card agreement ("Annual Fee: None") and on the PayPal marketing page ("There is no annual fee with the PayPal Cashback Mastercard").

Signup/Welcome Bonus
- Not found on any official page checked (PayPal marketing page, PayPal help article, Synchrony terms pages). No sign-up/welcome bonus is disclosed anywhere in the official sources reviewed.

Earn Rates ("Cash Rewards" / "Cash Back")
- Requires an active PayPal account in good standing, linked to the Card Account, to earn or redeem rewards. Per PayPal: "You will need an active PayPal account in good standing to apply for and manage your PayPal Cashback Mastercard."
- 3%: "Each Eligible Purchase made by you through your PayPal account online, or when you send money to other PayPal users through your PayPal account will earn 3% Cash Rewards."
- 1.5%: "Each Eligible Purchase made by you everywhere else that Mastercard is accepted will earn 1.5% Cash Rewards."
- Authorized users: earn 1.5% on all Eligible Purchases (even PayPal ones), unless/until PayPal in the future allows an authorized user to add their own Card to their own PayPal account, at which point their PayPal-account purchases would earn 3% and other purchases 1.5%.
- No minimum spend required to earn Cash Rewards and no maximum/cap on Cash Rewards earned.
- "Eligible Purchases" = dollar amount of purchases of goods/services on the Card Account, plus amounts sent to another PayPal user via the Card Account through PayPal. Eligible Purchases do NOT include: balance transfers, cash advances, convenience checks, disputed transactions, unauthorized/fraudulent transactions, interest, fees, or any other transaction Synchrony determines in its sole discretion is ineligible.
- Cash Rewards do not expire, but may be forfeited (see "Caveats" below).
- Fractional cents round up at $0.005 or more, otherwise round down.
- "Bonus Cash Rewards" (extra % on certain purchases) may be offered from time to time under separate promotional terms; no specifics available generally (offer-specific).
- Historical note: an older Synchrony-filed agreement (PayPal Extras Mastercard, CFPB filing, "accurate as of" no later than 2017/2021 revision) described a flat "2% Cash Rewards on all Eligible Purchases" including at PayPal.com and eBay.com — this is a legacy/superseded structure under a different product name and should not be used for the current card.

Redemption
- Cash Rewards are made available for transfer to the Accountholder's PayPal account within approximately 3 days after each Eligible Purchase.
- Minimum to transfer: must have at least $0.01 in Cash Rewards available.
- To use: log in to PayPal account, view the Card Account Rewards Summary Page, and transfer the chosen amount to your PayPal account.
- If you have a PayPal Balance account: transferred Cash Rewards can be used to make payments/purchases, send money to other PayPal users, or be transferred to a linked bank account/debit card.
- If you do NOT have a PayPal Balance account: the only option is to transfer Cash Rewards to a linked bank account or debit card.
- Cash Rewards earned by authorized users may only be transferred to the primary Accountholder's PayPal account (not the authorized user's own account).
- Cash Rewards "have no cash value" until redeemed/transferred per the terms; the Program is described as promotional, with no consideration paid for participation.

Caveats / Forfeiture Conditions
- If the Card Account is delinquent (missed minimum payment): rewards earning/redemption may be paused starting the next billing cycle until the account is brought current; if minimum payment is missed for 2 consecutive billing cycles, Cash Rewards earning stops and any accumulated Cash Rewards are forfeited starting the 3rd cycle.
- If the Card Account is closed for any reason: Program participation terminates and any unredeemed Cash Rewards are forfeited.
- If the linked PayPal account is suspended: redemption is blocked until the PayPal account is reinstated (rewards keep accruing per terms, but can't be transferred).
- If the linked PayPal account is closed, or the Card Account becomes unlinked from the PayPal account: the Card Account will be closed and any unredeemed Cash Rewards are forfeited.
- New York residents: may redeem accumulated Cash Rewards for up to 90 days after Synchrony sends notice that the account is closed or the Program is modified/canceled/terminated — except rewards are still forfeited if the account was delinquent or closed for fraud/misuse.
- Synchrony reserves the right to add caps, modify eligible purchases, exclude transactions, add restrictions, or terminate the Program at any time in its sole discretion.

Foreign Transaction Fee
- 3% of each transaction (applies to purchases and cash advances made in a currency other than U.S. dollars, and/or in a country other than the U.S., "whether or not the transaction was in a foreign currency").

APR
- Purchase APR (set at account opening based on creditworthiness, then variable with Prime Rate), per the January 1, 2026 Rates and Fees Table:
  - Account Type 1: 18.49% (Prime + 11.74%)
  - Account Type 2: 30.24% (Prime + 23.49%)
  - Account Type 3: 33.24% (Prime + 26.49%)
  - "A limited number of applicants are expected to receive Account Types 1 and 2" (i.e., most approved applicants likely land in Type 3, the highest tier).
  - None of the purchase/cash-advance/penalty APRs will exceed 35.99% regardless of Prime Rate moves.
- APR for Cash Advances: 33.24% (Prime + 26.49%), variable, capped at 35.99%.
- Penalty APR: 34.24% (Prime + 27.49%), variable, capped at 35.99%. Applies if the total minimum payment due is not received by the due date 2 or more times during any 12 consecutive billing cycles. Once in effect, the Penalty APR "may remain in effect indefinitely," though Synchrony states it will periodically review the account for a possible reduction.
- Interest is calculated via a daily rate (APR ÷ 365) applied to the daily balance for each balance type (purchases, cash advances, etc.), by the "daily balance" method (see below).
- No interest on purchases if the entire balance is paid by the due date each billing cycle (standard grace period); cash advances always accrue interest from the transaction date, with no grace period.
- Minimum Interest Charge: no less than $2.00 in any billing cycle in which interest is owed.

Other Fees (per Synchrony Bank Credit Card Agreement, effective/accurate as of January 1, 2026):
- Balance Transfer Fee: Not disclosed/not found in the official Rates and Fees Table or Agreement text reviewed. Balance transfers are referenced only as a category of transaction excluded from earning Cash Rewards; no balance-transfer fee amount or availability statement was found. Flagged below as unconfirmed.
- Cash Advance Fee: Either $10.00 or 5% of the amount of each cash advance, whichever is greater.
- Foreign Transaction Fee: 3% of each transaction (see above).
- Late Payment Fee: $29 if the total minimum payment was paid by the due date in each of the prior 6 billing cycles; otherwise $40 if it was missed in any one or more of the prior 6 billing cycles. Fee will never exceed the total minimum payment that was due.
- Returned Payment Fee: $30 if no returned payment fee was charged in any of the prior 6 billing cycles; otherwise $41 if one was charged in any of the prior 6 billing cycles. Will not exceed the amount permitted by applicable law.
- Paper Statement Fee: $1.99 per month, charged in any billing cycle where the balance exceeds $2.50 and a paper statement is sent (even if also provided electronically). Avoidable by electing electronic-only statements.
- Minimum Payment: The greater of (a) $29, or $40 if the total minimum payment was missed in any of the prior 6 billing cycles (includes past-due amounts); or (b) the sum of: any past-due amount + 1% of the new balance (excluding certain special-promotion balances) + any late payment fees charged in the current cycle + all interest charged in the current cycle + any payment due on a special promotional purchase. Rounded up to the next whole dollar. Never more than the new balance.
- Balance calculation method: "Daily balance" — starting balance each day, plus new charges/fees, minus payments/credits; daily rate (APR/365) applied to each day's balance for each balance type (purchases, cash advances, etc.), then summed over the billing cycle.
- Special promotional financing (e.g., "No Interest if Paid in Full Within 6/12/18 Months") may be offered on certain purchases per separate promotional disclosures; if the promotional balance isn't paid in full within the promo period, interest is retroactively assessed from the purchase date at the standard Purchase APR.
