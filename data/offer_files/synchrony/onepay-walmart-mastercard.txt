OnePay CashRewards World Mastercard (issued by Synchrony Bank, in partnership with OnePay/One Finance, Inc. and Walmart Inc.)

RELATIONSHIP TO THE OTHER ONEPAY/WALMART CARD (read before using this file):
This program has TWO distinct card products, confirmed via official Synchrony Bank cardholder-agreement PDFs (same filing family, "FR833282333"):
  1. THIS FILE — OnePay CashRewards World Mastercard: open-loop, usable anywhere Mastercard is accepted, earns cash back.
  2. OnePay Walmart Spend Card — closed-loop/private-label, usable ONLY at Walmart, earns NO rewards. See sibling file: data/offer_files/synchrony/onepay-walmart.txt
Both are issued by Synchrony Bank; both require the applicant to have a OnePay account in good standing; both were announced together (June 9, 2025 press release) as a single "credit card program" with Walmart, going live "this fall" (fall 2025). The Walmart Spend Card is explicitly positioned (per secondary/press coverage) as the private-label option "for shoppers who don't qualify for the Mastercard version" — i.e., likely a lower-tier/soft-decline product within the same application funnel, not a separately marketed card line. Terms differ meaningfully (rewards vs. no rewards; APR structure has two account tiers on this card vs. one flat rate on the Spend Card; this card allows cash advances, the Spend Card does not), so they are documented separately per instructions.

Annual Fee
- $0 / no annual fee (confirmed on OnePay marketing page and help-center article).

Signup/Welcome Bonus
- OnePay marketing page (fetched during a promotional window) showed: "$50 cash back upon account opening," described as awarded as OnePay Points, redeemable as a statement credit or deposit into a OnePay Cash account, applicable to applications made 6/18-7/7 (a limited-time window, exact year of that window not separately confirmed against today's date of 2026-07-04 — flagged below as not confirmed current).
- NOT CONFIRMED as the currently-active, generally-available welcome offer as of this research date — the promo dates captured appear tied to a specific application window and may be a stale/cached promo rather than the live 2026 offer.

Earn Rates (OnePay Points / cash back)
- 5% cash back at Walmart for cardholders with a linked Walmart+ membership.
- 3% cash back at Walmart for cardholders without a linked Walmart+ membership.
- 1.5% cash back on all other purchases everywhere Mastercard is accepted.
- Earnings accrue as "OnePay Points"; a 25-point minimum redemption threshold applies (per onepay.com fetch).
- Redemption: as a statement credit or deposit into a OnePay Cash deposit account (other redemption options referenced as "available" but not itemized on pages fetched).

Foreign Transaction Fee
- None. Per the official Synchrony Account Agreement (Section II, "How Fees Work" -> "Foreign Transaction Fee"): "We will not charge a foreign transaction fee." Standard Mastercard currency-conversion-procedure disclosure follows (Mastercard converts non-USD transactions using its own currency conversion rate/procedure, based on wholesale-market or government-mandated rates, which may reflect the transaction date or, in delayed-processing cases, the processing date).

APR (per official Synchrony Rates and Fees Table, document dated 3/2026; "as of January 1, 2026" figures given for illustration)
- Two account tiers, assigned at account opening based on creditworthiness ("Account Type 1" and "Account Type 2"):
  - Account Type 1: Purchase APR = Prime Rate + 13.49% (illustrative APR as of 1/1/2026: 20.24%, daily rate .05546%). Cash Advance APR = Prime Rate + 15.49% (illustrative APR as of 1/1/2026: 22.24%, daily rate .06094%; cash-advance APR is capped and will not exceed 35.99%, daily rate cap .09861%).
  - Account Type 2: Purchase APR = Prime Rate + 23.99% (illustrative APR as of 1/1/2026: 30.74%, daily rate .08422%). Cash Advance APR = Prime Rate + 25.49% (illustrative APR as of 1/1/2026: 32.24%, daily rate .08833%; same 35.99% cap applies).
- Prime rate is the highest bank prime loan rate published in The Wall Street Journal's Money Rates section on the last business day of the calendar month preceding the billing cycle. Rate changes apply to the entire account balance, effective the first day of the first billing cycle after the change.
- Grace period: due date is at least 23 days after the close of each billing cycle; no interest charged on purchases if the entire balance is paid by the due date each month. Interest on cash advances begins accruing on the transaction date (no grace period for cash advances).
- Minimum Interest Charge: no less than $2.00 if interest is charged.

Other Fees (per official Synchrony Account Agreement, dated 3/2026)
- Cash Advance Fee: $10 or 5% of the amount of each cash advance, whichever is greater (in addition to any ATM-owner fee for ATM cash advances).
- Late Payment Fee: $30 if the total minimum payment was paid on time in each of the prior six billing cycles; $41 if not. Never more than the total minimum payment that was due. (Note: the Section I summary table states "up to $41"; Section II gives the full $30/$41 tiered structure — both are from the same document, reproduced here for completeness.)
- Returned Payment Fee: $30 if no returned-payment fee occurred in the prior six billing cycles; $41 if one did occur in that period. Never more than the amount permitted by applicable law. May be charged even if the payment is honored on a subsequent representment.
- Minimum Payment: greater of (a) $30 (or $41 including past-due amounts, if the total minimum payment was missed by the due date in any of the prior six billing cycles), or (b) past-due amounts + 1% of new balance (excluding special-promotion balances) + late fees charged in the current cycle + interest charged in the current cycle + any special-promotion payment due. Rounded up to the next whole dollar; never more than the new balance.
- Balance calculation method: "daily balance" — interest is calculated separately per balance type (purchases, cash advances, and any special-promotion balances) by applying the daily rate to each day's balance; late/returned-payment fees and debt-cancellation fees are treated as new purchases for balance purposes; cash advance fees are added to the cash advance balance.

Account/Program Structure Notes (from the official Account Agreement)
- Governing law: federal law and, where state law applies, the laws of Utah (agreement "accepted by us in Utah"). Synchrony Bank address of record: 170 Election Road, Suite 125, Draper, UT 84020.
- "OnePay Account" clause: cardholders are not required to have this card to have a OnePay account, but must have a OnePay account in good standing to apply for and maintain this card account. If the linked OnePay account is closed for any reason, this card account will also be closed — i.e., the credit card is contractually dependent on the OnePay fintech account, not a freestanding bank relationship.
- Information sharing: applicant information is shared between Synchrony Bank, "One Finance, Inc." (OnePay's legal entity name), and Walmart Inc., for account administration and special-benefits offers.
- Standard binding arbitration clause (with 45-day opt-out right), standard Synchrony collections/credit-bureau-reporting/default provisions — no product-differentiating terms of note beyond what's captured above.
- Card usable "for purchases from Walmart, or from any merchant that accepts Mastercard credit cards" (open-loop) plus cash advances via convenience checks or ATM.
