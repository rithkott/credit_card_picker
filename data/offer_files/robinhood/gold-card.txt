Robinhood Gold Card (Robinhood)

Eligibility Requirement
- Requires an active Robinhood Gold membership. Robinhood Gold costs $5/month or $50/year (Robinhood Gold offers a first-30-days-free trial for new Gold members, but the Gold Card's elevated 3x earn rate requires an active, ongoing paid Gold subscription with no 30-day free trial applicable to the card itself).
- Requires an individual Robinhood investing/brokerage account. Only available to the primary cardholder — not open to authorized users (authorized users can be added to the account but the elevated rewards structure is tied to the primary cardholder's Gold membership).
- IMPORTANT for portfolio optimization purposes: a cardholder who is not already paying for (or willing to pay for) Robinhood Gold membership cannot obtain the card's advertised earn rate economically, since the membership cost is a separate, mandatory ongoing expense on top of the card itself.

Availability / Waitlist
- The card has had a phased, invite-only rollout since its 2024 launch. As of mid-2026, it remains a waitlist/invite process for new applicants (via robinhood.com/creditcard/waitlist) rather than fully open enrollment, though reported wait times have shortened significantly since the initial launch. Applicants are notified by email/in-app message when invited to apply.

Signup Bonus
- No signup/welcome bonus found on official Robinhood pages as of this research.

Earn Rates
- 3 points/$1 (equivalent to 3% cash back) on all eligible purchases, contingent on an active Robinhood Gold subscription.
- Up to 5 points/$1 on purchases made through Robinhood's travel portal (per third-party reporting; not independently confirmed on the primary official Robinhood card page during this research — NEEDS verification).

Annual Fee
- $0 (no annual fee on the card itself). Note: the $5/month or $50/year Robinhood Gold membership fee is a separate, mandatory cost to access the card's rewards rate, distinct from a traditional card annual fee.

Foreign Transaction Fee
- No foreign transaction fee (per official Robinhood card terms).

Redemption
- Points redeem at $0.01 per point when transferred as cash directly into the cardholder's Robinhood investing (brokerage) account.
- Cash back transferred into the Robinhood investing account can earn additional yield (Gold members enrolled in Robinhood's High-Yield Cash Program were advertised at 3.35% APY on that transferred cash as of this research; rate is variable and subject to change).
- Points redeemed for statement credit instead are worth less than $0.01 per point (exact statement-credit rate not confirmed — NEEDS verification).
- All cash back point redemptions are non-reversible.
- Full terms are governed by the Robinhood Gold Card Rewards Program Rules document, which is subject to change; a readable copy could not be extracted during this research (PDF was binary/compressed) — recommend a human confirm current program rules directly.
