EARN RATES
- 5X points on stays at participating Choice Hotels® properties and Choice Privileges point purchases.

- 1X points on other purchases.

SIGNUP BONUS
- 60,000 bonus points when you spend $1,000 in purchases in the first 3 months — redeemable for up to 7 reward nights at select Choice Hotels® properties. FEES (RATES & FEES DISCLOSURE, from agreement page)
- Annual Fee: None
- APR for Purchases: Prime Rate + 12.99% to Prime Rate + 21.74%, variable, based on creditworthiness.
- APR for Balance Transfers: Prime Rate + 12.99% to Prime Rate + 21.74%, variable, based on creditworthiness.
- APR for Cash Advances: Prime Rate + 22.74%, variable.

- Minimum Interest Charge: If charged, no less than $1.00.
