RATES AND FEES DISCLOSURE (Schumer box, from agreement page)

Interest Rates and Interest Charges
- APR for Purchases: "Prime Rate + 11.74% to Prime Rate + 21.74%, based on your creditworthiness." Variable, tied to U.S. Prime Rate.
- APR for Balance Transfers: "Prime Rate + 11.74% to Prime Rate + 21.74%, based on your creditworthiness." Variable, tied to U.S. Prime Rate.
- APR for Cash Advances & Overdraft Protection: "Prime Rate + 22.74%" Variable, tied to U.S. Prime Rate.
- Grace Period: "Your due date is at least 25 days after the close of each billing period." No interest charged on purchases if full balance paid by due date.
- Minimum Interest Charge: "$1.00" if interest is assessed.
- Balance Calculation Method: "Average daily balance (including new purchases)"

Fees
- Annual Fee: "None"
- Balance Transfer Fee: "Up to 5% for each balance transfer, with a minimum of $5"
- Cash Advance Fee: "Either $10 or 5% of the amount of each advance, whichever is greater"
- Foreign Transaction Fee: "3% of each transaction converted to U.S. dollars"
- Late Payment Fee: "Up to $40" (calculated as lower of $40 or Minimum Payment amount)

GUIDE TO BENEFITS (effective as of 09/15/2025, replaces all prior benefit disclosures)

- Cellular Telephone Protection: covers repair/replacement of damaged, stolen, or lost phones; requires paying monthly wireless bill with the covered card; $25 deductible per claim; maximum $600 per claim; limited to 2 claims per 12-month period ($1,200 max annually); cracked screens and cosmetic damage not covered.
- Emergency Cash & Card Replacement: available 24/7; Visa Global Customer Care "1-800-708-8217" or "1-303-967-1096"; may be available within 24 hours in some cases.
- Auto Rental Collision Damage Waiver: covers accidents/theft on rental cars paid with the card; maximum 15 consecutive days in US, 31 days internationally; $50,000 maximum coverage; does not cover exotic vehicles, antiques, or vans (except small group transport).
- Travel & Emergency Assistance Services: 24/7 support for medical, legal, and transportation emergencies; includes prescription assistance and document delivery coordination; "1-800-992-6029" or collect "1-804-673-1675".
- Roadside Dispatch: pay-per-use roadside assistance (no membership required); towing up to 5 miles, jump starts, lockouts, tire changes; "1-800-847-2869".
- Visa Signature Benefits: complimentary concierge services; access to Luxury Hotel Collection with room upgrades and amenities.
- Credit Close-Up® — complimentary FICO® Score monitoring and personalized credit insights for Wells Fargo Online customers.
- Zero Liability Protection for unauthorized transactions.
