Wells Fargo Active Cash® Card
Source pages: https://creditcards.wellsfargo.com/active-cash-credit-card/ ; https://www.wellsfargo.com/credit-cards/agreements/active-cash-agreement/ ; https://www.wellsfargo.com/credit-cards/active-cash/guide-to-benefits/
Captured: 2026-07-04

EARN RATES
- Unlimited 2% cash rewards on purchases, no categories to track.
- Rewards don't expire as long as the account remains open.
- Redemption: Pay with Rewards through PayPal, redeem toward past purchases (minimum $1), convert to gift cards, or apply as account credits.

SIGNUP BONUS
- $200 cash rewards bonus when you spend $500 in purchases in the first 3 months.

FEES
- Annual Fee: None
- APR for Purchases & Balance Transfers: Prime Rate + 11.74% to Prime Rate + 21.74%, variable, based on creditworthiness. (Product page states resulting range as 18.49%, 24.49%, or 28.49% variable.)
- APR for Cash Advances & Overdraft Protection: Prime Rate + 22.74%, variable.
- Intro APR: 0% for 12 months from account opening on purchases and qualifying balance transfers.
- Balance Transfer Fee: Up to 5% for each balance transfer, minimum $5.
- Cash Advance Fee: Either $10 or 5% of the amount of each advance, whichever is greater.
- Foreign Transaction Fee: 3% of each transaction converted to U.S. dollars.
- Late Payment Fee: Up to $40 (lower of $40 or Minimum Payment amount).
- Minimum Interest Charge: $1.00 if interest is assessed.
- Balance Calculation Method: Average daily balance (including new purchases).
- Grace Period: due date at least 25 days after close of billing period; no interest on purchases if paid in full by due date.

NOTABLE PERKS (Guide to Benefits, effective 09/15/2025, replaces all prior benefit disclosures)
- Cellular Telephone Protection: covers repair/replacement of damaged, stolen, or lost phones; requires paying monthly wireless bill with the covered card; $25 deductible per claim; maximum $600 per claim; limited to 2 claims per 12-month period ($1,200 max annually); cracked screens and cosmetic damage not covered.
- Auto Rental Collision Damage Waiver: covers accidents/theft on rental cars paid with the card; maximum 15 consecutive days in US, 31 days internationally; $50,000 maximum coverage; excludes exotic vehicles, antiques, vans (except small group transport).
- Emergency Cash & Card Replacement: available 24/7, sometimes within 24 hours.
- Travel & Emergency Assistance Services: 24/7 support for medical, legal, transportation emergencies; prescription assistance and document delivery coordination.
- Roadside Dispatch: pay-per-use (no membership required); towing up to 5 miles, jump starts, lockouts, tire changes.
- Visa Signature Benefits: complimentary concierge services; Luxury Hotel Collection with room upgrades and amenities.
- Credit Close-Up®: complimentary FICO® Score monitoring and credit insights for Wells Fargo Online customers.
- Zero Liability Protection for unauthorized transactions.
