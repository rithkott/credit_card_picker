Wells Fargo Reflect® Visa Credit Card
Source pages: https://creditcards.wellsfargo.com/reflect-visa-credit-card ; https://www.wellsfargo.com/credit-cards/reflect-visa/terms/ ; https://www.wellsfargo.com/credit-cards/reflect-visa/guide-to-benefits/
Captured: 2026-07-04

EARN RATES
- No rewards program on this card (it is a 0% APR / balance-transfer focused product).

FEES (RATES & FEES DISCLOSURE, from terms page)
- Annual Fee: None
- APR for Purchases: 0% introductory APR for 21 months from date of account opening. After that, 17.49%, 23.99%, or 28.24% variable.
- APR for Balance Transfers: 0% introductory APR for 21 months from date of account opening (balance transfers must be made within 120 days from account opening to qualify; up to 14 days to post a transfer). After that, 17.49%, 23.99%, or 28.24% variable.
- APR for Cash Advances: 29.49%.
- Balance Transfer Fee: 5% for each balance transfer, minimum $5.
- Cash Advance Fee: Either $10 or 5% of the amount of each advance, whichever is greater.
- Foreign Transaction Fee: 3% of each transaction converted to U.S. dollars.
- Late Payment Fee: Up to $40.
- Minimum Interest Charge: If charged, no less than $1.00.
- No grace period on balance transfers — interest accrues immediately upon posting (per product page); standard purchase grace period applies if paid in full by due date.
- Eligibility restriction (from product page text): may not be eligible for introductory APR/fees/rewards bonus offers if the applicant has the Wells Fargo Reflect Visa® or opened one within the last 48 months from the date of application.

NOTABLE PERKS (guide to benefits / product page)
- Cellular Telephone Protection: up to $600 coverage against damage/theft with $25 deductible (requires paying monthly wireless bill with card).
- Roadside Dispatch®: 24/7 pay-per-use emergency towing assistance, no membership required.
- My Wells Fargo Deals: personalized merchant offers with cash back.
- Zero Liability Protection: fraud protection for unauthorized transactions.
- Credit Close-Up®: complimentary FICO® Score monitoring and credit insights.
