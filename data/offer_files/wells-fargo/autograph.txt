EARN RATES
- Unlimited 3X points: restaurants (dining in, takeout, delivery, catering); travel (airfare, hotels, car rentals, cruises); gas stations and EV charging; transit (subways, rideshare, parking, tolls); popular streaming services; phone plans.
- 1X point on all other purchases.
- No earning limit stated.
- Transfer partners (per secondary sources, transfer program launched April 4, 2024): Choice Privileges, Air France-KLM Flying Blue, Avianca LifeMiles, British Airways Executive Club, AerClub, Iberia Plus.

SIGNUP BONUS
- 20,000 bonus points when you spend $1,000 in purchases in the first 3 months — stated value "$200 cash redemption."

FEES
- Annual Fee: $0
- APR: 0% intro APR for 12 months from account opening on purchases. 18.49%, 24.49%, or 28.49% variable APR thereafter.
- No foreign transaction fees.
- Precise fee figures (balance transfer fee, cash advance fee, late fee) were not independently re-confirmed from the official disclosure page for this card in this pass — assume parity with the Active Cash card's Schumer box (same issuer family, same APR tiers) pending direct verification: balance transfer fee up to 5% (min $5), cash advance fee greater of $10 or 5%, late fee up to $40.

NOTABLE PERKS (product page + guide to benefits)
- Cellular Telephone Protection (up to $600 with $25 deductible; requires paying monthly wireless bill with card).
- Auto Rental Collision Damage Waiver.
- Visa Signature® Concierge Services.
- Visa Signature® Luxury Hotel Collection (room upgrades, amenities).
- Travel and Emergency Assistance Services.
- Roadside Dispatch® (24/7, no membership required, pay-per-use).
- Credit Close-Up® — complimentary FICO® Score monitoring.
- My Wells Fargo Deals — personalized cash-back merchant offers.
- Zero Liability Protection.
- Redemption: Pay with Rewards via PayPal, redeem for purchases, gift cards, account credits, or transfer points to travel loyalty programs.
