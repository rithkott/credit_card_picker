Wells Fargo Autograph Journey℠ Visa® Card
Source pages: https://www.wellsfargo.com/credit-cards/autograph-journey-visa/terms/ ; https://www.wellsfargo.com/credit-cards/autograph-journey-visa/guide-to-benefits/
Captured: 2026-07-04

EARN RATES (terms page + secondary corroboration)
- Base: 1 Rewards Point per $1 on net purchases.
- Bonus categories (per official product marketing, corroborated by CNBC Select and The Points Guy): unlimited 5X points on hotels, 4X points on airlines, 3X points on other travel and restaurants, 1X points on other purchases.
- Transfer partners (per secondary sources: CNBC, NerdWallet): Choice Privileges, Air France-KLM Flying Blue, Avianca LifeMiles, British Airways Executive Club, AerClub, Iberia Plus, JetBlue, Virgin Atlantic (partner list per multiple outlets — not independently re-verified against an official partner list page in this pass).

SIGNUP BONUS
- 60,000 Rewards Points bonus when cardholder makes at least $4,000 in net purchases that post to the account within 3 months from the date the account is opened.

CREDITS
- $50 statement credit each year for an airline purchase of at least $50, automatically applied.

FEES
- Annual Fee: $95
- APR for Purchases & Balance Transfers: 19.49%, 25.49%, or 28.49%, based on creditworthiness. Variable, tied to U.S. Prime Rate.
- APR for Cash Advances & Overdraft Protection: 29.49% variable.
- No introductory APR offer mentioned for this card.
- Balance Transfer Fee: introductory fee of $5 or 3% of the amount of each transfer, whichever is greater, for 120 days; up to 5% thereafter.
- Cash Advance Fee: Either $10 or 5% of the amount of each advance, whichever is greater.
- Foreign Transaction Fee: None.
- Late Payment Penalty: Up to $40.

NOTABLE PERKS (Guide to Benefits)
- Cellular Telephone Protection: damage, theft, or accidental loss of phones on the account's wireless bill; $25 deductible; $1,000 maximum per claim; must pay monthly wireless bill with card; max 2 claims per 12 months, up to $2,000 annually.
- Visa Signature® Concierge Services: 24/7 assistance booking travel, events, reservations.
- Luxury Hotel Collection: best available rate guarantee, automatic room upgrades, complimentary breakfast, late checkout when booking through designated sites.
- Emergency Cash & Card Replacement: within 1-3 business days, sometimes within 24 hours.
- Travel & Emergency Assistance: 24/7 — medical referrals, legal assistance, emergency transportation, prescription help while traveling.
- Auto Rental Collision Damage Waiver: covers rental car damage up to $50,000 for accidents/theft when declining rental company's insurance; 15 consecutive days US, 31 days international.
- Lost Baggage Reimbursement: up to $3,000 per trip for baggage lost by common carriers.
- Roadside Dispatch®: pay-per-use, towing (5 miles), tire changes, lockouts, fuel delivery.
- Trip Cancellation/Interruption Protection: reimburses up to $15,000 per traveler for covered cancellations (illness, severe weather, terrorism, other specified reasons).
- $1,000,000 Travel Accident Insurance: covers accidental loss of life, limbs, sight, or speech during covered trips.

- "Wells Fargo Premier Autograph Visa Infinite Card" — offered to Wells Fargo Premier banking relationship clients (reported threshold ~$250k+ in assets).
- "Private Bank By Invitation Visa Signature Card" — offered to Wells Fargo Private Bank clients (invitation-only, asset threshold unspecified in sources found).
- Multiple sources (Frequent Miler, Doctor of Credit) describe these as sharing the same core rewards structure and benefits as the standard Autograph Journey Visa Signature card, distinguished primarily by: different card design/metal, Visa Infinite-tier benefit set (vs. Visa Signature for the base card) which may include enhanced travel protections, and a reported higher (~100,000 point) welcome offer for the Premier variant seen via in-branch/relationship-manager offers, per Frequent Miler ("100K Wells Fargo Autograph Journey offer for Premier members, in-branch only") and Upgraded Points coverage.
