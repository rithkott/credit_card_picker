Wells Fargo Choice Privileges® Select Mastercard® (Choice Privileges® Select World Elite Mastercard®)
Source pages: https://creditcards.wellsfargo.com/choice-hotels-privileges-select-mastercard ; https://www.wellsfargo.com/credit-cards/choice-select/guide-to-benefits/
Captured: 2026-07-04

MARKETING / PRODUCT PAGE CONTENT

- Welcome Bonus: "Earn 60,000 bonus points when you spend $3,000 in purchases in the first 3 months" — redeemable for up to 7 reward nights at select Choice Hotels® properties.
- Annual Fee: "$95"
- Rewards Rates:
  - 10X points on stays at participating Choice Hotels® properties and Choice Privileges point purchases.
  - 5X points on gas stations, grocery stores, home improvement stores, and phone plan services.
  - 1X points on other purchases.
- Key Benefits:
  - 30,000 bonus points every account anniversary year (worth approximately 3 reward nights per product page framing).
  - Automatic Choice Hotels Platinum Elite membership with 25% bonus points on qualifying stays.
  - 20 Elite Qualifying Nights credited annually.
  - Up to $120 statement credit for Trusted Traveler Program fees (Global Entry/TSA PreCheck) every 4 years.
  - No foreign transaction fees.
  - Cell phone protection up to $800 ($25 deductible).
  - Mastercard World Elite benefits.
- Notable restriction (per product page): not eligible if cardholder opened a Choice Privileges card within the last 15 months, or currently holds an open Choice Privileges account.

RATES & FEES (corroborated via WebSearch — a direct official agreement/terms page for this specific card was not isolated in this pass; figures below are cross-corroborated across NerdWallet, U.S. News, and BestCards.com and should be re-verified against an official wellsfargo.com/credit-cards/agreements/ page during the curation/verification pass)

- Regular Purchase APR: 20.99%, 25.24%, or 29.99% variable, based on creditworthiness and the Prime Rate.
- Balance Transfer APR: 20.99%, 25.24%, or 29.99% variable, based on creditworthiness and the Prime Rate.
- Cash Advance APR: 29.99% variable, based on the Prime Rate.
- No introductory APR offer for balance transfers or purchases.
- Balance Transfer Transaction Fee: either $5 or 5% of the amount of each transfer, whichever is greater.
- Cash Advance Transaction Fee: either $10 or 5% of the amount of each cash advance, whichever is greater.
- Foreign Transaction Fee: $0.

GUIDE TO BENEFITS (Choice Privileges Select World Elite Mastercard)

- Cellular Telephone Protection: $25 per claim deductible; up to $800 per claim for repair/replacement; maximum of 2 paid claims per 12-month period; $1,600 per 12-month period coverage limit.
- Mastercard ID Theft Protection: no charge, provided by financial institution; monitors compromised credentials, high-risk transactions, dark web activity; includes credit file monitoring and resolution services.
- Auto Rental Collision Damage Waiver: $50,000 maximum coverage; 15 consecutive days in the US, 31 consecutive days outside.
- Trip Cancellation and Interruption Protection: $5,000 maximum per covered traveler per trip; $20,000 annual account limit.
- Lost Baggage Reimbursement: $3,000 in total for all covered travelers per trip (New York residents: $2,000 per bag limit).
- Travel Accident Insurance: $1,000,000 worldwide coverage.
- Trusted Traveler Program Credit: up to $120 statement credit every four years for application fees.

Note: the guide-to-benefits page did not itself disclose annual fee, welcome bonus, or rewards rate figures — those came from the product marketing page. The $95 annual fee and 60,000/$3,000-in-3-months welcome offer above are corroborated by NerdWallet and Savings Grove coverage in addition to the direct product-page fetch.
