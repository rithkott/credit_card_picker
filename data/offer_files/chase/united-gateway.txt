United Gateway (Chase)

May be issued as Visa Signature or Visa Platinum depending on underwriting.

Signup Bonus
- 30,000 bonus miles after spending $1,000 in first 3 months from account opening. Allow 6-8 weeks to post. Miles don't count toward Premier status; account must stay open 6+ months or miles may be clawed back.
- 10,000 bonus miles after adding an authorized user in first 3 months (once per account, regardless of how many users added).
- Not available to current cardmembers, or previous cardmembers who received a bonus for this card within the last 24 months.

Earn Rates
- 1 mile/$1 base rate.
- 2 miles/$1 total: airline tickets and other purchases (seat upgrades, Economy Plus, inflight food/beverage/WiFi, baggage/other fees) made directly with United (excludes United Cruises, MileagePlus X, DIRECTV, Merchandise Awards, and tickets via discount travel sites/third-party packages); United Card Events from Chase; gas stations; local transit/commuting.
- Miles auto-transfer to MileagePlus after each billing cycle.

Notable Perks
- Award Flight Pricing: 10% fewer miles (15% for Premier members) on United/United Express award flights vs. base pricing; requires $10,000 calendar-year card spend to activate (~4-5 week activation delay), valid through end of following calendar year. Doesn't apply to Money+Miles or cash tickets.
- Two checked bag certificates/year after $10,000 calendar-year spend (United/United Express-operated flights only, not codeshare); expire 12 months from issue.
- 25% back (statement credit) on inflight food/beverage/WiFi purchases on United/United Express flights, and on premium drinks at United Clubs.
- Concierge service (cardmember pays for goods/services booked through it).

Issued by JPMorgan Chase Bank, N.A.
