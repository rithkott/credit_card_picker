United Quest (Chase)

Signup Bonus
- 60,000 bonus miles after spending $4,000 in first 3 months from account opening. Allow 6-8 weeks to post. Miles don't count toward Premier status.
- 500 additional PQP after spending $4,000 in first 3 months (doesn't count toward the calendar-year PQP cap).
- 10,000 bonus miles after adding an authorized user in first 3 months (once per account).
- Not available to current cardmembers, or previous cardmembers who received a bonus for this card within the last 24 months.

Earn Rates
- 1 mile/$1 base rate.
- 2 miles/$1: dining (incl. takeout/delivery); select streaming services; travel category (excludes United purchases earning 4x).
- 4 miles/$1: airline tickets and other purchases (seat upgrades, Economy Plus, inflight food/beverage/WiFi, baggage/other fees) made directly with United; United Card Events from Chase.
- 5 miles/$1: hotel purchases at Renowned Hotels and Resorts for United Cardmembers via Chase Travel.
- Miles auto-transfer to MileagePlus after each billing cycle.

Notable Perks
- First and second checked bags free (primary cardmember + 1 companion, United/United Express-operated flights only).
- 10,000-mile anniversary award flight discount deposited each cardmember anniversary (expires 12 months from issue).
- One additional 10,000-mile award flight discount per calendar year after $20,000 net spend.
- 2 global Economy Plus upgrade credits per calendar year after $40,000 net spend (expire 12 months from issue).
- 1 PQP per $20 net spend, up to 18,000 PQP/calendar year; plus 1,000 Card Bonus PQP each year (posted ~Feb 1, doesn't count toward the cap).
- Priority boarding (primary + companions on same reservation, United/United Express-operated flights only).
- Global Entry/TSA PreCheck/NEXUS application fee credit: one statement credit up to $120 every 4 years.
- 25% back (statement credit) on inflight food/beverage/WiFi and United Club premium drinks.
- $200 annual United TravelBank cash credit, auto-added at cardmember anniversary (expires per TravelBank terms).
- Up to $150/year Renowned Hotels and Resorts statement credit.
- Up to $100/year rideshare statement credit ($8/month Jan-Nov, $12 in December).
- Up to $80/year Avis/Budget statement credit ($40 each on first two qualifying rentals via cars.united.com with discount codes).
- $150/year JSX statement credit.
- Complimentary Premier Upgrades on award tickets for Premier-status primary cardmember + 1 companion.
- 3 months complimentary Instacart+ (activated 3/23/2025-12/31/2027), then 50% off annual rate through 12/31/2027, then full price; $10 + $5 monthly Instacart credits while active.

Issued by JPMorgan Chase Bank, N.A.
