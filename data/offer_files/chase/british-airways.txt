British Airways Visa Signature (Chase)

Signup Bonus
- 75,000 bonus Avios after spending $5,000 in first 3 months from account opening. Allow 6-8 weeks to post.
- Not available to current cardmembers, or previous cardmembers who received a bonus for this card within the last 24 months.

Earn Rates
- 3 Avios/$1 on flights purchased directly with British Airways, Aer Lingus, Iberia, LEVEL.
- 2 Avios/$1 on hotel accommodations purchased directly with the hotel.
- 1 Avios/$1 on everything else.
- Avios auto-transfer to British Airways Club after each billing cycle (requires US address on BA Club account).

Notable Perks
- 10% discount on British Airways mainline flights (US to London and beyond), booked via ba.com/Chase10; total fare including taxes/fees discounted; up to 7 additional passengers on the same booking.
- Reward Flight Statement Credit: up to $200/booking (taxes/fees) when redeeming Avios for a BA Reward Flight, originating in the US, on BA metal (may include codeshare partner for non-transatlantic segment). $100 for economy/premium economy, $200 for business/First. Max 3 bookings/$600 per calendar year.
- Travel Together Ticket: spend $30,000 in a calendar year → voucher for a free companion seat (or 50% off Avios as solo traveler) on BA/Iberia/Aer Lingus Reward Flights; taxes/fees still apply for companion. One per calendar year, valid 2 years.
- Paying with Avios: can use Avios to reduce cash taxes/fees on reward bookings down to $5 minimum, via BA Club Contact Centre phone bookings only (not ba.com).
- Complimentary DashPass (DoorDash/Caviar): 12 months free when activated 2/1/2025-12/31/2027, then auto-renews at then-current rate. $10 off quarterly on one DoorDash non-restaurant order while enrolled (no rollover).
- Access to Oneworld alliance partner redemptions (Alaska, American, Aer Lingus, Cathay Pacific, Finnair, Iberia, Japan Airlines, LEVEL, Malaysia, Qantas, Qatar, Royal Jordanian, Royal Air Maroc).

Points Notes
- Avios don't expire with British Airways Club program activity every 36 months. Avios earned on this card don't count toward BA Club tier progression/retention.
