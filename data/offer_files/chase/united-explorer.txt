United Explorer (Chase)

Signup Bonus
- 50,000 bonus miles after spending $3,000 in first 3 months from account opening. Allow 6-8 weeks to post. Miles don't count toward Premier status.
- 10,000 bonus miles after adding an authorized user in first 3 months (once per account).
- Not available to current cardmembers, or previous cardmembers who received a bonus for this card within the last 24 months.

Earn Rates
- 1 mile/$1 base rate.
- 2 miles/$1: dining (incl. takeout/delivery); hotel accommodations purchased directly with the hotel.
- 3 miles/$1: airline tickets and other purchases (seat upgrades, Economy Plus, inflight food/beverage/WiFi, baggage/other fees) made directly with United (excludes United Cruises, MileagePlus X, DIRECTV, Merchandise Awards, discount-site/third-party-package tickets); United Card Events from Chase.
- Miles auto-transfer to MileagePlus after each billing cycle.

Notable Perks
- Award Flight Pricing: 10% fewer miles (15% for Premier members) on United/United Express awards; ~5-week activation delay after card issued.
- First checked bag free (primary cardmember + 1 companion on same reservation; United/United Express-operated flights only).
- Two one-time United Club passes deposited annually (account open + every 12 months); each grants one-time lounge access.
- Priority boarding (primary + companions on same reservation, United/United Express-operated flights only).
- 1 Premier Qualifying Point (PQP) per $20 net spend, up to 1,000 PQP/calendar year (counts toward Premier status tiers up to 1K).
- Global Entry/TSA PreCheck/NEXUS application fee credit: one statement credit up to $120 every 4 years.
- 25% back (statement credit) on inflight food/beverage/WiFi purchases on United/United Express flights, and premium drinks at United Clubs (also covers authorized users).
- $100 annual TravelBank cash credit after $10,000 calendar-year spend (once/year); TravelBank cash expires 12 months after issue.
- Up to $100/year United Hotels statement credit ($50 each on first two prepaid $50+ stays via "Pay Now").
- Up to $60/year rideshare statement credit ($5/month, enrollment required, through 9/30/2027... check current end date).
- Up to $50/year Avis/Budget statement credit ($25 each on first two qualifying rentals via cars.united.com with discount codes).
- $100/year JSX statement credit.
- One 10,000-mile award flight discount per calendar year after $20,000 net spend (expires 12 months from issue).
- 3 months complimentary Instacart+ (activated 3/23/2025-12/31/2027), then 25% off annual rate through 12/31/2027, then full price.
