United Club (Chase)

Signup Bonus
- 80,000 bonus miles after spending $5,000 in first 3 months from account opening. Allow 6-8 weeks to post. Miles don't count toward Premier status.
- 10,000 bonus miles after adding an authorized user in first 3 months (once per account).
- Not available to current cardmembers of any United Club card, or previous cardmembers who received a bonus within the last 24 months.

Earn Rates
- 1 mile/$1 base rate.
- 2 miles/$1: dining (incl. takeout/delivery); travel category (excludes United purchases earning 5x).
- 5 miles/$1: airline tickets and other purchases (seat upgrades, Economy Plus, inflight food/beverage/WiFi, baggage/other fees) made directly with United; United Card Events from Chase; hotel purchases at Renowned Hotels and Resorts for United Cardmembers via Chase Travel.
- Miles auto-transfer to MileagePlus after each billing cycle.

Notable Perks
- United Club membership: primary cardmember + 1 adult guest (or dependent children) per visit; doesn't include Star Alliance lounges. Effective 8/1/2025, spending $50,000+/calendar year or earning Premier Gold+ status upgrades to United Club All Access (2 guests/visit, includes Star Alliance lounge access) for remainder of that year + following year.
- 4 United Club one-time passes per cardmember anniversary if holding All Access membership + an authorized user.
- First and second checked bags free (primary cardmember + 1 companion, United/United Express-operated flights only).
- Premier Access (priority check-in/security/boarding/baggage) for primary + companions on same reservation.
- 1 PQP per $15 net spend, up to 28,000 PQP/calendar year; plus 1,500 Card Bonus PQP each year (posted ~Feb 1, doesn't count toward the cap).
- Global Entry/TSA PreCheck/NEXUS application fee credit: one statement credit up to $120 every 4 years.
- 25% back (statement credit) on inflight food/beverage/WiFi and United Club premium drinks (also covers authorized users).
- Up to $200/year Renowned Hotels and Resorts statement credit ($200 max, prepaid via Chase Travel).
- Up to $150/year rideshare statement credit ($12/month Jan-Nov, $18 in December).
- Avis President's Club membership (enrollment required).
- Up to $100/year Avis/Budget statement credit ($50 each on first two qualifying rentals via cars.united.com with discount codes).
- $200/year JSX statement credit.
- Up to two 10,000-mile award flight discounts per calendar year (one per $20,000 spend, max $40,000 net spend); each expires 12 months from issue.
- Complimentary Premier Upgrades on award tickets for Premier-status primary cardmember + 1 companion (subject to availability).
- 10% Saver Award Discount on Economy Saver awards (fare classes X/XN) within continental US or US-Canada.
- IHG One Rewards Platinum Elite status (registration required).
- 2 months (through 12/31/2027 or 1-year minimum) complimentary Instacart+, then $99/year auto-renewal; two $10/month Instacart credits while active.

Issued by JPMorgan Chase Bank, N.A.
