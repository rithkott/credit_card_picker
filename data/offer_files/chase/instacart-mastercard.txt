Instacart Mastercard (Chase)

Signup Bonus
- Instacart Credit awarded automatically upon approval (only if this Instacart account hasn't previously received an Instacart new-cardmember bonus, e.g. Instacart Credit or free Instacart+). Available immediately to within 10 days.
- Not eligible if you already hold this card or received a cash-back new cardmember bonus in the past 24 months.
- Instacart credit cannot be applied to: restaurant orders on Instacart powered by Uber Eats, alcohol, gift cards, prescriptions, fees/taxes/tips.

Earn Rates
- 5% Cash Back on qualifying Instacart.com/app purchases (incl. Instacart memberships/subscriptions), on the first $6,000 spent annually (effective 4/30/2025 onward: 4% additional on top of 1% base, capped at $6k/year); 1% back after cap, no maximum. (Through 4/29/2025 it was uncapped 5%.)
- 5% Cash Back through Chase Travel portal.
- 2% Cash Back: gas stations; dining (incl. Instacart-powered-by-Uber-Eats restaurant orders, takeout, delivery); select streaming services.
- 1% Cash Back: everything else.
- Points: 1 point = $0.01 (100 points = $1 back).

Redemption
- Pay with Points at Instacart checkout (1 point = $0.01), or redeem for cash, gift cards, travel, products. Excludes Uber Eats restaurant orders, Instacart gift cards, Instacart+ membership, alcohol, prescriptions. Points don't expire while account open.

Fees / Approval
- Not available if you currently hold this card.

Issued by JPMorgan Chase Bank, N.A.
