Signup Bonus
60,000 points after spending $2,000 in first 3 months from account opening. Not available to current Cardmembers of any Southwest Rapid Rewards Credit Card, or previous cardmembers who received a bonus within the last 24 months (does not apply to Business/Employee cards). Allow 6-8 weeks for bonus to post; account must be open and not in default at fulfillment.

Earn Rates
- 4 points/$1 on purchases made directly with Southwest Airlines (flights, inflight, Southwest gift cards, Getaways by Southwest packages). Excludes Southwest Cruises, Charters, Cargo.
- 2 points/$1 on gas stations and dining.
- 1 point/$1 on all other purchases.
- 7,500 bonus points each account anniversary year.
- 2,500 Tier Qualifying Points (TQPs) per $5,000 spent annually, counting toward A-List/A-List Preferred status.
- Companion Pass qualifying points boost: 10,000 per calendar year, deposited by Jan 31 (or within 30 days of account opening).

Notable Perks (Southwest Rapid Rewards Priority-specific)
- Preferred/Standard seat selection at booking for cardmember + up to 8 companions on same reservation.
- First checked bag free for cardmember + up to 8 travelers on same reservation.
- Extra Legroom seat upgrade available within 48 hours of departure.
- Group 5 (or better) boarding for cardmember + up to 8 companions.
- 25% back (statement credit) on Southwest inflight drink/WiFi purchases, incl. authorized users.
- No change fees (fare difference may still apply); no blackout dates/unlimited reward seats on points bookings.

Credits/Perks (promotional, time-limited)
- Complimentary 12 months DashPass (DoorDash + Caviar) when first activated 02/01/2025-12/31/2027; auto-renews after at then-current rate.
- Up to $10 off quarterly on DoorDash non-restaurant orders while enrolled in DashPass; doesn't roll over.
- 3 months complimentary Instacart+ membership (activate 5/1/2025-12/31/2027); auto-renews at $99/year after.
- $10/month Instacart credit while Instacart+ active (5/1/2025-12/31/2027), one purchase ≥$10, doesn't roll over.
- 5X points (4 additional) on qualifying Lyft app purchases through 12/31/2027; excludes gift cards, car rentals, vehicle service centers, misc fees.

Fees/Approval
Issued by JPMorgan Chase Bank, N.A. Subject to credit approval.
