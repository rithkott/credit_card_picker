DoorDash Rewards Mastercard (Chase)

Notable Perks
- Complimentary DashPass: 12 months free for primary cardmember (auto-activated on approval by setting card as default DoorDash payment method); up to 1 authorized user also gets up to 12 months free (ends same time as primary's). After free period, auto-enrolled in DashPass Annual Plan at then-current annual rate until cancelled. Existing free/paid DashPass or Chase DashPass partnership subscriptions are ended/refunded upon activation of this benefit.
- DashPass Anniversary Benefit: each Anniversary Year (12-month periods from account open date) with $10,000+ spend on the card earns 12 more months of complimentary DashPass for primary + 1 authorized user.
- 10% off (up to $15/month) one Convenience/Grocery/Alcohol/Retail/DashMart order per month on DoorDash (not Caviar), through 12/31/2026. Doesn't roll over.
- Cell phone protection (secondary coverage; monthly phone bill must be paid with card).
- Concierge service (cardmember pays for goods/services booked through it).

Earn Rates
- 4% Cash Back on qualifying purchases directly through doordash.com/trycaviar.com or their apps (DashPass subscription fee itself doesn't earn this rate).
- 3% Cash Back: dining, purchased directly from a restaurant.
- 2% Cash Back: grocery stores.
- 1% Cash Back: everything else.
- Points: 1 point = $0.01 (100 points = $1 cash back).

Redemption
- Redeemable for DoorDash/Caviar purchases, cash, gift cards, or other program options. Points don't expire while account open.
