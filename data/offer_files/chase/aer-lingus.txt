Aer Lingus Visa Signature (Chase)

Signup Bonus
- 75,000 bonus Avios after spending $5,000 in first 3 months from account opening.
- Not available to current cardmembers, or previous cardmembers who received a new cardmember bonus for this card within the last 24 months.
- Allow 6-8 weeks for bonus Avios to post.

Earn Rates
- 3 Avios/$1 on flights purchased directly with Aer Lingus, British Airways, Iberia, LEVEL.
- 2 Avios/$1 on hotel accommodations purchased directly with the hotel.
- 1 Avios/$1 on everything else.
- Avios transferred automatically to Aer Lingus AerClub after each billing cycle.

Notable Perks
- Companion Ticket: spend $30,000 in purchases in a calendar year → one economy companion ticket voucher (taxes/fees waived on companion ticket). One per calendar year. Voucher valid 12 months from issue, must book 30+ days before travel, only valid on Aer Lingus-marketed/operated flights between US and Ireland (no codeshare/alliance).
- Priority boarding on Aer Lingus-operated flights (primary cardmember + authorized users), must display card at gate.
- Complimentary DashPass (DoorDash/Caviar): 12 months free when activated 2/1/2025-12/31/2027, then auto-renews at then-current rate. $10 off quarterly on one DoorDash non-restaurant order while enrolled (no rollover).

Points Notes
- Avios don't expire as long as AerClub membership stays active (flight activity, card spend, or other qualifying activity within any 36-month period).

Fees / Approval
- Not available to current cardmembers or those who got a bonus for this card in the last 24 months.
