Marriott Bonvoy Bountiful (Chase)

Signup Bonus
- 85,000 bonus points after spending $4,000 in first 3 months from account opening. Allow up to 8 weeks to post. Account must stay open 6+ months or points may be clawed back.
- Not available if you currently hold this card or received a bonus for it within the last 24 months.
- Also excluded if you hold/held (last 30 days) Marriott Bonvoy Amex or SPG Amex, or applied/approved (last 90 days) or got a bonus (last 24 months) for Marriott Bonvoy Business Amex, SPG Business Amex, Marriott Bonvoy Brilliant Amex, SPG Amex Luxury, or Marriott Bonvoy Bevy Amex.

Earn Rates
- 6 points/$1 at hotels participating in Marriott Bonvoy.
- 4 points/$1 total: grocery stores + dining (incl. takeout/delivery), combined, on first $6,000 spent annually (from account anniversary); falls back to 2 points/$1 after cap, no maximum.
- 2 points/$1: everything else.
- Points auto-transfer to Marriott Bonvoy after each billing cycle; don't expire with qualifying activity every 24 months.
- Marriott Bonvoy base earn (separate from card): 10 base points/$1 at most brands (5 at Element/Residence Inn/TownePlace Suites/Homes & Villas, 2.5 at Marriott Executive Apartments/ExecuStay) plus Gold Elite bonus (25%) — posts directly to Marriott account.

Credits / Elite Benefits
- 1K Stay Bonus: 1,000 bonus points per eligible paid-night stay at participating Marriott Bonvoy properties booked directly (not third-party/tour operator).
- Free Night Award each calendar year after $15,000 in purchases: valid for one night at a property up to 50,000-point redemption level, 12-month expiration, resort fees still apply.
- 15 Elite Night Credits within 60 days of account opening (subject to per-member-account annual cap across Marriott Bonvoy cards).
- Automatic Gold Elite status each calendar year.
- Complimentary DashPass: 12 months free when activated by 12/31/2027, then auto-renews at then-current monthly rate. $10 off quarterly on one DoorDash non-restaurant order while enrolled (no rollover).
- Concierge service (cardmember pays for goods/services booked through it).

Issued by JPMorgan Chase Bank, N.A.
