Freedom Flex (Chase)

Signup Bonus
- $200 cash back (20,000 points) after spending $500 in first 3 months from account opening.
- Not available to current cardmembers, or previous cardmembers who received a new cardmember bonus for this card within the last 24 months.
- Allow 6-8 weeks for bonus to post.

Earn Rates
- 1% Cash Back: base rate on all purchases.
- 3% Cash Back total: drugstores; dining at restaurants (incl. takeout/delivery).
- 5% Cash Back total: purchases through Chase Travel portal.
- 5% Cash Back total (rotating quarterly categories): up to $1,500 combined spend per quarter, requires quarterly activation by deadline; may include specific merchants as well as categories; falls back to 1% after the $1,500 cap. (This quarter's specific categories not recorded here — check chase.com/freedomflex for current list.)
- Points: 1 point = $0.01 (100 points = $1 cash back). Amazon Shop with Points redemption values points at $0.008 each.

Notable Perks
- Complimentary DashPass (DoorDash/Caviar): 6 months free when activated 2/1/2025-12/31/2027, then auto-renews at then-current rate. $10 off quarterly on one DoorDash non-restaurant order while enrolled (no rollover).
- 2% cash back total on qualifying Lyft rideshare/bike/scooter/subscription purchases through 9/30/2027 (excludes gift cards, car rentals, vehicle service centers).

Redemption
- Points don't expire while account open; redeemable for cash, gift cards, travel, pay-with-points.

Fees / Approval
- Not available to current cardmembers or those who got a bonus for this card in the last 24 months.

Issued by JPMorgan Chase Bank, N.A.
