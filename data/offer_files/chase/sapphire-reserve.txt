Sapphire Reserve (Chase)

Signup Bonus
- 100,000 points after spending $6,000 in first 3 months from account opening. Allow 6-8 weeks to post.
- Not available to current cardmembers, or previous cardmembers who received a bonus for this card.

Earn Rates
- 8 points/$1 on Chase Travel portal purchases (excludes FROSCH by Chase Travel, Valerie Wilson Travel by Chase Travel, Frosch International Travel LLC bookings).
- 4 points/$1: flights and hotels booked direct (incl. via Reserve Travel Designers/FROSCH/Valerie Wilson only if booked directly with airline/hotel).
- 3 points/$1: dining (incl. takeout/delivery).
- 1 point/$1: everything else.
- Purchases that earn the $300 Annual Travel Credit or $500 Edit Credit (below) do not also earn points.
- Points Boost: periodic promotional up-to-2x point value (vs standard $0.01/point) on select Chase Travel flight/hotel bookings.

Credits
- $300 Annual Travel Credit: automatic statement credit on travel-category purchases, up to $300/year (year = account anniversary cycle).
- $500 Credit for stays with The Edit (Chase's luxury hotel program): up to $250 twice a year (starting 1/1/2026) for prepaid 2+ night "Pay Now" stays via The Edit, max $500/year.
- $250 Credit for select hotels through Chase Travel in 2026: prepaid 2+ night stays at IHG, Montage, Pendry, Omni, Virgin Hotels, Minor Hotels, Pan Pacific — up to $250 in calendar year 2026.
- $300 Annual Dining Credit: up to $150 Jan-Jun and $150 Jul-Dec at select restaurants (via OpenTable Sapphire Reserve Exclusive Tables), identifiable direct-restaurant purchases only (excludes gift cards, third-party delivery apps).
- $300 Annual StubHub/viagogo Credit (through 12/31/2027): up to $150 per half-year, one-time activation required.
- $120 Annual Peloton Membership Credit (through 12/31/2027): up to $10/month for qualifying Peloton membership tiers, one-time activation required.
- 10X points on up to $5,000 total Peloton equipment/accessory purchases over $150, direct through Peloton, 4/1/2025-12/31/2027.
- $10 monthly Lyft credit (up to $120/year) through 9/30/2027, for Standard/Extra Comfort/XL/Black/Black SUV rides only (not memberships).
- 5X points on qualifying Lyft rideshare/bike/scooter/subscription purchases through 9/30/2027.
- Global Entry/TSA PreCheck/NEXUS application fee credit: one statement credit up to $120 every 4 years.
- Complimentary Apple TV and Apple Music: 12+ months free each with one-time activation by 6/22/2027; each suspends existing paid Apple subscription during free period. Also gives $7.50/month Apple One discount (max one benefit type applied per Apple Account).
- Complimentary DashPass: 12+ months free when activated by 12/31/2027. $5 off monthly on one DoorDash restaurant order + two $10-off non-restaurant orders monthly while enrolled (no rollover).

Notable Perks
- Priority Pass Select membership (airport lounges), automatic for primary + authorized users; 2 free guests each visit, $27/extra guest.
- Air Canada Maple Leaf Lounge/Café access (primary + authorized users, +1 guest each) on Star Alliance departures; extra guests ~$59.
- IHG One Rewards Platinum Elite status through 12/31/2027 (requires linking IHG account).
- Reserve Travel Designer booking service (no travel-agency fees; supplier fees like baggage/resort fees still apply).
- $75,000/calendar-year spend unlocks: World of Hyatt Explorist status; IHG One Rewards Diamond Elite status; $250 credit at The Shops at Chase; $500 Southwest Airlines Chase Travel credit; Southwest Rapid Rewards A-List status (all require linking respective loyalty accounts).
