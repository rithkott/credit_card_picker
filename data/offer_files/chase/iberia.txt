Iberia Visa Signature (Chase)

Signup Bonus
- 75,000 bonus Avios after spending $5,000 in first 3 months from account opening.
- Not available to current cardmembers, or previous cardmembers who received a new cardmember bonus for this card within the last 24 months.
- Allow 6-8 weeks for bonus Avios to post.

Earn Rates
- 3 Avios/$1 on flights purchased directly with Iberia, British Airways, LEVEL, Aer Lingus.
- 2 Avios/$1 on hotel accommodations purchased directly with the hotel.
- 1 Avios/$1 on everything else.
- Avios transferred automatically to Iberia Plus after each billing cycle.

Notable Perks
- $1,000 Airfare Discount Voucher: spend $30,000 in purchases in a calendar year → voucher good for $1,000 off two tickets on the same flights/class (taxes/fees still apply to full ticket value). One per calendar year; valid 12 months from issue; Iberia-operated flights only (no codeshare/alliance).
- 10% discount on Iberia mainline flight fares (excludes taxes/fees/carrier charges), booked via iberia.com/Chase10; one discount per roundtrip, up to 9 passengers per booking.
- Complimentary DashPass (DoorDash/Caviar): 12 months free when activated 2/1/2025-12/31/2027, then auto-renews at then-current rate. $10 off quarterly on one DoorDash non-restaurant order while enrolled (no rollover).

Points Notes
- Avios don't expire as long as Iberia Plus membership stays active (flight activity, card spend, or other qualifying activity within any 36-month period). Avios earned on this card don't count toward Iberia Plus tier progression/retention.

Fees / Approval
- Not available to current cardmembers or those who got a bonus for this card in the last 24 months.
