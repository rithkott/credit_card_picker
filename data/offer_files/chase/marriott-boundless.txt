Marriott Bonvoy Boundless (Chase)

May be issued as Visa Signature or Visa Platinum depending on underwriting.

Signup Bonus
- 125,000 bonus points after spending $3,000 in first 3 months from account opening. Allow up to 8 weeks to post. Account must stay open 6+ months or points may be clawed back.
- 1 Free Night Award (redemption level up to 50,000 points) after spending $3,000 in first 3 months from account opening; allow up to 8 weeks, 12-month expiration, not combinable with cash.
- Not available if you currently hold or received a bonus in the last 24 months for: Marriott Bonvoy Premier, Marriott Rewards Premier, Marriott Rewards Premier Plus, Marriott Bonvoy Bold, or this card (Boundless).
- Also excluded if you hold/held (last 30 days) Marriott Bonvoy Amex or SPG Amex, or applied/approved (last 90 days) or got a bonus (last 24 months) for Marriott Bonvoy Business Amex, SPG Business Amex, Marriott Bonvoy Brilliant Amex, SPG Amex Luxury, or Marriott Bonvoy Bevy Amex.
- Up to $100 Airline Credits: $50 statement credit per half-year period (2 periods) after spending $250+ directly with airlines each half; terms/dates vary for existing vs. new cardmembers (existing: activate then earn through 2026; new cardmembers applying 6/4/2026-12/31/2026: earn through mid-2027). One-time activation required for existing cardmembers.

Earn Rates
- 6 points/$1 at hotels participating in Marriott Bonvoy.
- 3 points/$1 total: gas stations + grocery stores + dining (incl. takeout/delivery), combined, on first $6,000 spent annually (from account anniversary); falls back to 2 points/$1 after cap, no maximum.
- 2 points/$1: everything else.
- Points auto-transfer to Marriott Bonvoy after each billing cycle; don't expire with qualifying activity every 24 months.
- Marriott Bonvoy base earn (separate from card): 10 base points/$1 at most brands (5 at extended-stay brands) plus Silver Elite bonus (10%) — posts directly to Marriott account.

Credits / Elite Benefits
- Anniversary Free Night Award: one night at a property up to 35,000-point redemption level, 12-month expiration, resort fees still apply.
- 15 Elite Night Credits within 60 days of account opening (subject to per-member-account annual cap across Marriott Bonvoy cards); plus 1 more Elite Night Credit per $5,000 spent (no limit).
- Automatic Silver Elite status each calendar year; Path to Gold Elite status by spending $35,000 in a calendar year (resets Jan 1).
- Complimentary DashPass: 12 months free when activated by 12/31/2027, then auto-renews at then-current monthly rate. $10 off quarterly on one DoorDash non-restaurant order while enrolled (no rollover).
- Concierge service (cardmember pays for goods/services booked through it).

Issued by JPMorgan Chase Bank, N.A.
