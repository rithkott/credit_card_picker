IHG One Rewards Premier (Chase)

Signup Bonus
- 140,000 bonus points after spending $3,000 in first 3 months from account opening. Allow 6-8 weeks to post.
- 10,000 bonus points after adding an authorized user in first 3 months (once per account).
- Not available to current cardmembers of any personal IHG One Rewards card, or previous cardmembers who received a bonus within the last 24 months.

Earn Rates
- 10 points/$1 at hotels participating in IHG One Rewards.
- 5 points/$1: travel (excluding IHG hotel stays that earn 10x); gas stations; dining (incl. takeout/delivery).
- 3 points/$1: everything else.
- Points auto-transfer to IHG One Rewards after each billing cycle; don't expire with 12 consecutive months of program activity.
- IHG base earn (separate from card): 10 base points/$1 at most brands (5 at Candlewood Suites/Staybridge, 3 at Army Hotels, 2.5 at IHG Residence) plus Platinum Elite bonus (60%) — posts directly to IHG account.

Credits / Elite Benefits
- Automatic Platinum Elite status while a Premier cardmember.
- Anniversary Free Night: redemption value up to 40,000 points (can add personal points above that), valid 12 months.
- $100 statement credit + 10,000 bonus points each calendar year with $20,000+ spend.
- Diamond Elite Status through Dec 31 of following year, each calendar year with $40,000+ spend.
- 4th Reward Night free on stays of 4+ consecutive nights (standard rooms, unlimited uses/year).
- Save 20% on IHG One Rewards points purchases.
- Early access to IHG Reward Night sales (not combinable with 4th-night-free benefit).
- Global Entry/TSA PreCheck/NEXUS application fee credit: one statement credit up to $120 every 4 years.
- United TravelBank Cash: $25 deposit twice yearly (Jan and July) into linked United MileagePlus account, after one-time registration; each deposit expires ~6 months after crediting. Doesn't count toward Premier status.
- Complimentary DashPass: 12 months free when activated 2/1/2025-12/31/2027, then auto-renews at then-current rate. $10 off quarterly on one DoorDash non-restaurant order while enrolled (no rollover).
- 3 months complimentary Instacart+ (activated 5/1/2025-12/31/2027), then auto-renews at $99/year unless cancelled; $10/month Instacart credit while active.
