World of Hyatt Credit Card (Chase)

Signup Bonus
- 30,000 bonus points after spending $3,000 in first 3 months from account opening. Allow 6-8 weeks to post. Account must stay open 6+ months or points may be clawed back.
- 2x bonus points total on all other purchases for first 6 months from account opening or until $15,000 spent on those purchases, whichever comes first.
- Not available to current cardmembers, or previous cardmembers who received a bonus for this card within the last 24 months.

Earn Rates
- 4 points/$1 at Hyatt hotels/resorts participating in World of Hyatt, and World of Hyatt Experiences (hyatt.com/experiences).
- 2 points/$1: restaurants (excluding Hyatt dining that earns 4x); airline tickets purchased directly with the airline; local transit/commuting; fitness club/gym memberships.
- 1 point/$1: everything else.
- Points auto-transfer to World of Hyatt account after each billing cycle; don't expire while there's qualifying World of Hyatt activity every 24 months.

Credits / Elite Benefits
- Free Night Award every cardmember anniversary: good for one night at Category 1-4 Hyatt properties (excludes Miraval, Hyatt Zilara, Hyatt Ziva), valid 12 months, resort/destination/facility fees waived. Authorized users don't receive this.
- Free Night Award after $15,000 in purchases each calendar year (same Category 1-4 terms).
- Automatic Discoverist status each calendar year while account open.
- 5 Tier-Qualifying Night credits each calendar year (issued each January), plus 2 more Tier-Qualifying Night credits per $5,000 spent (no limit).
- Complimentary DashPass: 12 months free when activated by 12/31/2027, then auto-renews at then-current monthly rate. $10 off quarterly on one DoorDash non-restaurant order while enrolled (no rollover).
- Concierge service (cardmember pays for goods/services booked through it).

Approval Notes
- Authorized users do not receive elite status, Tier-Qualifying Night credits, or Free Night Awards.

Issued by JPMorgan Chase Bank, N.A.
