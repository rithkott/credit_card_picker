Prime Visa (Chase)

Signup Bonus
- Amazon.com Gift Card awarded at approval to new cardmembers (not available if you currently hold an Amazon Visa or Prime Visa). Loaded directly into Amazon.com account.

Earn Rates
- 5% back at Amazon.com (incl. digital downloads, gift cards, Amazon Fresh, Prime subscriptions, third-party marketplace items), Whole Foods Market (US physical stores + wholefoodsmarket.com), and participating Amazon stores/sites, with an eligible Prime membership; 3% back on same without Prime.
  - Excludes: Amazon Pay merchant purchases, international Amazon sites, international Whole Foods, WF purchases via third-party sites (Instacart, Shipt, etc.), online WF gift cards/event tickets/classes, certain third-party WF vendors, WF massage services, Spa@Ink.
- 5% back (Prime) / 3% back (no Prime) through Chase Travel portal. Not earned on direct bookings with airlines/hotels/car rentals/cruises.
- 2% back: gas stations, local transit/commuting, restaurants.
- 1% back: everything else.
- Points: 1 point = $0.01 equivalent (100 points = $1).
- Prime Card Bonus: periodic promotional extra % back on eligible Amazon.com purchases for Prime members (stacks on top of base 5%); excludes Installments, Prime Wardrobe, Amazon Fresh purchases.

Redemption
- Redeemable for cash, travel, gift cards, Amazon.com purchases (Shop with Points), or program partners. Points don't expire while account open.

Fees / Approval
- Not available if you currently hold an Amazon Visa or Prime Visa.

Issued by JPMorgan Chase Bank, N.A.
