Southwest Rapid Rewards Premier (Chase)

Signup Bonus
- 55,000 bonus points after spending $1,500 in first 3 months from account opening. Allow 6-8 weeks to post.
- Not available to current cardmembers of any personal Southwest Rapid Rewards card, or previous cardmembers who received a bonus within the last 24 months (business/employee cards excluded from this restriction).

Earn Rates
- 3 points/$1 on purchases made directly with Southwest Airlines (flight, inflight, gift cards, Getaways by Southwest packages; excludes Southwest Cruises/Charters/Cargo).
- 2 points/$1 total: grocery stores + dining, combined, on first $8,000 spent per account anniversary year; falls back to 1 point/$1 after cap.
- 1 point/$1: everything else.
- 1 point/$1 on first $15,000 of balance transfers made in first 90 days from account opening.
- 6,000 bonus points each account anniversary year.
- 1,500 Tier Qualifying Points (TQPs) per $5,000 spent annually (toward A-List/A-List Preferred status).
- Points auto-transfer to Rapid Rewards after each billing cycle.

Notable Perks
- First checked bag free (primary cardmember + up to 8 companions on same reservation, Southwest-operated flights only).
- Preferred (or Standard) seat selection within 48 hours of departure (primary cardmember + up to 8 companions).
- One 15% off promo code per account anniversary year (base fare only, excludes Basic fares/group travel/Vacations/Getaways).
- Companion Pass Qualifying Points boost: 10,000 bonus Companion Pass qualifying points each calendar year.
- Group 5 boarding (or better, per fare/tier) for primary cardmember + up to 8 companions.
- 25% back (statement credit) on inflight drinks/WiFi purchases.
- No change fees (fare difference still applies); no blackout dates/unlimited reward seats on point bookings.
- Complimentary DashPass (DoorDash/Caviar): 12 months free when activated 2/1/2025-12/31/2027, then auto-renews at then-current rate. $10 off quarterly on one DoorDash non-restaurant order while enrolled (no rollover).
- 3 months complimentary Instacart+ (activated 5/1/2025-12/31/2027), then auto-renews at $99/year unless cancelled; $10/month Instacart credit while active.
- 3X points on qualifying Lyft rideshare/bike/scooter/subscription purchases through 12/31/2027 (excludes gift cards, car rentals, vehicle service centers).
