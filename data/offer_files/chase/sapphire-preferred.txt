Sapphire Preferred (Chase)

Signup Bonus
- 100,000 points after spending $5,000 in first 3 months from account opening. Allow 6-8 weeks to post.
- Not available to current cardmembers, or previous cardmembers who received a bonus for this card.

Earn Rates
- 5 points/$1 on Chase Travel portal purchases (excludes bookings through FROSCH by Chase Travel, Valerie Wilson Travel by Chase Travel, or Frosch International Travel LLC).
- 3 points/$1: vacation homes at top brands; gas stations; EV charging; dining (incl. takeout/delivery); top streaming services; online grocery (excludes Target, Walmart, wholesale clubs).
- 2 points/$1: travel category purchases not booked via Chase Travel portal (incl. bookings through FROSCH/Valerie Wilson/Frosch International).
- 1 point/$1: everything else.
- The first $100/year in Chase Travel hotel purchases that earns the $100 Annual Hotel Credit (below) does not also earn points.

Credits
- $100 Annual Chase Travel Hotel Credit: automatic statement credit on hotel purchases made through Chase Travel, up to $100/year (year = account anniversary cycle).
- Global Entry/TSA PreCheck/NEXUS application fee credit: one statement credit up to $120 every 4 years, for the application fee charged directly by DHS/CBP/TSA or authorized enrollment providers (not travel agencies or third-party assistance services).
- Complimentary Apple TV: 12+ months free with one-time activation by 12/31/2026; suspends any existing paid Apple TV subscription during the free period, which resumes after at then-current price. Also gives $7.50/month discount on an active Apple One subscription (same linked Apple Account).
- Complimentary DashPass: 12+ months free when activated by 12/31/2027 (shared activation window across primary + authorized users). $10 off monthly on one DoorDash non-restaurant order while enrolled (no rollover).
- 10X (Reserve/J.P. Morgan Reserve) or 5X (Preferred) points on up to $5,000 total Peloton equipment/accessory purchases over $150, through Peloton directly, 4/1/2025-12/31/2027 (excludes gift cards, apparel, non-US purchases, HSA/FSA Truemed checkout).
- 5X points total on qualifying Lyft rideshare/bike/scooter/subscription purchases through 9/30/2027 (excludes gift cards, car rentals, vehicle service centers).

Redemption
- Redeemable for cash, gift cards, travel, pay-with-points. Points don't expire while account open.

Issued by JPMorgan Chase Bank, N.A.
