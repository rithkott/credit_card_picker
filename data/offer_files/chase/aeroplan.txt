Chase Aeroplan Card

Signup Bonus
- 60,000 bonus points after spending $3,000 in first 3 months from account opening. Allow 6-8 weeks to post.
- Not available to current cardmembers of this card, or previous cardmembers who received a bonus for this card within the last 24 months.

Earn Rates
- 3 points/$1 on purchases made directly with Air Canada (excludes gift cards, car rentals, hotel bookings, other third-party partner products via aircanada.com, onboard WiFi; excludes tickets booked via travel agencies/discount sites/vacation clubs/tour operators/non-airline packages).
- 3 points/$1: grocery stores; dining (incl. takeout/delivery).
- 1 point/$1: everything else.
- 500 bonus points per $2,000 spent per calendar month, up to $6,000/month (max 1,500 bonus points/month).
- Points auto-transfer to Aeroplan after each billing cycle; don't expire while card account open.

Elite Status / Credits
- Aeroplan 25K Status for account-open year + following year (not available if received in last 60 months); can requalify each year with $15,000+ spend.
- Aeroplan 35K Status each calendar year with $75,000+ spend (through following calendar year).
- 5,000 Status Qualifying Credits (SQC) automatically each calendar year; up to 20,000 more SQC available (10,000 at $25,000 spend, another 10,000 at $50,000 spend); combined SQC cap of 25,000/year across all Aeroplan cards. SQC count only toward elite status, not redeemable for rewards.
- Free first checked bag (primary cardmember + up to 8 companions, Air Canada/Rouge/Express operated flights, checked at counter not gate).
- Preferred (lower) Aeroplan points pricing on Air Canada flight redemptions vs. non-cardholders.
- Global Entry/TSA PreCheck/NEXUS application fee credit: one statement credit up to $120 every 4 years.
- Pay Yourself Back: redeem Aeroplan points for statement credit on eligible purchases within 90 days (categories/values vary).
- 10% bonus on Chase Ultimate Rewards point transfers of 50,000+ points in a single transaction to Aeroplan (max 25,000 bonus points/year); only applies to primary cardmember's own Aeroplan account.
- Complimentary DashPass: 12 months free when activated 2/1/2025-12/31/2027, then auto-renews at then-current rate. $10 off quarterly on one DoorDash non-restaurant order while enrolled (no rollover).
- Concierge service (cardmember pays for goods/services booked through it).
- Card includes a credit-score-improvement educational resource (VantageScore 3.0 based) — not a monetary benefit.

Issued by JPMorgan Chase Bank, N.A.
