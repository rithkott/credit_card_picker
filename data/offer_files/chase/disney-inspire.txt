Disney Inspire Visa (Chase)

Signup Bonus
- $300 Disney Gift Card eGift, awarded automatically at approval (email delivery). Usable at Walt Disney World, Disneyland, Disney Cruise Line, Disney Store (US + online), Adventures by Disney.
- $200 statement credit after spending $1,000 in first 3 months from account opening. Allow up to 8 weeks to post.
- Not available to current cardmembers, or previous cardmembers who received a bonus for this card within the last 24 months. Account must stay open 6+ months or the gift card offer may be cancelled.

Earn Rates (Disney Rewards Dollars — 1 Dollar = $1)
- 10% total on Disney+/Hulu/ESPN+ subscriptions and add-ons purchased directly at DisneyPlus.com, Hulu.com, or plus.espn.com (not via third-party device/platform unless redirected, and not as part of a cable/other bundle).
- 3% total: US Disney Locations; gas stations.
- 2% total: grocery stores; dining (incl. takeout/delivery, excluding Disney-location dining which earns 3%).
- 1%: everything else.

Credits (per Anniversary Year unless noted)
- 200 Disney Rewards Dollars after $2,000 in qualifying US Disney Resort spend (direct hotel/folio/vacation package spend at Disney Resorts Collection, Disneyland Resort Hotels, AULANI, Hilton Head Island, Vero Beach; also Disney Cruise Line stateroom rates in USD). Excludes Adventures by Disney, National Geographic Expeditions, international resorts, and standalone theme park tickets not part of a package.
- $100 statement credit after $200 in qualifying US Disney Theme Park ticket spend (incl. Annual Passes/Magic Key Passes/Water Park tickets bought direct; excludes tickets bundled into vacation packages).
- $10/month statement credit after $10+ in qualifying Disney+/Hulu/ESPN+ subscription spend; requires annual reactivation; annual subscriptions only get a one-time $10 credit in the purchase month.

Redemption
- Pay Yourself Back: redeem Disney Rewards Dollars (1:1, $1 each) for statement credit on purchases at Disney Parks/Resorts, Disney Store (US + online), DisneyPlus/Hulu/ESPN+, and airline purchases (excl. third-party/travel agencies), within 90 days of purchase.

Notable Perks
- Special vacation financing on select Disney Resort/Adventures by Disney/National Geographic Expeditions/AULANI/Disney Cruise Line packages (booked direct, charged to card before trip).
- 10% off select in-stock merchandise at Disney Store/Outlet (US) and DisneyStore.com (many exclusions apply).
- 10% off select merchandise at Disney Theme Parks/Resorts and onboard Disney Cruise Line (many exclusions apply).
- Dining discount at a specific list of Walt Disney World/Disneyland/Vero Beach table-service restaurants (blackout dates apply around holidays).
- Discounted guided tours and select recreation experiences (carriage rides, horseback trail rides) at Walt Disney World/Disneyland (specific list; blackout dates apply for some).
- Cardmember Photo Opportunities (up to 6 guests/day) with complimentary PhotoPass downloads at designated park locations.
- Complimentary DashPass (DoorDash/Caviar): 12 months free when activated 2/1/2025-12/31/2027, then auto-renews at then-current monthly rate. $10 off quarterly on one DoorDash non-restaurant order while enrolled (no rollover).
