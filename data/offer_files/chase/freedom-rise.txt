Freedom Rise (Chase)

Signup Bonus
- 3% cash back total (1.5% additional) on dining purchases (incl. takeout/delivery) for new cardmembers, for first 6 months from account opening or until $6,000 in dining spend, whichever comes first.
- $25 statement credit after enrolling in automatic payments in first 3 months and staying enrolled 90+ consecutive days. Allow 6-8 weeks to post.
- Not available to current cardmembers, or previous cardmembers who received a new cardmember bonus for this card within the last 24 months.

Earn Rates
- 1.5% Cash Back on all purchases (base rate). Points: 1 point = $0.01 (100 points = $1). Amazon Shop with Points values points at $0.008 each.

Approval Notes
- Aimed at applicants with no prior credit card history (including as authorized user). Approval odds improved by holding a Chase checking/savings account with combined balance ≥$250, opened prior to application, funded within 4 days of applying. Not a guarantee.

Notable Perks
- Complimentary DashPass (DoorDash/Caviar): 6 months free when activated 2/1/2025-12/31/2027, then auto-renews at then-current rate. $10 off quarterly on one DoorDash non-restaurant order while enrolled (no rollover).
- 2% cash back total (0.5% additional) on qualifying Lyft rideshare/bike/scooter/subscription purchases through 9/30/2027 (excludes gift cards, car rentals, vehicle service centers).

Fees / Approval
- Not available to current cardmembers or those who got a bonus for this card in the last 24 months.

Issued by JPMorgan Chase Bank, N.A.
