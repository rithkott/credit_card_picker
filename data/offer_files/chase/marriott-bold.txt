Marriott Bonvoy Bold (Chase)

May be issued as Visa Signature or Visa Platinum depending on underwriting.

Signup Bonus
- 60,000 bonus points after spending $1,000 in first 3 months from account opening. Allow up to 8 weeks to post. Account must stay open 6+ months or points may be clawed back.
- Not available if you currently hold or received a bonus in the last 24 months for: Marriott Bonvoy Premier, Marriott Rewards Premier, Marriott Bonvoy Boundless, Marriott Rewards Premier Plus, or this card (Bold).
- Also excluded if you hold/held (last 30 days) Marriott Bonvoy Amex or SPG Amex, or applied/approved (last 90 days) or got a bonus (last 24 months) for Marriott Bonvoy Business Amex, SPG Business Amex, Marriott Bonvoy Brilliant Amex, SPG Amex Luxury, or Marriott Bonvoy Bevy Amex.

Earn Rates
- 3 points/$1 at hotels participating in Marriott Bonvoy.
- 2 points/$1: grocery stores; internet/cable/phone services; select streaming; taxicabs/limousines; select food delivery (DoorDash, Caviar, Uber Eats, Postmates, Grubhub, Seamless, Delivery.com).
- 1 point/$1: everything else.
- Points auto-transfer to Marriott Bonvoy after each billing cycle; don't expire with qualifying activity every 24 months.
- Marriott Bonvoy base earn (separate from card): 10 base points/$1 at most brands (5 at extended-stay brands like Residence Inn/TownePlace Suites/Element, 4 at StudioRes) plus Silver Elite bonus (10%) — posts directly to Marriott account, not on Chase statement.

Credits / Elite Benefits
- Automatic Silver Elite status each calendar year.
- 5 Elite Night Credits within 60 days of account opening (subject to per-member-account annual cap across Marriott Bonvoy cards).
- Pay Yourself Back: redeem points for statement credit on airline tickets (direct) and Marriott hotel purchases, up to $750/year in redemptions, at $100 per 12,500 points.
- Complimentary DashPass: 12 months free when activated by 12/31/2027, then auto-renews at then-current monthly rate. $10 off quarterly on one DoorDash non-restaurant order while enrolled (no rollover).
- Concierge service (cardmember pays for goods/services booked through it).
