Disney Visa (Chase)

Signup Bonus
- $100 Disney Gift Card eGift, awarded automatically at approval (email delivery). Usable at Walt Disney World, Disneyland, Disney Cruise Line, Disney Store (US + online), Adventures by Disney.
- $50 statement credit after spending $500 in first 3 months from account opening. Allow up to 8 weeks to post.
- Not available to current cardmembers, or previous cardmembers who received a bonus for this card within the last 24 months. Account must stay open 6+ months or the gift card offer may be cancelled.

Earn Rates (Disney Rewards Dollars — 1 Dollar = $1)
- 1% on all purchases (flat rate — no elevated categories on this base tier).

Redemption
- Rewards Dollars redemption mechanics not detailed in this excerpt (Pay Yourself Back program applies to other Disney Visa tiers — verify whether base Disney Visa supports the same statement-credit redemption).

Notable Perks
- Special vacation financing on select Disney Resort/Adventures by Disney/National Geographic Expeditions/AULANI/Disney Cruise Line packages (booked direct, charged to card before trip).
- 10% off select in-stock merchandise at Disney Store/Outlet (US) and DisneyStore.com (many exclusions apply).
- 10% off select merchandise at Disney Theme Parks/Resorts and onboard Disney Cruise Line (many exclusions apply).
- Dining discount at a specific list of Walt Disney World/Disneyland/Vero Beach table-service restaurants (blackout dates apply around holidays).
- Discounted guided tours and select recreation experiences (carriage rides, horseback trail rides) at Walt Disney World/Disneyland (specific list; blackout dates apply for some).
- Cardmember Photo Opportunities (up to 6 guests/day) with complimentary PhotoPass downloads at designated park locations.
- Complimentary DashPass (DoorDash/Caviar): 12 months free when activated 2/1/2025-12/31/2027, then auto-renews at then-current monthly rate. $10 off quarterly on one DoorDash non-restaurant order while enrolled (no rollover).
