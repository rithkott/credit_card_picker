Signup Bonus
$200 cash back (20,000 points) after spending $500 in first 3 months from account opening. Not available to current cardmembers, or previous cardmembers who received a new cardmember bonus for this card within the last 24 months. Allow 6-8 weeks for bonus to post; account must be open and not in default at fulfillment.

Earn Rates
- Base: 1.5% cash back (1.5 points/$1) on all purchases.
- 3% total (1.5% additional) on drugstores and dining (restaurants incl. takeout and eligible delivery).
- 5% total (3.5% additional) on purchases made through Chase Travel (portal-only).
- 2% total (0.5% additional) on qualifying Lyft app purchases through 09/30/2027; excludes gift cards, car rentals, vehicle service centers, misc fees.
- Rewards categories are based on merchant category codes; a merchant may not qualify even if it seems to fit a category.

Points/Redemption
Points don't expire while account is open (100 points = $1). Amazon Shop with Points: 100 points = $0.80 (i.e., points worth $0.008 each there, below standard value).

Credits/Perks
- Complimentary 6 months DashPass (DoorDash + Caviar) when first activated 02/01/2025-12/31/2027; auto-renews at then-current monthly rate after unless cancelled.
- Up to $10 off quarterly on DoorDash non-restaurant orders while enrolled in DashPass; doesn't roll over; excludes gift cards, alcohol in some states.

Fees/Approval
Issued by JPMorgan Chase Bank, N.A. Subject to credit approval.
