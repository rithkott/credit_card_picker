Membership Eligibility Requirement (IMPORTANT)
- PenFed Credit Union membership is required to hold this card.
- Per multiple 2026 sources (including a Yahoo Finance review explicitly titled "a top military credit union anyone can join"), membership is now effectively open to the general public with no donation required: the current standard path is simply opening a PenFed savings account and depositing at least $5 — no military/government/DoD affiliation and no donation to a partner organization required.
- The historical requirement — a one-time small donation (previously reported around $15-20) to a partner advocacy group such as Voices for America's Troops or the National Military Family Association — still appears as a listed alternative pathway in some older/secondary sources (themilitarywallet.com, allcards.com), but current-year sources describe the $5 savings deposit as the primary and simplest general-public route, with no donation needed.
- CAVEAT: penfed.org's own membership/FAQ page could not be directly fetched (blocked/timeout), so PenFed's own precise wording distinguishing pathways could not be quoted verbatim. Treat "no donation currently required via the $5 deposit path" as well-corroborated across secondary sources but not confirmed by a first-hand read of penfed.org's eligibility page. Re-verify directly if possible.

Annual Fee
- $0 (no annual fee).

Foreign Transaction Fee
- $0 (none).

Signup Bonus
- 15,000 bonus points after spending $1,500 in qualifying purchases within 90 days of account opening.

Earn Rates
- 5 points/$1 on gas paid at the pump.
- 3 points/$1 at supermarkets and restaurants.
- 3 points/$1 on cable/streaming services.
- 1 point/$1 on everything else.
- No spending caps/limits found in sources reviewed for any category.

Standard Purchase APR
- 17.99% variable. Every source checked (penfed.org via search, SuperMoney direct fetch, and a targeted search for a possible creditworthiness-based range) showed a single flat rate rather than a range — no evidence PenFed publishes a tiered purchase APR for this specific card.

Balance Transfer / Cash Advance Terms
- Balance transfer: intro 0% APR for 12 months on transfers made within the first 60 days of account opening; 3% balance transfer fee; reverts to the standard 17.99% variable APR after the intro period.
- Cash advance: 17.99% variable APR (per SuperMoney). SuperMoney's page also showed a "0%" cash advance fee, which is inconsistent with typical card fee structures and could not be independently corroborated on penfed.org — flag this fee figure as unconfirmed / possibly an aggregator extraction error.

Redemption
- Program: PenFed Platinum Rewards points, redeemed via PenFed's online "Rewards Center" (Online Account -> "View Rewards Point Balance" -> "Access my Rewards").
- Redeemable for travel (hotel, car rental, airline, and other travel/vacation packages), merchandise, and gift cards (300+ brands).
- Points expire 5 years from the end of the month in which they were earned.

Other Notable Terms
- The card operates on the Visa Signature network tier.

Gaps / Not Found
- Exact cash advance fee (conflicting/likely-erroneous "0%" figure from one aggregator, not confirmed elsewhere).
- Any per-category spending caps (none found, but the official terms PDF could not be fetched to confirm their absence definitively).

Issued by Pentagon Federal Credit Union (PenFed) directly — confirmed via penfed.org (via search), SuperMoney, and the CFPB's card registry; no partner bank identified in any source reviewed. PenFed is a federally chartered, federally insured credit union.
