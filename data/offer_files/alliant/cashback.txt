Cashback Visa Signature Card (Alliant Credit Union)

Membership Eligibility Requirement
- Alliant Credit Union membership is required to apply/hold the card. As of current terms, everyone in the U.S. is eligible to apply: qualify via (a) employment at or retirement from a partner business, (b) being an immediate family member or domestic partner of a current Alliant member, or (c) otherwise selecting to join the Alliant Credit Union Foundation during application — Alliant pays the one-time $5 foundation membership fee on the applicant's behalf. No membership fees are charged to the member. Non-U.S. residents may apply if they have an SSN or valid ITIN.
- IMPORTANT: this specific card is reported (via secondary sources — Doctor of Credit, Clark.com) to have been removed from new-applicant availability around June 2025; the official product page could not be directly confirmed at time of writing (see Gaps below) — verify current new-applicant availability before relying on this for portfolio recommendations.

Cash Back Rate
- Effective 11/1/2025 (per Alliant's own program terms/FAQ, corroborated by secondary sources): unlimited 1.50% cash back on all eligible purchases, per billing cycle, with no monthly or annual spending cap.
- This replaced a prior tiered structure that offered 2.5% cash back on up to $10,000 in eligible purchases per billing cycle (contingent on holding a qualifying Alliant high-rate checking account with a $1,000+ average daily balance) and 1.5% cash back above that $10,000 monthly threshold or without the qualifying checking account. That 2.5% tier and its checking-account/cap requirements no longer apply as of 11/1/2025 for existing cardholders, and the card was reportedly already closed to new applicants prior to that date.
- Redemption minimum was lowered from $50 to $25 as part of the same 11/1/2025 change.

Signup Bonus
- No signup/welcome bonus found on official sources.

Annual Fee
- None.

Foreign Transaction Fee
- None.

Redemption
- Cash back redeemable as statement credit or similar cash redemption; minimum redemption threshold is $25 (post-11/1/2025 terms).

Other Notable Terms
- Visa Signature benefits apply (e.g., concierge services, luxury hotel collection, rental car privileges) per standard Visa Signature program terms.
- Balance transfer fee: 3% of the balance transferred.
- APR: variable, calculated as Prime Rate plus a margin of 8.99%-18.99% on purchases/balance transfers, 16.99%-20.99% on cash advances; Penalty APR margin of 21.99%.
