Bread Cashback American Express Card (Bread Financial)

Annual Fee
- None / $0.

Foreign Transaction Fee
- None.

Signup Bonus
- No welcome/signup bonus found on official pages (breadfinancial.com, americanexpress.com/network/breadfinancial, comenity.net legal docs). Card is marketed purely on its flat cash-back rate.

Earn Rates
- Unlimited 2% cash back on purchases, with no rewards caps, opt-ins, or categories to manage.
- Rewards program is provided by Comenity Capital Bank and its terms may change at any time.
- Bonus cash back offered via special/promotional Amex Offers may carry its own maximum limits per the Rewards Terms and Conditions (exact promo caps not specified on pages reviewed).

Redemption
- Redeemable as statement credit or direct deposit.
- Cash back does not expire as long as the account remains open.
- No minimum redemption threshold specified on pages reviewed.

Credits / Perks
- Amex Offers.
- Special Ticket Access (presale and reserved tickets), via American Express network benefits.
- Car Rental Loss & Damage Insurance, up to $50,000 per rental.
- Retail/Purchase Protection, up to $1,000 per loss / $50,000 annual maximum.
- Zero Fraud Liability.
- Emergency Assistance.
- ID Theft Insurance, up to $2,500 per person.

APR & Fees (Schumer Box, comenity.net legal docs)
- Purchase APR: 18.99%-33.49% variable, based on creditworthiness at account opening; varies with the market based on the Prime Rate.
- Balance Transfer APR: 18.99%-33.49% variable.
- Cash Advance APR: 33.49% variable.
- Balance Transfer Fee: greater of $10.00 or 5.0% of the amount of each transfer.
- Cash Advance Fee: greater of $10.00 or 5.0% of the amount of each cash advance.
- Foreign Transaction Fee: None.
- Late Payment Fee: up to $41.00.
- Returned Payment Fee: up to $41.00.
- Minimum Interest Charge: no less than $3.00 if interest is charged.
- Interest can be avoided on purchases by paying the entire balance by the due date each month.

Gaps / Not Found
- No signup/welcome bonus was found; card appears to have none, but this should be re-checked at verification time in case a limited-time offer is added later.
- Exact caps/exclusions (if any) on promotional bonus cash-back offers (Amex Offers) were not detailed on the pages reviewed.
- Minor discrepancy in issuer naming across official sources: the breadfinancial.com product page phrased it as "Comenity Bank, a Bread Financial company, pursuant to a license from American Express," while the Amex network page, the Comenity Schumer Box/legal documents, and the CFPB Terms of Credit Card Plans (TCCP) survey all state the issuer as "Comenity Capital Bank" (Salt Lake City, Utah). The Schumer Box/legal-document language and CFPB filing are treated as authoritative for the issuer line below.

Issued by Comenity Capital Bank, Salt Lake City, Utah, pursuant to a license from American Express.
