Cash+ Visa Signature (U.S. Bank)

Fees
- Annual Fee: $0.
- Foreign Transaction Fee: 3% (per some third-party sources; not stated on official product page).
- Balance Transfer Fee: 5% of each transfer amount, $5 minimum.

Signup Bonus
- $250 cash back (increased from a prior $200 offer) after eligible Net Purchases totaling $1,000 or more within 90 days of account opening. (Some third-party reviews cite a 120-day window and/or the older $200 amount — 90 days / $250 is what the current official usbank.com product page states; verify at approval since offers change.)

Earn Rates — quarterly choose-your-own-categories mechanic
- 5% cash back on the first $2,000 in combined eligible Net Purchases each quarter across TWO categories the cardholder chooses themselves each quarter ("Choose and activate your Cash+ Categories"). Purchases in the chosen 5% categories beyond the $2,000 quarterly combined cap drop to 1%.
- Selectable 5% categories (choose 2 per quarter): cell phone providers; department stores; electronics stores; fast food; furniture stores; ground transportation; gyms/fitness centers; home utility bills; movie theaters; sporting goods stores; select clothing stores; TV/internet/streaming services.
- 2% cash back on ONE everyday category selected each quarter from: gas stations/EV charging stations, grocery stores, or restaurants (excludes discount stores/supercenters and wholesale clubs).
- 1% cash back on all other eligible purchases (this is also the fallback rate on 5%-category spend if categories aren't selected/activated for the quarter).
- Additional 4% (5% total) cash back on prepaid air, hotel, and car reservations booked through the U.S. Bank Rewards Travel Center.
- Category selection/activation must be done each quarter; if a cardholder does not choose categories, purchases earn the base 1% only.

APR
- 0% intro APR on purchases and balance transfers for the first 15 billing cycles.
- Standard variable APR thereafter: approx. 17.49%–27.99% (range varies slightly by source; official page does not display the exact figure in fetched content — confirm at application).

Redemption
- Redeemable as statement credit, deposit into an eligible U.S. Bank checking/savings account, or a rewards card. Maximum Cash Rewards value applies to redemptions for statement credit or U.S. Bank account deposit.

Other Benefits
- ExtendPay installment plans (0% fee within first 60 days per some cards in this family; confirm terms for Cash+ specifically).
- Real-Time Rewards (redeem points instantly at checkout with participating merchants).
- Cash-back deals / merchant offers marketplace.
