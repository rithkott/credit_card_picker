Altitude Go Visa Signature (U.S. Bank)

Fees
- Annual Fee: $0.
- Balance Transfer Fee: 5% of each transfer amount, $5 minimum.
- Foreign Transaction Fee: not disclosed in fetched official content (need to verify — Altitude family cards typically do not charge one, but this was not explicitly confirmed for Go on the page fetched).

Signup Bonus
- 20,000 bonus points after spending $1,000 in Net Purchases within the first 90 days of account opening.

Earn Rates
- 4X points (1 base + 3 bonus) per $1 on dining, fast-food restaurants, and bars — on the first $2,000 in combined purchases per quarter in this category; presumably drops to 1X after the cap (cap mechanic confirmed, post-cap rate not explicitly restated on page but consistent with U.S. Bank's other quarterly-cap cards).
- 2X points (1 base + 1 bonus) per $1 at grocery stores/supermarkets, gas stations/EV charging stations.
- 2X points (1 base + 1 bonus) per $1 on qualifying streaming service purchases.
- 1X point per $1 on all other eligible Net Purchases.

Credits / Perks
- $15 automatic statement credit per 12-month period after 11 consecutive calendar months of eligible streaming service purchases (e.g. Netflix, Hulu, Apple Music), applied within 2 billing cycles following the qualifying period, account must be in good standing.
- 0%-fee ExtendPay Plans available within the first 60 days of account opening.
- Real-Time Rewards capability.

APR
- 0% intro APR on purchases and balance transfers for the first 15 billing cycles.
- Standard variable APR thereafter (specific figure not disclosed in fetched content — verify at application; sibling cards in the family run roughly 17.49%-27.99% variable).

Redemption
- Points redeemable as deposit into eligible U.S. Bank accounts, merchandise, gift cards, travel, or charity (100% matching on charitable-contribution redemptions).
- Points do not expire as long as the account remains active.
