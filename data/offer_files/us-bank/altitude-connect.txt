Altitude Connect Visa Signature (U.S. Bank)

Fees
- Annual Fee: $0 (personal card; U.S. Bank's official page states no annual fee — note some older reviews reference a prior $95 fee that has since been removed/waived permanently for the consumer version).
- Balance Transfer Fee: 5% of each transfer amount, $5 minimum.
- Foreign Transaction Fee: None — "no foreign transaction fees when making purchases outside the United States."

Earn Rates
- 5X points per $1 on prepaid hotel and car rental reservations booked through the U.S. Bank Rewards Travel Center.
- 4X points per $1 on other travel purchases (airlines, hotels, car rentals, etc. outside the Travel Center portal).
- 4X points per $1 at gas stations and EV charging stations, on the first $1,000 in combined spend per quarter; drops to 1X after the cap.
- 2X points per $1 on dining, streaming services, and grocery stores/supermarkets (excludes discount stores/supercenters and wholesale clubs).
- 1X point per $1 on all other eligible purchases.

Signup Bonus
- 20,000 bonus points after spending $1,000 in Net Purchases within the first 90 days of account opening.

Credits / Perks
- Global Entry/TSA PreCheck application fee statement credit — covers the application fee, credited within 1-2 billing cycles.
- Complimentary Priority Pass Select airport lounge membership.
- Complimentary GigSky global mobile data access in 175+ countries.
- Travel protections: trip cancellation/interruption and travel delay reimbursement (exact coverage limits not captured in fetched content).
- ExtendPay Plans (no interest, fixed monthly plan fee applies).

APR
- 0% intro APR on purchases and balance transfers for the first 15 billing cycles.
- Standard variable APR thereafter (specific figure not disclosed in fetched content — verify at application; sibling cards in the family run roughly 17.49%-27.99% variable).

Redemption
- Points redeemable as deposit into eligible U.S. Bank accounts, merchandise, gift cards, travel, or charity.
- Points do not expire while the account remains active; expire after 12 consecutive statement cycles with no account activity.
