Smartly Visa Signature (U.S. Bank)

Fees
- Annual Fee: $0.
- Balance Transfer Fee: 5% of each transfer amount, $5 minimum.
- Foreign Transaction Fee: 3% (per third-party sources; not confirmed directly on official page).

Signup Bonus
- No welcome/signup bonus found on the official product page or in search results — this card's value proposition is its ongoing cash-back rate, not a signup bonus.

Earn Rates — tiered by combined Smartly banking relationship balance
- Base: unlimited 2% cash back on every purchase, no spending cap on the base rate.
- Bonus tiers require a U.S. Bank Smartly Checking account paired with qualifying combined balances across eligible U.S. Bank deposit/investment accounts, checked each billing cycle:
  - $10,000-$49,999 combined balance: total 2.5% cash back (base 2% + 0.5% bonus).
  - $50,000-$99,999 combined balance: total 3% cash back (base 2% + 1% bonus).
  - $100,000+ combined balance: total 4% cash back (base 2% + 2% bonus).
- The Smartly Earning Bonus (the elevated-tier portion above the base 2%) applies to a maximum of $10,000 in eligible Net Purchases per card billing cycle — spend above that in a cycle earns the base 2% only on the excess.
- Note: as of a prior program update (reported ~April 2025), U.S. Bank raised the minimum qualifying balance threshold for earning any bonus tier from $5,000 to $10,000 and added the $10,000/cycle spending cap on bonus-tier earnings — confirm current thresholds match the live page since these tiers have changed before.

APR
- 0% intro APR on purchases and balance transfers for the first 12 billing cycles.
- Standard variable APR thereafter: reported as roughly 18.24%-28.24% (source-dependent; not confirmed with an exact figure directly on the official page fetch — verify at application).

Redemption
- Cash back redeemable as direct deposit into an eligible U.S. Bank checking/savings account, as a statement credit, a rewards card, or merchant gift cards.
- Full advertised percentage tiers apply "when rewards are redeemed into an eligible U.S. Bank deposit account" per official page language — redeeming via other methods may yield a different (lower) effective rate; verify exact mechanic during curation.

Other Benefits
- ExtendPay Plans.
- 60-second application decision (marketing claim).
- Cash-back deals / merchant offers marketplace.
