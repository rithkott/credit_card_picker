Enjoy Cash (Truist)

Annual Fee
- No annual fee ("None").

Foreign Transaction Fee
- None.

Signup Bonus
- No welcome/signup bonus found on the official product page or disclosures. See Gaps section.

Rewards Structure (choose one at application; cannot be changed after)
- You choose your rewards type when you apply — either the 3-2-1 Rewards option or the 1.5% flat option. You just can't change it later.

Earn Rates — Option A: 3-2-1 Rewards
- 3% Cash Rewards on eligible gas purchases at service stations, automated fuel dispensers, and electric vehicle charging stations.
- 2% Cash Rewards on eligible grocery store/supermarket purchases.
- 2% Cash Rewards on eligible utilities purchases such as phone, cable, television streaming services, gas, water, and sanitary services.
- 1% Cash Rewards on all other Eligible Purchases (no limit).
- Each month, you may earn 2% or 3% Rewards on up to $1,000 in combined Eligible Purchases made at merchants assigned a specific Merchant Category Code (MCC) in the above bonus categories combined (the "Monthly Category Cap"). Once you reach the $1,000 monthly spend total across those categories, you no longer earn 2%/3% on further purchases in those categories that month — you continue to earn 1% for the remainder of the month. The Monthly Category Cap is calculated on Eligible Purchases from the first calendar day of a month through the last calendar day of that month.
- Category qualification is determined solely by the Merchant Category Code (MCC) assigned to the merchant by a third party; Truist does not assign or control MCCs. Example given: an Eligible Purchase of groceries at a warehouse club or pharmacy may carry a "warehouse" or "pharmacy" MCC and will not earn the 2% grocery rate.
- To earn cash rewards, the credit card account must be in Good Standing (open, not in default or delinquent, and in full compliance with the Card Agreement — determined in Truist's sole discretion).

Earn Rates — Option B: 1.5% Flat Rewards
- 1.5% Cash Rewards on all Eligible Purchases, no limit. ($0.015 in Cash Rewards for each $1.00 in eligible purchases.)
- Truist may feature special Cash Rewards promotional offers from time to time; terms disclosed when available.

Eligible / Excluded Purchases (both options)
- Eligible Purchases: any signature-based purchase, internet purchase, purchase made using a credit card terminal, phone or mail-order purchase, or automatic bill payment.
- Excluded from earning rewards: cash disbursements, cash advances, peer-to-peer payment platform transfers, balance transfers, cash equivalents, fees of any kind, interest charges, ATM transactions, credit insurance, fraudulent or unauthorized transactions, credits, returns, and quasi-cash transactions including the purchase of traveler's checks, access checks, foreign currency, or money orders.
- Cash Rewards are considered earned when posted to the Program account. Any credits (except payments to the account) reduce the Cash Rewards available; if returns/credits exceed eligible purchases, a negative Cash Rewards balance may post.

Intro APR
- 0.00% introductory APR for 12 months on purchases, from account opening.
- 0.00% introductory APR for the first 12 months on balance transfers made in the first 90 days after account opening.

Standard APR (after intro period)
- 18.74%–27.74% variable APR on purchases and balance transfers.
- Purchases/Balance Transfers APR is determined monthly by adding a margin of 11.99%–20.99% to Prime Rate.
- Cash Advance APR: 27.74% variable (Prime Rate + 20.99%).
- Penalty APR: None.

Fees
- Balance Transfer Fee: 3% of each transfer ($10.00 minimum).
- Cash Advance Fee: 4% of each advance, or $10.00 minimum on transactions greater than $10.
- Late Payment Fee: up to $40.
- Over-Limit Fee: None.
- Returned Payment Fee: None.
- Minimum Interest Charge: None.
- Balance Calculation Method: Average Daily Balance (including new transactions).
- Grace Period: at least 25 days after the close of each billing cycle.

Perks
- Customizable card controls for added security.
- Contactless payments and mobile wallets for faster checkout.
- Visa® Zero Liability protection against unauthorized card use.

Loyalty Cash Bonus (bonus added when redeeming cash rewards via electronic deposit into an eligible Truist Personal Deposit Account — personal checking, personal savings, or personal money market)
- Baseline: 10% Loyalty Cash Bonus added to the cash redemption amount for standard eligible Truist Personal Deposit Account holders.
- Truist Marquee Checking: 50% Loyalty Cash Bonus when also electronically depositing the redemption into an eligible Truist Personal Deposit Account.
- Truist One Checking (tiered by Truist One Checking Level, based on highest Portfolio value of the prior 3 months; leveling up can occur monthly, leveling down only every 3 months):
  - Level Premier ($100,000+): 50%
  - Level 4 ($50,000–$99,999.99): 40%
  - Level 3 ($25,000–$49,999.99): 30%
  - Level 2 ($10,000–$24,999.99): 20%
  - Level 1 ($0–$10,000): 10%
  - Accounts opened via a company-sponsored Financial Wellness program start at Level 2; Premier/Wealth-identified clients start at Level Premier; a linked Truist Dynamic Business Checking account grants Level Premier.
- Truist Dimension Checking, Truist Signature Advantage Checking, and Truist Asset Management Account clients (tiered by highest combined eligible consumer deposit/investment "Portfolio" balance across the prior 3 calendar months; percentage may increase or decrease monthly):
  - 50% if highest Portfolio value is $100,000 or greater
  - 25% if highest Portfolio value is between $25,000 and $99,999.99
  - 10% if highest Portfolio value is less than $25,000
- New deposit accounts are not eligible for a Loyalty Cash Bonus until the first month following account opening. The Loyalty Cash Bonus percentage updates by the 5th business day of each month and persists until the next update; it reflects the percentage in effect at the time a cash back redemption is completed (not retroactive). If more than one eligible account/relationship applies, the highest applicable percentage is used.
- No Loyalty Cash Bonus is earned on an attempted electronic deposit redemption that is rejected (the redeemed amount is refunded to the Rewards Account).

Redemption
- Cash Rewards are redeemable in U.S. dollars only; $25.00 minimum for cash back redemptions (statement credit, electronic deposit to an eligible Truist Personal Deposit Account, or mailed check). Electronic deposit is not available to non-Truist accounts.
- Rewards may also be redeemed for gift cards, merchandise, and travel (airline tickets, hotel bookings, car rental bookings, cruise bookings, and activities/event tickets), via Truist Online Banking, the Truist Mobile Banking App, or the Rewards Service Center.
- Redemption for travel: a $25.00 travel Rewards Offerings redemption fee is charged per Rewards Offering at time of booking (subject to change without notice); if a travel item costs more than the Cash Rewards redeemed, the difference may be paid with the card. Airline ticket redemptions may separately carry a $28.00 airline ticket redemption fee, a $14.95 paper-itinerary fee, and a $30.00 charge if booking through the Program without redeeming any Cash Rewards. Airline tickets are non-refundable/non-changeable and subject to airline penalties/restrictions.
- Cash Rewards cannot be combined with frequent-flyer or other non-Truist mileage/loyalty programs. Under certain limited circumstances, accrued Rewards balances may be transferred between eligible Truist rewards programs (Truist Enjoy Cash, Truist Enjoy Travel, Truist Enjoy Beyond); transfers to a Truist Wealth Credit Card rewards program are not permitted, and Truist may decline any transfer at its sole discretion.
- Unredeemed Cash Rewards balances have no monetary value. All redemptions are final.
- Cash Rewards are tracked and redeemed on a first-to-expire basis and expire at the end of the month that is five (5) years from the date of issuance (e.g., rewards earned November 15, 2025 are redeemable through November 30, 2030).
- If the account is closed, Cash Rewards may still be used if the account is in Good Standing and was open or closed within the last 90 days; otherwise all related Rewards balances are forfeited if not redeemed within 90 days of account closure.
